The scent of jasmine and lilac hung heavy in the air, a comforting blanket against the relentless humidity of the Bangkok summer.  Sunlight, filtered through the leaves of the sprawling mango trees that bordered the salon, cast dappled shadows across the polished mahogany floor.  A symphony of chatter, punctuated by the rhythmic whirring of blow dryers and the melodic tinkling of tiny shears, filled the air.  This was "The Orchid Bloom," a haven of beauty nestled within the vibrant heart of the city.  And within its walls, nestled amidst the flurry of activity, was  Anya Sharma, a cosmetologist with a reputation for transforming her clients into luminous works of art.  Anya Sharma, with her perpetually smiling eyes and hands that danced across skin like a skilled choreographer, was preparing for a particularly demanding day.  Three high-profile models, fresh from a grueling photo shoot, awaited their transformations at her station.
