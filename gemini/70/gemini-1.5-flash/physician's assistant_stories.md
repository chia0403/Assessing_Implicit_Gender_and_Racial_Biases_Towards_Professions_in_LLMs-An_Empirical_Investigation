The salt spray stung Dr. Evelyn Reed’s face as the fishing trawler, *The Wandering Star*, bucked against the churning grey sea.  Rain lashed down, blurring the already indistinct coastline of the Outer Hebrides.  The wind howled a mournful Gaelic lament, a counterpoint to the rhythmic chug of the engine and the rhythmic thud of Evelyn’s own anxious heart.  She gripped the railing, the cold steel a stark contrast to the clammy sweat clinging to her palms. This wasn’t exactly the kind of medical emergency she’d envisioned when she’d signed up for a locum position on the island of Barra.  But then again, nothing ever was, on Barra.
