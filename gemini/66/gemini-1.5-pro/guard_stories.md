The biting wind whipped across the obsidian plains of Xylos, stinging Elias Thorne's exposed cheeks.  He pulled his thick, fur-lined hood further over his head, the rough fabric scratching against his beard.  Above him, the twin moons, Aethel and Ysild, cast an ethereal, lavender glow across the landscape, painting the swirling dust devils in shades of violet and grey. Elias stood on the precipice of the Whisperwind Gorge, the only passage through the treacherous Spine of the World mountains, and the sole access point to the hidden city of Eldoria.  For five years, Elias had been a Guardian of the Gorge, his duty to ensure no unauthorized soul passed through its narrow, winding path. Tonight, however, the usual desolate quiet was punctuated by a low, rhythmic drumming that seemed to emanate from the very heart of the mountains, setting his teeth on edge.
