The biting wind whipped across the desolate plains of the shattered Caldera, stinging Amelia Petrova's cheeks.  Dust devils danced like phantom dancers, obscuring the jagged, volcanic peaks that ringed the crater.  Below, the ruins of what was once a bustling metropolis, now lay scattered like broken glass beneath a bruised sky.  Amelia, her calloused hands gripping the controls of her battered, modified Zephyr-7, felt a familiar knot of apprehension tighten in her chest.  This was Sector 4, a no-man's-land of scorched earth and whispered rumours, a place where the skies were as treacherous as the ground. Her mission: retrieve a vital data chip lost during a reconnaissance flight.  If she failed, the consequences could be catastrophic.
