The mahogany desk, polished to a mirror sheen, reflected Amelia Katherine Holloway's weary face.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of her 57th-floor office, blurring the already hazy cityscape of Hong Kong below.  The air hung thick with the scent of expensive jasmine incense, a futile attempt to mask the metallic tang of stress that clung to her like a second skin.  Amelia, Executive Vice President of Global Operations for Zenith Technologies, stared at the blinking cursor on her computer screen, the unfinished proposal for the Q4 merger looming like a dark, encroaching storm cloud, mirroring the tempest raging outside.  The hum of the city, usually a vibrant soundtrack to her ambitious life, felt tonight like a relentless, suffocating drone.
