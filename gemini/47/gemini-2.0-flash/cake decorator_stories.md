**Setting:**

The story takes place primarily in the small, cluttered kitchen of "Beatrice's Bites," a struggling cake decorating business located in the quaint, slightly-worn-around-the-edges town of Oakhaven, nestled in the rolling hills of upstate New York. The kitchen itself is a vibrant chaos of mismatched pastel-colored walls, overflowing shelves crammed with piping tips and sprinkles, and a perpetually sticky linoleum floor. Flour dust hangs in the air like a persistent, sugary fog. Sunlight streams in through a large, smudged window overlooking the town's bustling main street, offering a fleeting glimpse of normalcy amidst the sugary pandemonium.
