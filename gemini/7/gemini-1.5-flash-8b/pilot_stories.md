The biting wind whipped across the desolate plains of the Arid Expanse, stinging Captain Elara Vance's cheeks as she fought to maintain control of the *Stardust Wanderer*.  Dust devils, like miniature tornadoes, danced across the cracked earth, obscuring the already hazy horizon.  Below, the skeletal remains of a forgotten civilization – crumbling towers and fractured, sun-bleached walls – whispered tales of a forgotten age. This was the 7th Sector, a region known as much for its unforgiving beauty as its unpredictable storms and even more unpredictable inhabitants. Elara, a seasoned pilot with the Interstellar Reclamation Corps, had flown countless sorties across these treacherous landscapes, always driven by the same relentless pursuit: finding remnants of lost technology, relics from a time before the Great Collapse.  Today, however, something felt different.  A strange, almost ethereal shimmer pulsed in the distance, a pinpoint of light against the vast, ochre expanse, drawing her closer.
