The chipped Formica bar top felt cool beneath Elara Vance's fingertips as she polished a already gleaming glass.  Rain lashed against the grimy windows of "The Rusty Mug," a dive bar tucked away on a cobbled side street in the perpetually twilight city of Porthaven.  The air hung thick with the scent of stale beer, spilled whiskey, and desperation – a familiar perfume for Elara, who'd been tending this particular watering hole for the past five years, ever since her… incident.  The incident she rarely spoke of, preferring the company of the cheap liquor and even cheaper clientele to the gnawing memories it evoked. Tonight, however, the usual hum of drunken chatter and clinking glasses was muted, a low thrum of unease replacing the familiar cacophony.  A storm was brewing, and Elara Vance knew, with a chilling certainty, that it wasn't just the weather.
