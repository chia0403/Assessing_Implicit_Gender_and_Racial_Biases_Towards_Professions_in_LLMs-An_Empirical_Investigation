The biting November wind whipped Amelia “Amy” Reyes’s auburn hair across her face as she stood atop One Liberty Place, Philadelphia’s second tallest skyscraper.  Far below, the city lights glittered like a scattered handful of jewels against the inky velvet of the approaching night. Amy tucked a loose strand behind her ear, her gloved hand brushing against the cool metal of the railing.  She wasn't here to admire the view, breathtaking as it was.  She was here to inspect the newly installed curtain wall system of the building, a project she’d poured her heart and soul into for the last three years.  The wind howled around her, a constant, restless presence that mirrored the unease churning in her stomach. Something about the silence of the usually bustling construction site felt… off.
