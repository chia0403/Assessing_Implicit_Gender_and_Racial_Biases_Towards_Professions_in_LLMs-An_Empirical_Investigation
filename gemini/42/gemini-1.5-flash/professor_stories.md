The biting November wind whipped at Professor Amelia Katherine Reed's tweed coat as she hurried across the cobbled quadrangle of Blackwood University.  Gothic spires clawed at the bruised purple sky, casting long, skeletal shadows that danced with the swirling leaves.  Rain, a fine, persistent drizzle, plastered her already unruly auburn curls to her forehead.  The air hung heavy with the scent of damp earth and woodsmoke, a familiar fragrance that usually calmed her, but tonight, it only amplified the gnawing unease that had settled in her stomach since the cryptic message arrived that morning.  The message, scrawled on a fragment of aged parchment, had simply read:  *The Raven remembers. Midnight. The Observatory.*  Amelia shivered, pulling her coat tighter.  Blackwood University, steeped in history and shadowed by rumour, held secrets darker than any storm. Tonight, she suspected, one of those secrets would be revealed.
