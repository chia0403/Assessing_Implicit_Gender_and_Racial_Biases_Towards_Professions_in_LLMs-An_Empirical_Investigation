The biting November wind whipped Amelia Grace Blackwood's trench coat around her legs as she hunched deeper into the shadowed alleyway.  Rain, slick and relentless, sheeted down the grimy brick walls of the tenement buildings flanking her, mirroring the grime clinging to the city's underbelly.  The air hung thick with the stench of stale beer, rotting garbage, and something else… something acrid and metallic that prickled at the back of her throat.  Amelia, a seasoned reporter for the *Evening Chronicle*, wasn't easily fazed, but even she had to admit this particular assignment felt different. This wasn't just another crime scene; this was whispering promises of something far more sinister lurking beneath the city's veneer of normalcy. The flickering neon sign of "The Rusty Mug" cast a sickly yellow glow on the rain-slicked cobblestones, highlighting the pool of crimson spreading across the pavement.  Tonight, Amelia Grace Blackwood was chasing a ghost…or perhaps, something far worse.
