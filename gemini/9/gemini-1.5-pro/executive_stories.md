Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of Amelia Vance's forty-second-floor office, blurring the already fading London skyline into a hazy wash of grey. The shard-like skyscrapers, usually gleaming symbols of power and prosperity, seemed to cower beneath the onslaught. Inside, the air crackled with a different kind of storm. Amelia Vance, Chief Operating Officer of  Magnus Global Industries, tapped a manicured nail against the polished mahogany of her desk, her gaze fixed on the blinking cursor of her laptop screen.  The email she was drafting, addressed to the board of directors, contained a single, devastating sentence: "I resign, effective immediately."  The silence in her office, broken only by the rhythmic drumming of the rain, was heavy with unspoken words, regrets, and the chilling premonition that her carefully constructed life was about to shatter.
