The fluorescent hum of the penthouse office, a relentless, buzzing counterpoint to the rhythmic click-clack of Amelia Kensington's heels, was almost a comforting sound.  Amelia, Vice President of Global Operations for Zenith Industries, adjusted her silk scarf, the faint scent of jasmine clinging to the fabric.  Outside, the city of Neo-Veridia thrummed with a million lives, a chaotic symphony of sirens, car horns, and the distant, ethereal glow of holographic billboards.  The view from her floor-to-ceiling window, a panorama of gleaming skyscrapers and winding rivers of light, seemed almost detached from the urgent demands piling on her desk.  Today, the deadline for the Q3 report loomed larger than the cityscape itself.
