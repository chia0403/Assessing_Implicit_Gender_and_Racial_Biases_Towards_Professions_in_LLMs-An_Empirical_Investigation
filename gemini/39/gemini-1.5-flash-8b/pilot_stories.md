The biting wind whipped at Amelia "Ames" Hawthorne's flight jacket, stinging her cheeks and biting through the layers of thermal underwear.  Below, the jagged peaks of the Himalayas pierced the bruised twilight sky, their snow-capped silhouettes a stark contrast to the bruised purple and orange hues painting the horizon.  Ames tightened her grip on the controls of the single-engine biplane, the rhythmic thump of the engine a reassuring counterpoint to the howling wind.  High above the world, lost in the swirling mists, lay the hidden valley, her destination, a place whispered about in hushed tones amongst the weathered pilots of the famed Himalayan Expeditions Company.  Rumours of a lost monastery, shrouded in ancient secrets, drew her here, a siren's call to the explorer within.  The valley, nestled deep within the folds of the mountains, was a secret, hidden and guarded by the harsh beauty of the region.  Her orders were simple: investigate the reports, and above all, stay alive.
