The salt spray stung Dr. Eleanor Vance’s face as she wrestled the recalcitrant fishing net onto the deck of the *Sea Serpent*.  The late afternoon sun, a bruised plum sinking below the turbulent Atlantic, cast long shadows across the chaotic jumble of lobster pots and fishing gear. The air, thick with the smell of brine, seaweed, and something indefinably fishy, was far removed from the sterile, clinical environment of Eleanor’s usual workplace – the gleaming, modern kitchens of the prestigious  Wellspring Wellness Clinic in Boston.  This, however, was no ordinary assignment.  She wasn't here to chart carbohydrate intake; she was here to unravel a mystery that smelled suspiciously of cod, conspiracy, and something far more sinister.
