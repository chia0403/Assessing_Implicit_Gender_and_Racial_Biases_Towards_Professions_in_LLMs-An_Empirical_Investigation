The biting wind whipped off Lake Michigan, slicing through Detective Isabella “Izzy” Diaz’s worn leather jacket as she stood on the rooftop of the abandoned Packard Plant. Rust-colored snow dusted the crumbling concrete under her boots, and the city of Detroit sprawled out before her, a tapestry of flickering lights against the bruised twilight sky.  Gargoyles, eroded by decades of neglect, perched on the building’s edges, silent witnesses to the scene unfolding below.  A single spotlight illuminated a white chalk outline, stark against the grime, a grim centerpiece in this urban ruin.  Izzy exhaled, a plume of white vapor disappearing into the gathering darkness.  This was her city, beautiful and broken, and tonight, it had offered up another victim.
