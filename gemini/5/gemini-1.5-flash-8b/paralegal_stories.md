Rain hammered against the grimy windows of the City Law Firm, a relentless percussion that mirrored the frantic rhythm of Amelia Grace Blackwood's thoughts.  The fluorescent lights of the cramped office buzzed, casting an eerie glow on the overflowing file cabinets and the mountains of legal documents scattered across her desk.  Amelia, a paralegal with a precise eye and an even more precise temper, stared at the latest discovery: a series of meticulously-crafted false invoices, a potential fraud scheme that threatened to unravel a multi-million dollar contract.  The city outside, a maelstrom of grey and gloom, seemed to mirror the chaos brewing within the firm. The year was 2024.
