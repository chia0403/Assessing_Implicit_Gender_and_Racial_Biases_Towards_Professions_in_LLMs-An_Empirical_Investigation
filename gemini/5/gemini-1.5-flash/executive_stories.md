The mahogany desk, polished to a mirror sheen, reflected Amelia Holloway's grim expression back at her.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of her 47th-floor office, mirroring the tempest brewing inside her.  The city of Chicago sprawled beneath her, a glittering tapestry of lights struggling to pierce the gloom, much like Amelia was struggling to pierce the fog of impending disaster.  The scent of expensive leather and brewing coffee did little to soothe the knot of anxiety tightening in her stomach.  Amelia Katherine Holloway, CEO of Holloway Industries, stared at the damning email on her screen, the subject line a stark, unforgiving: “Project Nightingale – Catastrophic Failure.”  The city outside was beautiful, but tonight, it felt cold and indifferent to her impending doom.
