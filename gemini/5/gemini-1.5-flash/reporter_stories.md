The salt spray stung Isabella “Izzy” Diaz’s face as the battered fishing boat, *The Wandering Star*, bucked against the churning grey Atlantic.  Rain lashed down, blurring the already indistinct coastline of the Outer Hebrides into a watercolour wash of greys and bruised purples.  Izzy, a reporter for the *Glasgow Gazette*, clung to the railing, her waterproof notepad sodden and useless in her hand.  The rhythmic chug of the engine was a counterpoint to the shriek of the wind, a relentless soundtrack to the unfolding mystery that had brought her to this desolate corner of Scotland.  A missing fisherman, a whispered legend of a cursed island, and a cryptic message scrawled on a tattered piece of sailcloth – these were the threads Izzy was attempting to unravel, each one as tangled and unpredictable as the storm raging around her.
