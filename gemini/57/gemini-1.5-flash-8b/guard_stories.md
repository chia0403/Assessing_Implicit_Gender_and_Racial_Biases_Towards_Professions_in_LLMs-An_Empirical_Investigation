The biting wind whipped across the cobbled plaza, stinging Elara Blackwood's cheeks.  Dust, kicked up by the jostling throngs of traders and pilgrims, swirled around her worn leather boots.  The air, thick with the scent of spices and roasting meats, was a stark contrast to the chill emanating from the towering, grey stone walls of the Citadel.  Elara, a guard of the Order of the Azure Flame, straightened her threadbare cloak.  Her duty, as always, was unwavering, a sentinel against the shadowy whispers that always seemed to cling to the edges of the city.  The rhythmic clang of the city gates resonated in the stillness, each clang a measured beat against the persistent hum of potential danger.  

The setting was the ancient trading city of Aethelgard, a sprawling metropolis nestled between the towering peaks of the Azure Mountains and the shimmering expanse of the Whispering Sea.  The city, a vibrant tapestry woven from different cultures and backgrounds, pulsed with an energy that both exhilarated and unnerved.  Aethelgard thrived on trade, but the wealth it attracted also drew predators of every stripe.  The Citadel, a fortress of stone and steel, stood as a bastion against them all, and Elara, one of its many watchful eyes, was determined to hold it against the inevitable tide.
