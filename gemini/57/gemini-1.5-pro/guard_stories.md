The biting wind whipped across the obsidian walls of Ravenspire, stinging Elias Thorne's cheeks and tugging at the edges of his thick, fur-lined cloak.  Ice crunched beneath his heavy boots as he patrolled the northern ramparts, the only sound besides the mournful whistle of the wind and the distant howl of some unseen beast in the frozen wastes beyond.  Ravenspire, a solitary sentinel against the encroaching darkness, jutted upwards from the precipice like a jagged tooth, its black stone stark against the perpetual twilight that clung to this desolate northernmost corner of the kingdom of Eldoria.  For five years, Elias had been a Guard of the Spire, sworn to protect its secrets and the eccentric mage who dwelt within.  Tonight, however, the air held a different kind of chill, a preternatural stillness that prickled his skin and whispered of something far more sinister than the usual winter storms.
