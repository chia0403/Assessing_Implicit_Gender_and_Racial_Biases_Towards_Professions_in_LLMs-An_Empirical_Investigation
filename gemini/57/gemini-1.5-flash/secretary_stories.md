The chipped ceramic mug warmed Amelia Hernandez’s hands, the lukewarm coffee doing little to chase away the pre-dawn chill that clung to the skyscraper office.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of her corner office, a relentless drumming that mirrored the frantic rhythm of her own pulse.  Amelia,  Executive Assistant to the notoriously volatile CEO of OmniCorp,  wasn't usually this stressed before 7 AM.  But today was different.  Today, the CEO's already volatile disposition was amplified tenfold by the looming shadow of a board meeting, a meeting that could decide the fate of the company – and possibly, Amelia's job.  The city sprawled beneath her, a concrete jungle glittering with the cold light of a thousand defiant windows, oblivious to the storm brewing within the glass and steel fortress of OmniCorp Tower.
