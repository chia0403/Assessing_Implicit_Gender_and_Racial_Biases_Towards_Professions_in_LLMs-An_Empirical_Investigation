The flickering neon sign of the Rusty Mug cast a sickly yellow glow on Amelia "Sparrow" Finch, her reflection shimmering in the polished mahogany bar.  Rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof, a relentless drumming that matched the rhythm of the anxious thoughts swirling in her head.  The small, smoky tavern, tucked away on a side street in the rain-slicked town of Oakhaven, was practically deserted, the only other patrons being a lone, hunched figure hunkered over a chipped mug at the back table and a wizened old woman sipping tea at the corner booth.  Sparrow, despite the quiet, felt the weight of the unspoken pressing down on her. Tonight, the tension hung heavier than the stale beer she was pouring.  Oakhaven, a town steeped in whispers and long-forgotten secrets, was about to spill its latest one onto her counter.
