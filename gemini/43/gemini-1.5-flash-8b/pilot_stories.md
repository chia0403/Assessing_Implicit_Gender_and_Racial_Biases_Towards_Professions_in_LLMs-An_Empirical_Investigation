The biting wind whipped Amelia "Storm" Blackwood's crimson scarf around her neck, stinging her cheeks as she stared out at the jagged peaks of the Veridian Mountains.  Below, the shimmering turquoise lake of Aethelgard reflected the bruised purple sky, a stark contrast to the vibrant, almost hallucinatory colours that painted the surrounding landscape.  The air, thick with the scent of pine and something faintly metallic, hung heavy. Amelia, her name a whispered legend among the sky-faring folk of the Aeridorian Republic, adjusted the controls of her sleek, obsidian-black Zephyr.  The tiny, humming engine of the craft vibrated a low thrum against her hand.  This was the forbidden valley of K'thara, a place whispered to be steeped in ancient magic and guarded by forces beyond human comprehension.  Amelia, though, wasn't interested in legends. She was after answers.
