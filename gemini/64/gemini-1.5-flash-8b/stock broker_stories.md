Amelia "Amy" Blackwood, a seasoned stock broker with a sharp mind and an even sharper tongue, hunched over her terminal, her fingers dancing across the keyboard. Amy, a woman sculpted from granite and steely resolve, wore the crisp, navy pinstripe suit that was her uniform.  Her tailored blazer, despite the suffocating heat, stayed firmly in place, an emblem of the meticulous control she exerted over her life, and the equally calculated risks she took in the world of high finance.  The trading floor, a labyrinth of interconnected desks and looming monitors, stretched before her like a vast, impersonal chessboard.
