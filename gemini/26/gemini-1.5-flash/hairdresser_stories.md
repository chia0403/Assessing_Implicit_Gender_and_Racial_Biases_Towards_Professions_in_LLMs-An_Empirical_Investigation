The scent of lavender and burnt sugar hung heavy in the air, a bizarre perfume only Aurelia "Rel" Nightingale could concoct.  Her salon, "Shear Genius," nestled on a cobbled side street in the perpetually twilight district of Oakhaven, was less a place of beauty and more a carefully curated chaos.  Mismatched velvet armchairs, upholstered in shades of emerald and rust, were scattered amongst overflowing shelves crammed with hair products, vintage hair dryers, and an alarming collection of porcelain dolls with alarmingly realistic eyes.  Rel herself, perched on a stool amidst the clutter, was a whirlwind of auburn curls escaping a haphazard bun, nimble fingers deftly weaving a complex braid into the thick black hair of her client, a scowling goblin-faced chihuahua named Mr. Snuggles.  Outside, the rain hammered against the stained-glass window, blurring the already indistinct gaslight glow of the street.
