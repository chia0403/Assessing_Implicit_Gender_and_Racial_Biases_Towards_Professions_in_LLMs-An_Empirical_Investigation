The relentless Nevada sun beat down on the dusty storefront of Yucca Springs Pharmacy, its faded green awning offering little reprieve. Inside, amidst the sterile scent of antiseptic and the faint sweetness of cough syrup, Eleanor Vance meticulously counted out metformin tablets, her brow furrowed in concentration.  Yucca Springs itself was a town clinging precariously to life, a cluster of low-slung buildings huddled around a dried-up well that had long since given up the ghost. The only things thriving were the cacti and the persistent rumors of something strange stirring in the abandoned silver mines that scarred the surrounding hills.  It was Tuesday, July 18th, 2023, a day Eleanor expected to be as uneventful as all the others.  She was wrong.
