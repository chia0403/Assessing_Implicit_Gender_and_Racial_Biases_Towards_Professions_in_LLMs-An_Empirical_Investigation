The biting wind whipped Detective Isabella "Izzy" Diaz's dark hair across her face as she stared across the frozen expanse of Lake Monona.  Madison, Wisconsin, usually exuded a cozy, collegiate charm, but tonight, shrouded in a pre-dawn gloom and iced over with a brittle layer of frost, it felt stark and menacing.  The flashing blue and red lights of the squad cars reflected off the glassy surface, creating an unsettling strobe effect.  A small crowd of onlookers huddled together, their breath clouding the air, whispering amongst themselves.  Izzy pulled her thick wool coat tighter around her, the stiff fabric doing little to ward off the penetrating chill. Her gaze remained fixed on the dark shape bobbing just beneath the ice, a grim reminder of why she was there.
