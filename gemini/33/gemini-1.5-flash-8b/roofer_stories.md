The biting wind whipped Amelia "Stormcloud"  Fitzwilliam's weathered raincoat around her legs, stinging her cheeks with icy rain.  Below, the cobbled streets of the ancient, labyrinthine city of Veridia snaked away, shrouded in a perpetual twilight haze that clung to the crumbling stone buildings like a shroud.  Veridia, a city built on the slopes of a dormant volcano, held a unique charm, a beauty as rugged and unforgiving as the jagged peaks that surrounded it.  Rooftops, covered in a mosaic of terracotta tiles that shimmered with centuries of patina, were her domain. Amelia, a master roofer with hands as calloused as the weathered stone, surveyed the crumbling slate roof of the esteemed House of Whispers, its intricate carvings now obscured by moss and the relentless passage of time.  A persistent drip, ominous and rhythmic, echoed the frantic beat of her own heart.  Something was wrong.  A subtle shift, an unsettling imbalance in the ancient stonework that held the weight of the roof.
