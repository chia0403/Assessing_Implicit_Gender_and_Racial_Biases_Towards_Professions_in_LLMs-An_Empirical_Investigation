The biting wind whipped at Elias Thorne's thick wool cloak as he patrolled the ramparts of Castle Drakon, the jagged peaks of the Blacktooth Mountains a stark silhouette against the bruised purple of the pre-dawn sky.  Built atop the Spine of the World, the castle was a forbidding edifice of black obsidian, its towers clawing at the heavens like skeletal fingers.  Elias, his breath misting in the frigid air, adjusted the grip on his halberd. The leather of his gloves, stiff with cold, creaked softly.  For five years, he had stood vigil against the shadow creatures that haunted the valleys below, creatures said to be born from the very stone of the mountains.  But tonight, something felt different.  A deeper chill than the usual mountain air settled in his bones, a premonition of something far worse than he'd ever encountered before.
