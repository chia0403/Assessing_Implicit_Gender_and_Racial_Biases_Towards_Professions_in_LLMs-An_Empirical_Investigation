The stale recycled air hung heavy in the cabin, thick with the scent of airplane coffee and anxious anticipation.  Rain lashed against the windows of the Boeing 787 Dreamliner, obscuring the lights of the tarmac at Narita International Airport.  Alexandra "Alex" Moreau, her navy blue uniform crisp despite the fourteen-hour flight from Paris, adjusted the silk scarf at her neck.  A nervous flutter ticked in her stomach, a sensation she’d normally attribute to turbulence, but this was different.  This was Tokyo.  This was the beginning. Alex had bid on this particular route not for the cherry blossoms or the vibrant street style, though those certainly held appeal. She was here for something – or someone – else entirely. A ghost from her past, a name whispered on the wind: Kenji Tanaka.
