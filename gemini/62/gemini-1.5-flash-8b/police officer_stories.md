Rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof of the precinct, a relentless rhythm that mirrored the frantic pounding in Detective Isabella "Izzy" Diaz's head.  The flickering fluorescent lights of the interrogation room cast long, skeletal shadows across the worn linoleum floor.  The air hung thick with the smell of stale coffee and fear, a scent Izzy knew intimately.  Outside, the city of Veridian, nestled between the jagged peaks of the Obsidian Mountains and the shimmering expanse of the Azure Sea, was shrouded in a grey, oppressive gloom.  Veridian, a town built on the promises of forgotten goldmines and the restless energy of its diverse populace, was tonight, once again, bleeding into the darkness.
