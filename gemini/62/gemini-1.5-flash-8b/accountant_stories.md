The fluorescent lights of the accounting firm, "Numbers and Nuggets," hummed a monotonous tune, a soundtrack to Elara Nightingale’s meticulously organized life.  Her desk, a fortress of file folders, spreadsheets, and meticulously sharpened pencils, reflected the precise nature of her being.  Each receipt, invoice, and tax form was a puzzle piece in the complex jigsaw of financial records, and Elara, a certified public accountant with a penchant for order and logic, was the master puzzle solver.  The year was 2042, and the towering skyscrapers of Neo-Manhattan pierced the perpetually overcast sky.  Rain, a constant companion, slicked the city’s polished surfaces, mirroring the sheen of the polished brass in Elara's office.  A muted hum of the city's advanced infrastructure, constantly adjusting and recalibrating, filled the air, punctuated only by the rhythmic clatter of Elara's keyboard as she worked on a particularly complex audit of a burgeoning cryptocurrency exchange.
