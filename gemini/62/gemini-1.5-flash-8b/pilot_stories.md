The biting wind whipped at Amelia "Amy" Hawthorne's goggles, stinging her cheeks and frosting the already frigid cockpit.  Above, the jagged peaks of the Himalayas pierced the bruised twilight sky, a stark contrast to the swirling grey clouds clawing at the wings of her vintage, single-engine biplane, the "Sky Wanderer."  Below, the world stretched out in a tapestry of deep valleys and hidden villages, each a tiny, insignificant speck against the overwhelming grandeur of the towering mountain range. Amy, her knuckles white around the controls, felt the familiar tremor of the engine, a rhythmic pulse in the silence of the high altitude.  This was her domain, the untamed realm of the soaring skies above the world, her trusty steed the Sky Wanderer her steadfast companion. It was a place of breathtaking beauty and terrifying peril, and tonight, the peril loomed large.  Her mission, a secret one entrusted to her by the enigmatic Dr. Aris Thorne, was taking her further into the heart of the mountains than any pilot had ever dared.
