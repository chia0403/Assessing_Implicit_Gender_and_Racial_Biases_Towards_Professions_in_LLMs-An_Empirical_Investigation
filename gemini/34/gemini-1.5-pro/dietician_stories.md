The rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof of the "Nourish to Flourish" clinic, a rhythmic counterpoint to the frantic tapping of Amelia Grace Hawthorne's pen against her notepad.  Damp tendrils of her auburn hair escaped her usually impeccable bun, clinging to her forehead. Outside, the monsoon season had transformed the usually dusty streets of Chiang Mai, Thailand, into a swirling tapestry of ochre mud and emerald green reflections from the overflowing rice paddies.  Inside, the air hung thick and heavy with the scent of ginger tea and the underlying anxiety radiating from Amelia's newest client, a renowned Muay Thai fighter struggling to reconcile his rigorous training regime with a newly diagnosed gluten intolerance.
