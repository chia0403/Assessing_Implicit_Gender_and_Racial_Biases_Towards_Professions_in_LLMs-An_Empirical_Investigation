The chipped porcelain mug warmed Amelia Hernandez’s hands, the lukewarm chamomile tea doing little to soothe the frayed nerves buzzing beneath her skin.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of her office, blurring the already hazy cityscape of Buenos Aires sprawling below.  Each drop sounded like a tiny, insistent accusation, mirroring the gnawing anxiety in her gut.  Amelia, a travel agent whose specialty was crafting bespoke, once-in-a-lifetime adventures for the ultra-wealthy, had just received an email that threatened to unravel everything she’d meticulously built.  It wasn't a cancelled booking, or a disgruntled client, but something far more… unsettling. The email, unsigned and ominously brief, simply read: "The Serpent's Eye awaits."  The scent of jasmine, usually a comforting aroma from the overflowing planter in the corner, now felt heavy and suffocating in the air thick with the impending storm.
