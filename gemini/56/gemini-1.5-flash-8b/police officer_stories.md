The biting wind whipped across the deserted docks, stinging Detective Isabella "Izzy" Rodriguez's cheeks.  Salt spray, a constant companion in the port city of Alderhaven, stung her eyes, blurring the already dim light of the pre-dawn hour.  The rhythmic clang of the shipyard's machinery, usually a comforting lullaby, now sounded like a frantic heartbeat echoing through the skeletal remains of the fishing trawler, *The Wanderer*.  The air hung thick with the metallic tang of blood, a scent that clung to the rusted metal and the skeletal remains of the vessel like a shroud.  Izzy, her breath fogging in the frigid air, knelt beside a pool of crimson that spread like a stain on the weathered deck.  The crimson was tinged with a strange, oily sheen.  She pressed her gloved hand against it.  It wasn't just blood.
