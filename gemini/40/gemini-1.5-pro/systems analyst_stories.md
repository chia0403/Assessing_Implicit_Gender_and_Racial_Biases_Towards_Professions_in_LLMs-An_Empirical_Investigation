Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of the Zenith Tower, blurring the already fading London skyline.  Inside, the air thrummed with the low hum of servers and the frantic click-clack of keyboards.  Eleanor Vance, Systems Analyst for Chimera Technologies, hunched over her monitor, the flickering glow reflecting in her tired eyes.  Lines of code scrolled past, an intricate digital tapestry she was desperately trying to unravel.  It was 2:17 AM, a time normally reserved for dreams, but tonight, a rogue virus was holding the company's entire network hostage, and Eleanor was the only one left standing between Chimera and digital oblivion. The air conditioning battled against the heat radiating from the overloaded servers, creating an uncomfortable, almost feverish atmosphere.  Empty energy drink cans littered her desk like fallen soldiers, testament to the hours she'd already poured into this fight.
