The biting wind whipped Amelia "Amy" Cartwright's scarf around her neck, stinging her cheeks like a thousand tiny needles.  The skeletal branches of the ancient oak trees, draped in a shroud of frost, clawed at the perpetually overcast sky above the abandoned mining town of Veridian.  Dust devils, kicked up by the icy gusts, danced across the cracked asphalt streets, swirling remnants of forgotten dreams and shattered hopes.  Veridian, once a bustling hub of industrial might, was now a ghost town, its buildings crumbling monuments to a bygone era.  Amy, a seasoned investigative journalist for the *Northstar Chronicle*, shivered not just from the cold but from a creeping sense of dread.  This was her story.  This was the story of the vanishing miners.
