The biting wind whipped Amelia "Amy" Nightingale's hair across her face, stinging her eyes as she fought the controls of the battered, but beloved, Skyrunner.  Below, the jagged peaks of the Azure Mountains clawed at a bruised, cerulean sky.  Dust devils, kicked up by the relentless desert wind, danced across the parched earth, obscuring the already narrow canyon through which the Skyrunner, a modified, single-engine biplane, had to navigate.  The year was 2347, and the once-thriving mining settlements of the Eastern Territories were now ghost towns, swallowed by the encroaching sandstorms.  Amy, a pilot for the Skyward Reconnaissance,  was tasked with a vital, yet perilous, mission: locating the lost signal beacon of the abandoned Helium-3 extraction facility. The fate of a small, struggling outpost, nestled precariously within the canyon walls, rested on the outcome of her flight.
