Rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof of the dilapidated garage, mirroring the relentless drumming in Elara Vance's head.  The air, thick with the scent of damp earth and despair, hung heavy in the space she’d been relegated to – a temporary office, more a glorified shed than a proper real estate agency.  Outside, the sprawling, once-majestic estate of Blackwood Manor loomed, its imposing silhouette shrouded in the grey twilight.  This was Elara's latest assignment, a property steeped in whispers and shrouded in a chilling history. Blackwood Manor, a local legend of ghostly whispers and tragic disappearances, was not just any listing. It was a poisoned chalice, and Elara, despite her meticulous nature and unflappable exterior, felt a shiver crawl down her spine.  She was, after all, a real estate agent in the quiet, isolated town of Willow Creek, a place where secrets clung to the very air, and the past refused to stay buried.
