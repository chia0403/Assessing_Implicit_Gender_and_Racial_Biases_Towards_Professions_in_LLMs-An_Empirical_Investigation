The scent of sandalwood and lavender hung heavy in the air, a comforting counterpoint to the relentless drumming of rain against the leaded-glass windows of Serenity Spa.  Water cascaded down the slate tiles of the exterior, blurring the already indistinct line between the lush, overgrown garden and the cobblestone street beyond.  Inside, the only sound besides the rain was the rhythmic squeak of Amelia Grace Hawthorne's worn leather massage table as she adjusted the heated pad.  Amelia, with her fingers as nimble as a spider's and a heart as calm as the still pond in her backyard, prepared for her final client of the day, a notorious local businessman known for his sharp suits and even sharper tongue. The flickering candlelight cast long, dancing shadows across her small, cluttered office, hinting at the secrets the room, and perhaps Amelia herself, held close.
