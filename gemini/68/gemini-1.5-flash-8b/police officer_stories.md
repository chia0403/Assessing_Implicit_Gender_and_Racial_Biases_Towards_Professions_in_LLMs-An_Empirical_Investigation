The rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof of the precinct, a relentless tattoo that echoed the frantic beat of Detective Isabella "Izzy" Rodriguez's heart.  The flickering fluorescent lights of the interrogation room cast long, dancing shadows on the worn linoleum floor, accentuating the growing unease in the air.  A thick, acrid smell of stale coffee and fear hung heavy, clinging to the damp walls like a shroud.  Outside, the city of Veridian sprawled beneath a pewter sky, a labyrinth of dimly lit streets and shadowy alleyways, each one a potential hiding place for the truth.  Izzy, her trench coat plastered to her skin, stared at the suspect – a gaunt man with eyes that darted around the room like trapped birds – trying to piece together the fragments of a shattered night.  Veridian was a city of secrets, and tonight, one of them was about to come crashing down.
