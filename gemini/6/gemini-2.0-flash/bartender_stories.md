Outside, the wrought-iron balconies dripped with Spanish moss, whispering secrets to the humid air. Inside, bathed in the warm glow of amber-colored lamps, Elias Thorne presided over his domain, a mahogany bar worn smooth by years of elbows and spilled dreams.
