The flickering neon sign of the "Lucky Dragon" noodle shop cast a lurid glow across Amelia "Mia" Hernandez's rain-slicked trench coat.  Rain hammered the grimy streets of Neo-Kyoto, a city where ancient temples huddled cheek-to-jowl with towering chrome skyscrapers, a chaotic symphony of tradition and technology.  Mia, a systems analyst for the sprawling Kaito Corporation, pulled her collar tighter, the damp chill seeping into her bones.  The air, thick with the scent of exhaust fumes and simmering broth, did little to warm her.  She wasn't here for ramen, though.  A crumpled, encrypted message, tucked into her worn leather satchel, had summoned her to this unlikely corner of the city at 3 AM, a summons that smelled far less of noodles and far more of trouble.
