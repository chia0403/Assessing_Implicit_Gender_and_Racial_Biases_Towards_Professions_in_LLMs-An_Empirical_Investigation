The flickering neon sign of "The Binary Bean" cast a sickly green glow across Amelia Grace Holloway's tired face.  Rain lashed against the window, blurring the Seattle skyline into a shimmering, impressionistic mess. Inside, the cafe was a haven of warm, roasted coffee aromas and the gentle hum of laptops. Amelia, hunched over a corner table, tapped furiously at her keyboard, her fingers a blur against the backlit keys.  Lines of code scrolled across her screen, a cryptic language only she seemed to understand.  Tonight, fueled by triple-shot espressos and the looming deadline, she was determined to crack the encryption that had been plaguing her for weeks. Outside, the rain intensified, mirroring the rising storm within Amelia's own mind.  Something about this code was different, something unsettling, and the closer she got to breaking it, the more she felt a prickle of unease crawling up her spine.
