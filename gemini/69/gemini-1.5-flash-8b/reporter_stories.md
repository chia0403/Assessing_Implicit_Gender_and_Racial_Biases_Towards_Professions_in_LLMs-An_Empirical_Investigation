The biting wind whipped Amelia Blackwood's reporter's notebook into a frenzy, scattering scraps of paper across the cobblestones of the Rue des Étoiles.  Dust devils danced around her boots, swirling the already thick Parisian smog into a gritty haze.  Above, the skeletal branches of ancient chestnut trees clawed at a bruised, overcast sky, mirroring the turmoil in Amelia’s stomach.  She squinted, peering through the grime, trying to discern the faintest details from the overflowing refuse bins lining the narrow street.  This was the heart of the Marais, a district whispered to hold the city's darkest secrets, and Amelia, a young, ambitious reporter for the *Le Journal de Paris*, was on the trail of one.  Her assignment: uncover the truth behind the disappearances plaguing the district.
