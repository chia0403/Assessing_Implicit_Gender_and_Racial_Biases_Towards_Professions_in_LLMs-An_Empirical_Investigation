The fluorescent lights of the "Little Sunbeams" daycare hummed a monotonous tune, a soundtrack to the controlled chaos that was 2:30 PM on a Tuesday. A chorus of mismatched socks, sticky fingers, and gleeful shrieks echoed around the brightly painted room, where miniature Picassos were in the making at one table and a dramatic reenactment of "The Very Hungry Caterpillar" was unfolding near the toy kitchen. Amidst the fray, clinging desperately to the last vestiges of her sanity, stood Persephone Willowbrook, known to the twenty-odd children under her care as Miss Percy.
