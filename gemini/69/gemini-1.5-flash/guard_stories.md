The biting wind howled a mournful dirge across the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains, clawing at the worn leather of Sergeant Valerius Thorne's jerkin.  Snow, driven by the relentless gale, stung his cheeks and blurred the already fading light.  Valerius, his breath misting in the frigid air, gripped the chipped shaft of his halberd tighter, its cold steel a small comfort against the pervasive chill that seeped into his bones.  He stood atop the windswept precipice of Fort Bastion, the only outpost of the Kingdom of Eldoria for leagues, a lonely sentinel guarding the treacherous pass against whatever shadowy things might lurk beyond the unforgiving landscape.  Below, the chasm yawned, a black maw promising oblivion to any who dared to fall.  Tonight, however, Valerius's gaze was fixed not on the abyss, but on the swirling snow, searching for any flicker of movement, any sign that would break the oppressive silence before the storm truly hit.
