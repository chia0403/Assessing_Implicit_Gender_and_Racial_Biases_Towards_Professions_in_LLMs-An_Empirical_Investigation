The biting wind howled through the shattered window of the abandoned warehouse, carrying with it the acrid scent of burnt plastic and the persistent crackle of hungry flames. Captain Isabella "Izzy" Rodriguez squinted, her soot-stained face barely visible beneath the helmet. The flickering orange light painted grotesque shadows on the walls, revealing graffiti tags and piles of forgotten junk that had become fuel for the inferno. Water cascaded down her gear, steaming as it hit the fiery heart of the blaze. This wasn't just another call; this was a five-alarm inferno threatening to engulf the entire derelict industrial complex on the edge of Oakhaven, Pennsylvania.
