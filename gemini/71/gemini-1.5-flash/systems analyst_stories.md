The stale air of the server room hung heavy with the scent of ozone and desperation.  Fluorescent lights hummed a discordant symphony above Amelia Hernandez, their pale glow reflecting off the blinking LEDs of countless servers.  Amelia, a systems analyst with a reputation for solving seemingly impossible tech nightmares, felt a familiar knot of tension tightening in her stomach.  Tonight, however, it wasn't just the usual pre-deadline jitters.  Tonight, the entire system of the city's largest hospital was crashing, and the only person standing between thousands of patients and complete medical chaos was her.  The rhythmic whirring of cooling fans was the soundtrack to a potential disaster, and Amelia, armed with nothing but her laptop and an unwavering resolve, was ready to face it head-on.
