The biting November wind whipped through the cobblestone streets of Arkham, Massachusetts, tugging at the hem of Eleanor Vance’s wool coat.  Dead leaves, brittle and brown, danced around her ankles as she hurried towards the imposing brick facade of Elderwood Manor.  Gargoyles perched menacingly on the roofline, their granite faces grim under the perpetually overcast sky. Eleanor, an occupational therapist with a decade of experience under her belt, adjusted her satchel, a familiar knot of apprehension tightening in her stomach. Elderwood wasn't just any assisted living facility; it was home to Arkham's most… unique residents.  Those whose needs went beyond simple physical therapy, those whose minds held secrets best left undisturbed. Today, Eleanor was meeting a new patient, a Mr. Silas Blackwood, rumored to be both brilliant and deeply troubled.
