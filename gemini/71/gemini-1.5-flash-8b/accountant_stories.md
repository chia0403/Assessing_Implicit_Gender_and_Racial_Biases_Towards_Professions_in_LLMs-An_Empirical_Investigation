The fluorescent lights of the accounting firm, "Numbers & Nuggets," hummed a monotonous tune, a soundtrack to Amelia Peabody's meticulous life.  Rain lashed against the grimy windows of the tenth-floor office, mirroring the relentless torrent of numbers cascading across Amelia's computer screen.  The year-end audit for "Global Gadgets," a sprawling conglomerate with a reputation as unpredictable as the stock market itself, was nearing its deadline, and the pressure was palpable.  Paperwork piled high on Amelia's desk, a tangible representation of the mountains of data she was tasked with sifting through.  The air, thick with the scent of stale coffee and desperation, was a familiar foe in her relentless battle against the clock. Amelia, a meticulous accountant with a sharp mind and an even sharper wit, knew the only way to conquer this particular Goliath was one painstaking, number-by-number step at a time.
