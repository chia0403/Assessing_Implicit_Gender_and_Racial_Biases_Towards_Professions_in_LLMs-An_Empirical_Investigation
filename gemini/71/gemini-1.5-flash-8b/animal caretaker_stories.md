The biting wind whipped Amelia Blackwood’s tweed jacket around her as she surveyed the sprawling, grey expanse of the Whispering Pines Sanctuary.  Dust devils danced across the parched, ochre earth, swirling up the scent of pine needles and something indefinably… metallic.  The Sanctuary, a sprawling estate nestled deep within the Whispering Peaks mountains, seemed to hold its breath, waiting for the inevitable storm that always followed these prolonged droughts.  Amelia, Head Animal Caretaker at Whispering Pines, adjusted her spectacles, her gaze sweeping over the sprawling enclosures.  Each cage, each paddock, held a story, a silent plea for attention.  Today, though, the unspoken language was one of desperation. The reservoirs were near empty.  Water troughs, once brimming, were now little more than cracked reflections of the unforgiving sky.
