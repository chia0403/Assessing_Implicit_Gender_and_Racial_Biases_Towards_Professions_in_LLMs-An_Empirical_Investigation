The biting wind whipped across the desolate plains of the Outer Rim, stinging Elias Thorne's cheeks.  Dust devils, swirling miniature tornadoes, danced across the cracked earth, obscuring the already meager horizon.  The rusted skeleton of a long-abandoned freighter, its name faded to illegibility, cast long, skeletal shadows, like a forgotten specter watching over the desolate training grounds.  This was the proving ground of the Razor's Edge, a place where the galaxy's most promising—and often foolhardy—pilots honed their skills in the face of oblivion.  And Elias Thorne,  Head Coach of the Razor's Edge, was preparing his squadron for the deadliest trials yet.
