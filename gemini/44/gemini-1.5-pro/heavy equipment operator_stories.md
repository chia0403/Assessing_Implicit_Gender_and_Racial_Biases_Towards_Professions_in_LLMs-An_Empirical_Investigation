The biting wind whipped across the desolate expanse of the Klondike Plateau, stinging Amelia “Milly” Cartwright’s cheeks even through the layers of fleece and wool.  Dawn hadn't yet cracked the horizon, painting the world in a pre-dawn indigo, but the floodlights of Milly's D11 dozer already cut through the gloom.  Around her, the frosted breath of the other machines plumed into the frigid air, a silent orchestra of diesel exhaust accompanying the rumble and groan of metal on permafrost.  Camp Black Spruce, a temporary settlement clinging to the edge of the newly discovered gold claim, was slowly shuddering to life.  For Milly, though, the day had already begun hours ago.  This wasn't just another day; this was the day they were finally going to break ground on the main shaft, and if anyone could wrestle the frozen earth into submission, it was Milly and her trusty dozer.
