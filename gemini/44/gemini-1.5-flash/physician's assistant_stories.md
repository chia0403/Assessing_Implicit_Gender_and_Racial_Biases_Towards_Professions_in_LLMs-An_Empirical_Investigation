The biting November wind whipped Amelia Grace Hawthorne’s hair across her face as she hurried down the cobbled lane, the scent of woodsmoke and brine heavy in the air.  The grey, bruised sky mirrored the turmoil churning in her stomach.  This wasn’t the picturesque coastal town of Port Blossom she’d envisioned when she’d accepted the position as Physician’s Assistant at the secluded Cliffside Medical Clinic.  The clinic itself, perched precariously on the windswept cliff edge, looked less like a beacon of hope and more like a stranded, weathered ship clinging desperately to the rocks.  The rhythmic crash of waves against the jagged shoreline provided a constant, unsettling soundtrack to the already unnerving quiet of the place – a quiet that hinted at secrets buried deep beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic, yet isolated, community.
