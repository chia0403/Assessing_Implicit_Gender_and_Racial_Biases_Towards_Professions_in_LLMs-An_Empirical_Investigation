The chipped Formica counter, stained a perpetual beige from years of spilled coffee and forgotten hairspray, hummed with the low thrum of the old hairdryer.  Sunlight, filtered through the grimy windowpanes of "Scissors & Sighs," cast long, theatrical shadows across the small salon.  Amelia Penelope Featherstone, her name a whisper against the soundtrack of buzzing clippers and the soft sighs of her clients, meticulously trimmed a strand of raven hair, her fingers moving with practiced grace.  Outside, the city of Aethelgard rumbled with its own symphony of sirens and hurried footsteps, a stark contrast to the quiet sanctuary within the salon.  Amelia, despite the constant murmur of the city, felt a comforting hush settle over her as she worked.  Aethelgard, a sprawling metropolis steeped in grey stone and shadowed alleys, held its secrets close, and in Amelia's small, quiet shop, those secrets occasionally found a temporary home.
