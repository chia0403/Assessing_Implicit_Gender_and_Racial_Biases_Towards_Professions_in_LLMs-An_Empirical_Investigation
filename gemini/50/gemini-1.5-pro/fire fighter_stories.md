The biting wind whipped at Amelia “Ace” Hernandez’s turnout coat as she stood on the precipice of Widow’s Peak, the highest point overlooking the town of Alder’s Bend. Below, nestled in the valley, the flickering lights of the town resembled a scattered handful of fireflies against the encroaching darkness.  Smoke, acrid and thick, curled upwards from the western edge of the valley, staining the twilight sky an angry orange.  Ace could feel the heat radiating even from this distance, a tangible wave against her face.  Alder's Bend, a town carved from the very heart of the Redwood National Park, was built amongst ancient giants, and tonight, those giants were burning.
