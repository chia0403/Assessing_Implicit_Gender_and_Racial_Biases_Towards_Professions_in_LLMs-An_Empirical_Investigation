Rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof of the Precinct 7 station, mimicking the relentless drumming in Detective Isabella "Izzy"  Delacroix's head.  The air, thick with the scent of damp earth and stale coffee, hung heavy in the cramped interrogation room.  Outside, the city of Aethelgard, nestled amidst the jagged peaks of the Silvan Range, was shrouded in an oppressive grey, a fitting backdrop for the escalating tension within.  Precinct 7, a small outpost struggling to maintain order in a rapidly changing region, felt more like a pressure cooker than a place of work.  The wind, whistling through the gaps in the weathered windows, carried the faint, chilling cry of a hawk circling high above.  Izzy, her trench coat plastered to her skin, stared at the nervous suspect, a young man named Kael Thorne, whose eyes darted around the room as if seeking an escape route.  The case, a seemingly simple theft of a rare obsidian amulet, had unraveled into something far more sinister, a mystery intertwined with the whispers of ancient prophecies and the hidden power of the Silvan stones.
