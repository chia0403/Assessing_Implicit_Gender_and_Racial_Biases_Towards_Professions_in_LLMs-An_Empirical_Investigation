The fluorescent lights of the accounting firm, "Numbers & Nuggets," hummed a monotonous tune, a soundtrack to Amelia Nightingale's meticulously organized life.  Documents, crisp and numbered, filled her meticulously-ordered workspace.  Stacks of invoices, receipts, and tax forms, all meticulously filed and color-coded, towered like miniature mountains around her.  Amelia, a seasoned accountant with a precise haircut and a calm demeanor, was known for her ability to navigate the labyrinthine world of figures with the grace of a seasoned sailor.  Today, however, the rhythmic pulse of the office felt jarring, punctuated by an almost palpable anxiety.  A cryptic note, slipped under her door this morning, had sent ripples of unease through her meticulously calculated world.  It read simply, "The missing millions are hiding in plain sight."
