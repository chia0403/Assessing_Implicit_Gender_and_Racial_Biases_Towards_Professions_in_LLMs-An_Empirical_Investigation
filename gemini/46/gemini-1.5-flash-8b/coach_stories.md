The biting wind whipped across the frozen expanse of the High Peaks, stinging Amelia Blackwood's cheeks.  Snow, thick and relentless, coated the jagged peaks, transforming the already formidable mountain range into a stark, white monument.  Below, nestled in a valley shrouded in a perpetual twilight, lay the village of Silverstream, its few cottages clinging to the hillside like petrified dreams.  Amelia,  a figure clad in layers of insulated clothing and a weathered, crimson ski jacket, adjusted her bulky scarf, her breath puffing out in frosty clouds.  She surveyed the sprawling, windswept training ground, the meticulously groomed slopes a stark contrast to the raw, unforgiving landscape.  This was the crucible where her team, the Silverstream Snow Serpents, would forge their destiny, or perhaps, their demise.  Tonight, the pressure was palpable, thick as the falling snow.  The regional championships loomed, a looming, icy threat over the tiny community.
