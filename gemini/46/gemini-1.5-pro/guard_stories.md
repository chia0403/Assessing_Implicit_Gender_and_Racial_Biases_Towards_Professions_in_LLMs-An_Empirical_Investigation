The biting wind whipped across the obsidian cliffs of the Nightwatch, tugging at Elias Thorne's thick woolen cloak. Below him, the churning grey waters of the Whisper Sea crashed against the jagged rocks, their rhythmic roar a constant companion to the lonely vigil.  Elias, his full name Elias Thornewood the Third, but known simply as Thorne to his fellow guardsmen, shifted his weight from one foot to the other, the cold seeping through the layers of leather and steel protecting his feet. The Nightwatch, a solitary tower perched precariously on the cliffs' edge, was the easternmost sentinel of the kingdom of Eldoria, its purpose to watch for the return of the Shadow Fleet, a legendary armada said to sail on moonless nights, bringing with them an age of darkness.  Tonight, however, the moon was a sliver in the inky sky, casting long, skeletal shadows across the desolate landscape, making the task of vigilance all the more daunting.
