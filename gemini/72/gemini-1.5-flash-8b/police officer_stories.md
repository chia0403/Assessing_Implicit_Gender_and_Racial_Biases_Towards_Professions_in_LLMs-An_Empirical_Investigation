Rain hammered against the corrugated iron roof of the precinct, a relentless drumming that mirrored the frantic rhythm of Detective Isabella "Izzy" Rossi's thoughts.  The flickering fluorescent lights of the small, cramped interrogation room cast long, skeletal shadows that danced with the water droplets clinging to the peeling paint.  The air hung thick with the scent of stale coffee and fear.  It was 3 AM, and the city of Havenwood, nestled in the foothills of the Cascade Mountains, was a silent, sleeping beast, its only sound the ceaseless rhythm of the storm.  Izzy, a seasoned detective with eyes that held the weariness of countless sleepless nights, stared across the table at the suspect, a young man named Ethan Vance, his face pale and etched with a silent terror that mirrored her own growing dread.  Havenwood was a town steeped in secrets, and tonight, one of them was about to shatter.
