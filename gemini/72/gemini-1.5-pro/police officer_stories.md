The biting wind whipped off Lake Erie, carrying the scent of iron and ice as Detective Isabella "Izzy" Diaz slammed the car door shut. The rusted skeleton of the Lorain Pellet Terminal loomed over her, a monument to a dying industry and the perfect backdrop for the city's latest tragedy.  Jagged shadows stretched from the skeletal structure across the frozen ground, obscuring the cluster of police cruisers already gathered at the edge of the pier.  A single spotlight illuminated a small, huddled shape draped in a white sheet, the stark whiteness a brutal contrast to the grimy, grey landscape. Izzy pulled her collar tighter against the wind, a shiver of more than just cold running down her spine.  This was her city, her beat, and another life lost under her watch.
