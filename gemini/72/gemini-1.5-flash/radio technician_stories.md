Inside, Elias Thorne, his face illuminated by the flickering glow of a bank of vacuum tubes, wrestled with a recalcitrant amplifier. Elias, his brow furrowed in concentration, adjusted a dial with the precision of a brain surgeon performing delicate microsurgery.
