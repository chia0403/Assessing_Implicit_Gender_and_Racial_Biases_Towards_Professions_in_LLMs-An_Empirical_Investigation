The scent of rain-soaked petrichor hung heavy in the air, mingling with the intoxicating perfume of lilies and jasmine that spilled from the open doorway of "Petal & Stem."  Inside, amidst a riot of colour and fragrance,  Aurelia “Auri”  Hawthorne hummed a low, almost mournful tune, her nimble fingers deftly arranging a cascade of crimson roses into a heart-shaped arrangement.  The bell above the door jingled, a sharp counterpoint to the gentle thrum of the city outside, announcing the arrival of another customer into her small but beloved flower shop nestled on a cobbled side street in the heart of Prague's Old Town. Rain lashed against the ancient, leaded windows, blurring the already hazy gaslights into soft, shimmering orbs.  Auri glanced up, a smile playing on her lips, already anticipating the floral desires that would soon bloom before her.
