The biting wind whipped Dr. Eleanor Vance's coat around her legs, stinging her cheeks with icy particles.  The skeletal branches of the ancient oak trees clawed at the bruised twilight sky, casting long, skeletal shadows across the frozen expanse of the Whispering Woods.  Snow, thick and undisturbed, crunched under her boots as she trudged deeper into the forest, the only sound besides the wind's mournful howl the rhythmic thump of her own heart.  This wasn't a normal evening call.  Nor was it a normal animal.

**Setting:** The Whispering Woods, a secluded and notoriously eerie forest bordering the rural town of Oakhaven, known for its ancient trees and unsettling legends.  A biting winter's night, shrouded in a thick layer of undisturbed snow.  The air hung heavy with the scent of pine and damp earth, punctuated by the occasional, chilling snap of a twig.
