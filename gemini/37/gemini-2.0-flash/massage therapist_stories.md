The scent of lavender and eucalyptus hung heavy in the air, a predictable but comforting blanket over Tranquility Now, the name Amelia Willowbrook had chosen for her tiny massage studio. Rain hammered against the frosted windows, blurring the already muted grey of Portland, Oregon, outside. Amelia adjusted the soft, fleece throw draped over the massage table, a nervous habit she hadn't been able to break in the seven years since she'd opened her practice. Today was particularly nerve-wracking. Her first celebrity client.
