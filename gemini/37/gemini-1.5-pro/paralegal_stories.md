Rain lashed against the ornate, leaded windows of the office, mirroring the tempest brewing inside Amelia Grace Holloway.  She tapped a manicured nail against the mahogany desk, the rhythmic click a counterpoint to the relentless drumming of the storm.  Her office, tucked away on the third floor of Prescott, Sterling & Blackwood, Attorneys at Law, usually offered a sanctuary of quiet elegance with its rich Persian rug and shelves overflowing with legal tomes.  Tonight, however, the opulent space felt more like a gilded cage.  Outside, the Charleston, South Carolina sky roiled with an October squall, and inside, Amelia was wrestling with a case that threatened to unravel her carefully constructed world. A missing document, a hostile witness, and a looming deadline had coalesced into a perfect storm of her own making.
