The biting wind whipped across the obsidian walls of the Citadel of Whispers, stinging Elias Thorne's cheeks raw despite the thick fur lining his hood.  Snow, the colour of bone dust, swirled around his worn leather boots, piling against the parapets in ghostly drifts.  Seven hundred feet below, the city of Veridia sprawled like a spilled jewel box, its myriad lights twinkling against the encroaching dusk. Elias, his breath clouding the frigid air, tightened his grip on the spear shaft, its iron tip glinting dully in the fading light.  He was on the Night Watch, the lone sentinel against the unseen terrors that roamed the shadowed plains beyond the city walls, and tonight, the air hummed with a peculiar, unsettling energy.
