The salt spray stung Elias Thorne's face as he wrestled the stubborn hatch open, the rusty hinges groaning a mournful protest against the encroaching Atlantic.  Wind, smelling of seaweed and something vaguely metallic, whipped around him, threatening to snatch his worn leather toolbag from its precarious perch on the splintered deck.  Below, the churning grey water of the North Sea threatened to swallow the entire dilapidated fishing trawler whole – the *Seraphina*, Elias’s current, and arguably most challenging, workplace.  He wasn’t used to fixing faulty wiring whilst battling a tempest, but then again, Elias Thorne, Master Electrician (and reluctant maritime adventurer), wasn't used to much these days.
