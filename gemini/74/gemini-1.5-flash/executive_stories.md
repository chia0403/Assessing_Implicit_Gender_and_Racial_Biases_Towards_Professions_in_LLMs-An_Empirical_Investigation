The mahogany desk, polished to a mirror sheen, reflected Amelia Katherine Hayes's grim expression.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of her 72nd-floor office, mirroring the tempest brewing inside her.  The city of Chicago sprawled beneath her, a glittering tapestry of lights battling the encroaching darkness, a fitting backdrop to the corporate warfare she'd found herself embroiled in. Amelia, CEO of Hayes Industries, leaned back in her leather chair, the scent of old leather and expensive coffee barely masking the metallic tang of stress clinging to the air.  The board meeting had ended just an hour ago, a brutal display of backstabbing and veiled threats that left her feeling more exposed than she ever had before.  The future of the company, her family's legacy, hung precariously in the balance.
