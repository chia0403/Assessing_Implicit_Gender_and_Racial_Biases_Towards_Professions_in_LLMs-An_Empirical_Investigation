The relentless Miami sun beat down on Ocean Drive, turning the turquoise water into a shimmering mirage.  Palm trees swayed rhythmically in the humid breeze, their fronds rustling like whispered secrets.  It was 7:00 am, and already the air thrummed with the energy of a city waking up.  Amidst the joggers, dog walkers, and rollerbladers gliding along the beachfront path, a figure stood out.  Tall and lean, with a cascade of raven hair pulled back into a high ponytail,  Isabella “Izzy” Rodriguez surveyed her assembled class.  Her bright coral sports bra and leggings popped against the backdrop of the Art Deco buildings lining the street.  A portable speaker blasted a high-energy Latin beat, and a microphone clipped to Izzy’s ear magnified her already infectious enthusiasm.  “Buenos días, everyone!” she called out, her voice ringing with a mix of warmth and authority. “Are we ready to sweat?”
