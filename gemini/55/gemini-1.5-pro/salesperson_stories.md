The flickering neon sign of the Starlight Motel cast a lurid, purple glow across the rain-slicked asphalt of Highway 17.  Inside, the air hung thick with the scent of stale cigarettes and cheap coffee.  Amelia Rose Hawthorne, star salesperson for "Miracle Mop" and self-proclaimed Queen of Clean, shivered despite the mug warming her hands.  She'd pictured a triumphant return to the regional office after smashing her sales targets, not huddled in a roadside diner halfway between nowhere and oblivion, a busted radiator hose her only companion.  Thunder rumbled in the distance, mirroring the grumbling in Amelia’s stomach.  This wasn’t how she envisioned spending her Friday night, not at all.
