The biting wind howled a mournful dirge across the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains, whipping snow into frenzied spirals that stung Elara Thorne's cheeks.  Elara, a seasoned guard of the Obsidian Keep, hunched deeper into her thick furs, the rough wool scratching against her hardened skin.  The Keep itself, a monolithic structure of black volcanic rock that seemed to claw its way from the mountainside, loomed above her, a grim silhouette against the bruised purple of the twilight sky.  Below, the chasm yawned, a bottomless abyss promising certain death to any who stumbled – or were pushed – over the edge.  Tonight, the silence, broken only by the wind's relentless wail, felt heavier than usual, pregnant with an unspoken tension that prickled at Elara's senses.
