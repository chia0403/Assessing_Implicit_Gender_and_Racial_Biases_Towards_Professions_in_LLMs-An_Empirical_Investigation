The biting wind whipped across the desolate plains of Aethelgard, stinging Elara Greystone's cheeks and rattling the iron bars of the prison cell.  Dust devils danced across the cracked earth, swirling fragments of broken pottery and rusted tools into swirling motes of grey.  The sun, a pale disc sinking below the jagged horizon, cast long, skeletal shadows that stretched across the barren landscape.  Aethelgard Penitentiary, a crumbling fortress of grey stone and forgotten ambitions, stood silent sentinel against the encroaching gloom.  Elara, a Guard of the First Order, her weathered tunic stained with the grime of a thousand patrols, shivered, not only from the cold, but from a growing unease.  Tonight, the silence was more unsettling than the wind.  For tonight, the whispers of the prison held a new, unsettling rhythm.
