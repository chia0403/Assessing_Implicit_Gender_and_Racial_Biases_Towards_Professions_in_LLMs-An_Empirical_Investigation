The mahogany desk, polished to a mirror sheen, reflected Amelia Katherine Hayes’s grim expression.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of her 72nd-floor office, mirroring the tempest brewing inside her.  Below, the city of Chicago sprawled, a glittering tapestry of lights struggling to pierce the storm’s inky veil.  But Amelia saw none of it.  Her gaze was fixed on the stark white envelope lying in the centre of the desk, its crispness a cruel contrast to the turmoil churning within her.  The embossed lettering – *Hayworth Industries – Confidential* – felt like a brand, searing its message into her soul: a message that threatened to unravel everything she’d painstakingly built, everything she’d sacrificed, to reach this pinnacle of success as CEO.
