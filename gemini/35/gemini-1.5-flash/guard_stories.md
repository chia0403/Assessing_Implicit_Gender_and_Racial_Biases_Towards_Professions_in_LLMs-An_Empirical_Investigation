The biting wind whipped across the desolate plains of Xylos, stinging Sergeant Valerius Thorne’s cheeks raw.  He hunched deeper into his worn, grey greatcoat, the collar pulled high to shield his face from the relentless assault of sand and ice crystals.  Xylos, the forgotten frontier, was a land of harsh beauty, a brutal tapestry woven from jagged, snow-capped peaks and endless stretches of frozen wasteland.  Valerius, a veteran of countless patrols along the Whisperwind Pass, stood watch at the crumbling stone outpost known as Fort Argentum, its once-proud battlements now scarred and crumbling like a decaying tooth.  The flickering torchlight cast long, dancing shadows that seemed to writhe and twist in the howling wind, mirroring the uneasy feeling that gnawed at him.  This wasn't just the usual bone-chilling cold; tonight, something felt… different.
