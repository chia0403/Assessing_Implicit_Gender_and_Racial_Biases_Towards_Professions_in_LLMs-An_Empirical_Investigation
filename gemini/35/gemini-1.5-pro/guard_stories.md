The biting wind whipped across the obsidian ramparts of the Citadel of Whispers, stinging Elias Thorne's cheeks and turning his breath to frost. Far below, the city of Veridia sprawled like a spilled jewel box, its myriad lights twinkling against the encroaching darkness. Elias, his full name Elias Vance Thorne, shifted his weight from one foot to the other, the steel plates of his armor groaning in protest. He clutched his halberd tighter, the cold metal a reassuring presence against his gloved hand.  For five years, he'd stood watch on this very parapet, guarding against threats both real and imagined, a solitary sentinel against the vast, unknown expanse of the Whispering Woods that bordered Veridia's western edge.  Tonight, however, something felt different. The air hummed with an unnatural energy, and the usual nocturnal symphony of crickets and owls was conspicuously absent.
