Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of Amelia Reyes's forty-second-floor office, blurring the already fading twilight view of the Chicago skyline.  The rhythmic drumming of water against glass echoed the relentless thrum of tension in her chest. Amelia Reyes, Chief Operating Officer of Thorne Industries, a sprawling multinational corporation dealing in everything from sustainable energy to aerospace technology, rarely felt the sting of uncertainty. But tonight, the air crackled with it.  Her mahogany desk, normally a testament to meticulous order, was a battlefield of scattered documents, each one a fragment of a puzzle she couldn't quite assemble.  The soft glow of her computer screen illuminated the worry lines etched around her dark eyes, reflecting the email that had arrived just an hour ago, its subject line chillingly simple: "Leak."
