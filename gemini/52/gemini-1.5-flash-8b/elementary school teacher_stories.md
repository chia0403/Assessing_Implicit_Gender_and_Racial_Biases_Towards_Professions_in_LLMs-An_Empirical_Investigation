This was Miss Amelia Butterfield's kingdom, a miniature world of tiny hands, buzzing with whispered secrets and the rhythmic scratch of pencils.  Amelia, a woman whose heart pulsed a quiet symphony of patience and understanding, felt a familiar tremor of anxiety.  Today was the day.
