The salt spray stung Detective Inspector Amelia "Mia" Stone's face as the November wind howled a mournful dirge across the desolate Cornish coastline.  Jagged cliffs, slick with the relentless Atlantic surge, clawed at the bruised sky, mirroring the turmoil churning in her gut.  Below, the crumpled wreckage of a small fishing boat, the *Sea Serpent*, lay half-submerged amongst the rocks, a grim testament to the fury of the storm that had raged through the night. The air hung thick with the smell of brine, diesel, and something else… something metallic and sickeningly sweet that clung to the back of her throat like a phantom’s kiss. This wasn't just a fishing accident; Mia knew, with a chilling certainty, that this was something far more sinister.
