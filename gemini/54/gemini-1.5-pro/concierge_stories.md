The opulent lobby of the Grand Chateau Dumont shimmered, every polished surface reflecting the crystalline chandeliers overhead.  Rain lashed against the arched windows, a dramatic counterpoint to the hushed elegance within.  Behind the gleaming mahogany concierge desk,  Genevieve "Gigi" Dubois adjusted the pearl earrings that had been a gift from her grandmother, a subtle reminder of the pragmatism that tempered her love for the extravagant world she inhabited.  Tonight, however, even Gigi’s unflappable demeanor was beginning to fray. The annual Diamond Gala, the Chateau's most prestigious event, was only hours away, and a crucial piece of the evening – the legendary Dumont Diamond necklace, centerpiece of the auction – was missing.
