The humid Florida air hung heavy, even at 6:00 am.  Dew clung to the Spanish moss draping the live oaks that lined the manicured lawns of Pelican Cay, turning the wispy tendrils into shimmering silver beards.  A rhythmic thumping bass, barely audible over the screech of cicadas, pulsed from the open-air pavilion overlooking the turquoise waters of the Gulf. Inside, bathed in the soft glow of sunrise and strategically placed LED spotlights,  Amelia “Amy” Rodriguez bounced on the balls of her feet, a wireless microphone clipped to her neon pink tank top.  Her dark ponytail swung in time with the music as she surveyed the twenty or so bleary-eyed residents who had dragged themselves out of bed for her “Sunrise Sculpt and Stretch” class.  Amy, a whirlwind of energy despite the early hour, grinned, her teeth a flash of white against her tanned skin. "Alright, Pelican Cay, let's get those endorphins flowing!"
