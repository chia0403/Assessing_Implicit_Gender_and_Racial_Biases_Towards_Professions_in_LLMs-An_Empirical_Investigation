The stale air of the executive suite hung heavy, thick with the scent of old leather and simmering ambition.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of the fifty-second floor, blurring the already hazy cityscape of Hong Kong below.  Amelia Katherine Stone, CEO of Stonebridge International Holdings, stared out at the tempestuous night, her reflection a pale ghost amidst the glittering chaos.  The city, a concrete jungle she'd conquered with ruthless efficiency, mirrored the storm raging within her.  Tonight, the stakes were higher than ever.  The impending merger with Zenith Corporation, a deal that could either solidify Stonebridge's position as an industry titan or send it plummeting into oblivion, hung precariously in the balance.  A single, unsigned document sat on her mahogany desk, a ticking clock counting down to a decision that would define the rest of her life.
