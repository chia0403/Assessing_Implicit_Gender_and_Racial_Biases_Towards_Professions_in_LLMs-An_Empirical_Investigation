The scent of lavender and sandalwood hung heavy in the air, a comforting balm against the relentless November rain lashing against the leaded-glass windows of Serenity Spa.  Water cascaded down the panes, blurring the already fading twilight into an impressionistic smear of grey and purple.  Inside, however, the atmosphere was a haven of carefully curated calm.  Low, soothing music hummed from hidden speakers, and the only sounds besides were the gentle hiss of the steam room and the occasional soft sigh escaping the lips of a relaxed client.  Amelia Grace Hawthorne, a massage therapist whose touch was legendary amongst the city's elite and weary professionals, sat at her oak desk, the faintest smile playing on her lips as she reviewed her schedule.  The flickering candlelight danced in her hazel eyes, reflecting the warmth that radiated from her, a warmth that extended far beyond the physical touch of her skilled hands.
