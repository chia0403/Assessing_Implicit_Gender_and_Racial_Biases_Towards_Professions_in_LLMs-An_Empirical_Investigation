The biting November wind whipped Dr. Eleanor Vance’s auburn braid across her face as she wrestled the recalcitrant door of her clinic open.  The sign above – *Vance Chiropractic & Wellness – A Holistic Approach* – creaked mournfully in the gale, mirroring the ache in her own bones after a long day spent manipulating spines and soothing sore muscles.  Inside, the small waiting room was a haven of warmth, smelling faintly of cinnamon and pine needles – a deliberate counterpoint to the raw, unforgiving chill that clawed at the windows of her isolated practice nestled beside the windswept cliffs of Cornwall, England.  The muted glow of the antique lamp on the oak table cast long shadows, accentuating the dust motes dancing in the amber light.  Tonight, however, the usual peaceful quiet was broken only by the rhythmic tick-tock of a grandfather clock in the corner, a relentless counter to the storm raging outside.
