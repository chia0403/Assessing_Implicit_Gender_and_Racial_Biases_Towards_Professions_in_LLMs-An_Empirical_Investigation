The relentless Miami sun beat down on Ocean Drive, turning the turquoise water into a shimmering mirage.  Palm trees swayed rhythmically in the humid breeze, casting dappled shadows over the Art Deco facades.  Inside the gleaming, white-walled sanctuary of "Body by Beatriz," the air thrummed with a different kind of energy.  Electronica pulsed through hidden speakers, mingling with the rhythmic thud of twenty exercise balls being bounced in perfect synchrony. Beatriz "Bea" Rodriguez, clad in a neon pink sports bra and black leggings, surveyed her class with a hawk-like gaze, her dark ponytail whipping back and forth as she demonstrated a particularly challenging oblique twist. Her voice, amplified by a headset microphone, boomed with an almost unnatural cheerfulness, "Feel the burn, beautiful people!  Embrace the discomfort! This is where change happens!"  Sweat glistened on her tanned skin, mirroring the determination in her dark brown eyes. But beneath the surface of her energetic facade, Bea harbored a secret, one that threatened to shatter the carefully constructed world she'd built around herself.
