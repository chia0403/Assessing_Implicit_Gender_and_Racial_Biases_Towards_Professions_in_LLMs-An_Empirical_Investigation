The biting wind whipped across the desolate plains of Aeridor, stinging Sergeant Kael Theron's cheeks.  Dust, the colour of rust and decay, swirled around his boots, obscuring the already skeletal landscape.  He hunched deeper into his worn leather jerkin, the faint scent of ozone clinging to the air, a reminder of the looming storm that threatened to consume the region.  The sun, a pale disc sinking below the jagged, purple-hued horizon, cast long, skeletal shadows across the crumbling stone watchtower that was his lonely post.  Aeridor, once a bustling trade route, was now a forgotten whisper in the teeth of the encroaching Withering.
