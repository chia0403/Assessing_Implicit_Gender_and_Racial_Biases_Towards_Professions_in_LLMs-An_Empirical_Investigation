Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of Elias Thorne's forty-second-floor office, mirroring the tempest brewing inside him.  The cityscape of Chicago, usually a glittering tapestry of ambition, was obscured by a grey, swirling curtain.  Elias, his normally impeccable charcoal suit slightly rumpled, stared down at the flashing red numbers on his Bloomberg terminal, each tick a fresh hammer blow to his carefully constructed world.  It was October 26th, 2023, a date that was already etched in his memory as the day everything started to unravel. The air in his office, usually crisp with the scent of expensive leather and ambition, was thick with the metallic tang of fear.  His phone buzzed incessantly, a chorus of panicked clients demanding answers he didn't have.  The Algorithmic Apex Fund, the crown jewel of Thorne & Sterling, was hemorrhaging money faster than he could staunch the flow.  And he, Elias Thorne, the golden boy of Chicago finance, was about to face his biggest challenge yet.
