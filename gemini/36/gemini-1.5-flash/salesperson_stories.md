The air in the showroom hung thick and sweet, a cloying blend of new car leather and industrial-strength air freshener – “New Car Smell, Intensified,” the placard near the cappuccino machine proudly proclaimed.  Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of Prestige Motors, blurring the already indistinct cityscape into a grey watercolour.  Amelia Katherine Blackwood, Senior Sales Associate and reigning queen of Prestige’s quarterly sales competition (a title she fiercely defended), tapped a perfectly manicured fingernail against the polished mahogany desk.  Her reflection, sharp and composed in the gleaming surface, barely registered the storm raging outside.  Inside, the battle was just beginning.  Tonight was the last night of the fiscal quarter, and Amelia was only two sales shy of her goal.
