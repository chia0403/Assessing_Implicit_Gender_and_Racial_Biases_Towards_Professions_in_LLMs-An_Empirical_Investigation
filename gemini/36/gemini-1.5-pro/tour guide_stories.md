The midday sun beat down on the bleached stones of the Forum Romanum, turning the air thick and shimmering.  Dust motes danced in the columns of light that pierced the gaps in the crumbling brickwork.  Cicadas buzzed insistently from the umbrella pines clinging to the Palatine Hill, their drone a constant backdrop to the hushed whispers of tourists and the clatter of camera shutters. Amelia "Mia" Rossi, her dark hair pulled back in a tight bun to escape the oppressive heat, adjusted the microphone clipped to her crisp white linen shirt.  She gestured towards the shattered remains of the Temple of Vesta, her voice carrying clearly over the background hum.  “As you can see, even in ruins, the grandeur of ancient Rome is breathtaking…”
