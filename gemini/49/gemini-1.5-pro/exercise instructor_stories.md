The relentless Miami sun beat down on Ocean Drive, turning the turquoise water into a shimmering mirage.  Inside the air-conditioned sanctuary of "Sculpt & Sweat,"  Amelia “Mia” Rodriguez adjusted the microphone clipped to her neon pink tank top.  Her dark hair, pulled back into a high ponytail, swished against her shoulders as she surveyed the room. Twenty-five expectant faces, a mix of toned regulars and nervous newbies, stared back at her.  Giant ferns draped from the ceiling, attempting to soften the industrial chic of exposed brick and polished concrete floors.  A pulsing bass throbbed through the speakers, a prelude to the high-energy Latin-infused workout Mia had meticulously planned.  This was her domain, her kingdom of burn and bliss.  And at 8:00 am sharp, it was time to reign.
