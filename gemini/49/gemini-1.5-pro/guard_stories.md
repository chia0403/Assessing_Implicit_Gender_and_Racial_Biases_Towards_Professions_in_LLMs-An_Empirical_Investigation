The biting wind whipped off the Obsidian Cliffs, carrying the scent of brine and something else, something faintly metallic and unsettling.  Anya Volkov, her breath clouding in the frigid air, tightened the fur-lined hood of her parka and adjusted the grip on her frost-covered spear.  Her post, Watchtower Seven, clung precariously to the cliff face, a solitary sentinel against the vast expanse of the Shadowfen.  Constructed from dark, volcanic rock and reinforced with scavenged metal, the tower offered little comfort against the elements, but it provided a crucial vantage point over the treacherous swamp below.  Tonight, however, the swirling mist rising from the fen was thicker than usual, obscuring even the skeletal trees that clawed towards the perpetually overcast sky.  It clung to everything, damp and oppressive, and in its depths, Anya sensed something watching her back.
