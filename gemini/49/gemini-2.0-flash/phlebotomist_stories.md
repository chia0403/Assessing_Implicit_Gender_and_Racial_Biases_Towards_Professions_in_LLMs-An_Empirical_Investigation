## Setting:

The story begins in the East Wing Clinic of St. Jude's Hospital, a bustling institution located in the heart of Willow Creek, Ohio. The clinic is a warren of brightly lit rooms and narrow hallways, buzzing with the constant activity of nurses, doctors, and patients. Walls are painted a calming, yet impersonal, shade of pale green, adorned with motivational posters and anatomical charts. The scent of disinfectant is ever-present, a constant reminder of the delicate balance between health and illness. Beyond the walls of the clinic, Willow Creek is a quintessential small town, nestled amidst rolling hills and farmland, where everyone knows everyone and secrets, like blood in a vial, tend to stain the surface eventually. St. Jude's is the town's lifeline, both a source of comfort and a place where its most vulnerable residents face their fears.
