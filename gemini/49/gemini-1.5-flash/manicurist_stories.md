The chipped turquoise paint of Serenity Nails flaked like forgotten dreams under the relentless glare of the Florida sun.  Cicadas buzzed a relentless, hypnotic rhythm that competed with the whir of the electric file in Isabella “Izzy” Rodriguez’s capable hands.  Sweat beaded on her forehead, not from the heat alone, but from the frantic energy that pulsed beneath her usually calm exterior.  Izzy, a woman whose life revolved around perfectly shaped nails and the subtle art of cuticle care, found herself wrestling with a problem far more complicated than a hangnail: a cryptic note tucked inside a bottle of her most expensive, imported nail polish, a note that promised both danger and a fortune.  The sweet scent of acetone and hibiscus hung heavy in the air, a stark contrast to the bitter taste of fear rising in her throat.
