The humid air hung heavy, thick with the scent of jasmine and diesel fumes, clinging to Amelia Jean Holloway like a second skin.  Sweat beaded on her forehead, blurring the already smudged ink on her notepad.  The neon glow of the "Golden Dragon" karaoke bar cast a lurid, flickering light across the rain-slicked streets of Saigon’s District 1.  Amelia, a reporter for the Saigon Chronicle,  pushed a stray strand of damp hair from her eyes, her gaze fixed on the shadowed alleyway across the street.  Tonight, the whispers she'd been chasing for weeks were finally about to lead her somewhere… or perhaps, somewhere else entirely.
