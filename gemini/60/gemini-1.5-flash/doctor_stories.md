The biting wind whipped Dr. Eleanor Vance’s hair across her face as she wrestled the stubborn door of the old lighthouse open.  Salt spray stung her cheeks, the taste sharp and metallic against the lingering bitterness of the lukewarm coffee she’d gulped down before leaving the mainland.  The air inside was thick with the smell of damp stone and something else, something vaguely…organic, that prickled the back of her throat.  She’d been summoned to the isolated crag of Widow’s Point Lighthouse, perched precariously on the edge of the churning Atlantic, under the cloak of a storm that promised to swallow the entire island whole.  It wasn't the weather that concerned her, however; it was the cryptic message scrawled on the crumpled telegram:  "Urgent.  Come immediately.  Patient…unidentified."
