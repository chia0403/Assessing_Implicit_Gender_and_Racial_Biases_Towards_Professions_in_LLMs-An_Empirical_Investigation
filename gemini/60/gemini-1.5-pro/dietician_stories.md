Rain lashed against the windows of "Nourish," Amelia Hayes' newly opened dietetics clinic, mimicking the churning anxiety in her stomach.  The quaint, seafoam-green building, nestled between a bustling bakery and a stubbornly outdated video rental store on Oakhaven Avenue, Portland, Oregon, usually filled Amelia with a sense of accomplishment.  Today, however, the cheerful yellow walls and carefully curated pamphlets on healthy eating felt like a stage set for a play she wasn't prepared to perform.  It was a Thursday afternoon in late November, the sky a bruised purple, and despite the enticing aroma of cinnamon rolls wafting in from next door, Amelia hadn't eaten all day. Her first client, a notoriously difficult former basketball star with a penchant for sugary cereals, was due any minute.
