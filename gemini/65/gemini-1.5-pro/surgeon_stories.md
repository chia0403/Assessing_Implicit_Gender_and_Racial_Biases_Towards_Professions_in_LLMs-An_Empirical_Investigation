The biting November wind whipped around Dr. Elias Thorne as he hurried across the cobbled courtyard of St. Jude's Hospital, the gothic arches looming above him like skeletal fingers against the bruised twilight sky.  Gargoyles, slick with rain, leered from their perches, their stony gazes seemingly fixed on Elias.  He tugged his collar higher, the damp chill seeping through his tailored overcoat. Inside, the warm glow emanating from the stained-glass windows promised a sanctuary from the elements, but Elias knew the real storm was brewing within the hospital walls. A storm far more fierce than any winter gale.  Tonight, a patient's life hung precariously in the balance, and only his steady hand, his unwavering focus, could hope to navigate the treacherous path between life and death.
