The biting November wind whipped around Dr. Elias Thorne as he hurried across the cobbled marketplace of Port Blossom, Maine. Flakes of snow, fat and lazy, began to drift down, dusting the vibrant red and green Christmas decorations already strung across the shopfronts. Elias tugged his scarf higher, the wool scratching against his beard.  The aroma of pine needles and woodsmoke mingled with the briny tang of the sea air, a familiar scent that usually calmed him. But today, a knot of unease tightened in his stomach.  Port Blossom was a town steeped in secrets, and Elias, despite his gentle hands and healing touch, found himself caught in the heart of one. He just didn't know it yet.
