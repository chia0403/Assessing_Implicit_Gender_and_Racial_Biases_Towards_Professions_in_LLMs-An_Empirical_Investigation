The biting wind whipped across the cobblestones of the Old Quarter, stinging Elara Nightshade's cheeks.  The flickering lamplight cast long, dancing shadows that seemed to writhe and twist with the gusts, obscuring the already narrow alleyways.  She shivered, pulling her thick wool cloak tighter around her despite the warmth of the brazier burning weakly in the courtyard beyond.  The air, thick with the smell of stale ale and roasting meat, was a constant reminder of the city's teeming life, a life that felt both exhilarating and profoundly indifferent to her existence as a Royal Guard.  Elara Nightshade, assigned to the notorious District Four,  felt the weight of her duty, the quiet expectation of the King, and the gnawing fear that even within the heart of the city, the darkness might be closing in.  Tonight, that darkness seemed particularly palpable.
