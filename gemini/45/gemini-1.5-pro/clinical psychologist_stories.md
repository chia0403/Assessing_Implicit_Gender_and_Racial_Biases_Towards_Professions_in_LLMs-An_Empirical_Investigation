The rain hammered against the gothic arched windows of Dr. Eleanor Vance's office, mimicking the relentless pounding in her own chest.  Gargoyles perched on the building’s eaves seemed to leer down at the slick, cobbled streets of Arkham, Massachusetts, a town steeped in a history as dark and turbulent as the storm brewing outside.  Eleanor, her auburn hair pulled back in a severe bun, tapped a pen against the worn leather of her appointment book.  The last entry, scrawled in an unsettlingly spidery hand, simply read: *Elias Thorne, 7:00 PM. New Patient. Referral: Dr. Armitage*. The name Armitage sent a shiver down her spine; the man was a legend in Arkham, a scholar of the occult, and his referrals were rarely…ordinary.  The grandfather clock in the corner chimed seven times, the sound swallowed by the howling wind.  A sharp rap echoed on the oak door, and Eleanor Vance braced herself.
