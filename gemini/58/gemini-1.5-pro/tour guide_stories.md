The salt spray kissed Amelia "Amy" Reyes's face as she leaned against the railing of the *Mermaid's Kiss*, a battered but beloved ferry chugging its way towards Aisling Isle.  The island, a jagged emerald claw jutting out of the turbulent Irish Sea, was shrouded in a mist that clung to the cliffs like a second skin. This wasn't unusual; Aisling Isle was known for its moody weather, as fickle as the legendary fairies said to inhabit its craggy slopes. Amy tightened her windbreaker, the embroidered logo of "Celtic Charms Tours" barely visible beneath the salt-encrusted fabric.  This was her hundredth tour to the island, yet a shiver of anticipation still ran down her spine. Aisling Isle held secrets, whispered on the wind and hidden in the crumbling stones of ancient ruins.  And today, Amy had a feeling, something extraordinary was about to be revealed.
