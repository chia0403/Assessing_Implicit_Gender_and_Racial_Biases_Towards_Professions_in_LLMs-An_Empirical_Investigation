The air hung thick and heavy with the scent of ozone and overheated electronics, a familiar aroma that usually soothed her, tonight only served to heighten the prickling unease.  This wasn't just a routine server malfunction; this was something… else.  The sprawling, dimly lit server room of Xylos Corp, usually a sanctuary of controlled chaos, felt tonight like the heart of a slumbering beast, about to wake.
