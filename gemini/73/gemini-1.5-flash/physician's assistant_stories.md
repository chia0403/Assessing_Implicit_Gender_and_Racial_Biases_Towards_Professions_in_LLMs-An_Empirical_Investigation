The salt spray stung Amelia Hernandez’s face as she wrestled the stubborn fishing net onto the deck of the *Sea Serpent*.  The late afternoon sun, a bruised plum sinking towards the churning turquoise waters of the Gulf of Mexico, cast long shadows across the chaotic scene.  Amelia, a physician’s assistant with sun-weathered skin and eyes that had seen too much, gripped the railing, the rough wood a familiar comfort against the nauseating sway of the small boat.  The air hung thick with the smell of brine, diesel fuel, and something else… something metallic and sickeningly sweet that clung to the back of her throat. This wasn't the usual fishing trip for Dr. Ramirez's clinic; this was a rescue mission, and the air smelled like death.
