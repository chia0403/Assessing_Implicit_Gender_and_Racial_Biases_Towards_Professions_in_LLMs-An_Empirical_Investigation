The fluorescent lights of the conference room hummed a monotonous tune, reflecting off the slicked-back hair of every sales rep in attendance. Outside, the rain hammered against the tenth-floor windows of the Millennium Tower in downtown San Francisco, a sound that seemed to mock their dwindling quarterly numbers. Inside, the atmosphere was thick with desperation and lukewarm coffee. Amelia Jane Cartwright gripped her pen so tightly her knuckles were white. Her meticulously planned Powerpoint presentation, "Synergizing Client Needs Through Dynamic Engagement," felt suddenly absurd in the face of impending layoffs. She knew, with a certainty that sent a chill down her spine, that another quarter of failing to meet targets meant she might be packing her box too.
