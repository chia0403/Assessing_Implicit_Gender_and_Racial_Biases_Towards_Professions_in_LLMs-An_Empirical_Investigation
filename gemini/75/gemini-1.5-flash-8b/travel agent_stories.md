Rain lashed against the panoramic windows of the "Wanderlust World" office, mirroring the tempest brewing inside Amelia "Amy"  Abernathy.  The office, typically a vibrant kaleidoscope of brochures, maps, and overflowing travel journals, was shrouded in a muted grey light.  Empty coffee cups littered the polished mahogany desk, their remnants a testament to the sleepless hours Amy had spent wrestling with a client's increasingly demanding itinerary.  Outside, the city of Aethelgard, a sprawling metropolis built on the remnants of a forgotten empire, pulsed with a rhythm Amy couldn't quite connect with.  The towering spires of the Cathedral of the Ascendant Sun, gleaming obsidian in the downpour, loomed over the street below, a stark contrast to the fragile peace Amy desperately sought.  Amy, her auburn hair plastered to her forehead, stared at the itinerary, the meticulously planned route to the mythical Oasis of Serenity now a tangled mess of conflicting desires.  Her client, Lord Valerius Thorne, a renowned antiquities collector and self-proclaimed "explorer of the extraordinary," had requested an itinerary that combined historical pilgrimage with near-impossible feats of mountain climbing.  Now, the mountain climbing was proving far more challenging than any historical pilgrimage.
