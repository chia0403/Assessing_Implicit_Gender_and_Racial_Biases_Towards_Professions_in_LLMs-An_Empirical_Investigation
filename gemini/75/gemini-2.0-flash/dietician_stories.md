## The Salt Flats Sermon

The shimmering heat haze danced above the Bonneville Salt Flats, making the mountains in the distance appear to ripple like melting ice cream. A merciless sun beat down on the blinding white expanse, reflecting upward with the intensity of a thousand mirrors. Inside her dusty, rented Ford Escape, Dr. Eleanor Josephine Pritchard felt the sweat prickling beneath her linen blouse despite the air conditioning blasting on high.

She adjusted her sunglasses, peering at the GPS screen. “You’ve reached your destination,” the robotic voice announced, as helpful as ever. Eleanor sighed. Her destination appeared to be… nothing. Just a vast, unbroken ocean of salt stretching to the horizon in every direction. 

This, she thought, had better be worth it.
