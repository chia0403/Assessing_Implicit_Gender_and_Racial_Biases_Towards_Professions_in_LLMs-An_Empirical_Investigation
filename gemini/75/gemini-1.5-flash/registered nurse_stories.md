The humid Louisiana air hung heavy and sweet, thick with the scent of honeysuckle and impending rain.  Cicadas buzzed a relentless symphony, a soundtrack to the oppressive heat clinging to the cypress trees lining the driveway of Beau Chene Plantation.  Registered Nurse Evangeline Dupuis, wiping sweat from her brow with the back of her hand, felt the familiar prickle of anxiety beneath her crisp white uniform.  The wrought-iron gates, black against the bruised purple sky, seemed to loom, a foreboding entrance to the opulent yet decaying mansion that was her current assignment.  Inside, waited not just a wealthy family, but a mystery shrouded in the shadows of ancient oaks and whispered secrets.
