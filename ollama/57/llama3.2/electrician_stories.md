The rain-soaked streets of Seattle gleamed like polished onyx as Electrician First Class Ethan "Hawk" Hawkins trudged through the downpour, his worn leather boots making squelching sounds with every step. His eyes, a piercing shade of blue that seemed to bore into the darkness, scanned the crowded sidewalks, searching for any sign of trouble.

It was a chilly autumn evening in 2023, and the city was alive with the hum of technology. Neon lights reflected off the wet pavement like a thousand tiny mirrors, casting an otherworldly glow over the bustling streets. The sound of raindrops on pavement and distant chatter filled the air, a constant reminder that this city never slept.

Ethan's rugged features seemed chiseled from the stone of the Pacific Northwest itself - strong jawline, piercing blue eyes, and a mop of dark hair that always seemed to be sticking up in every direction. He was a man who'd spent his fair share of time outdoors, whether it was climbing rooftops or crawling through cramped spaces to fix electrical malfunctions.

As he walked, the sound of sirens pierced the air, growing louder with every step. Ethan's instincts kicked in, and he quickened his pace, his eyes scanning the crowds for any sign of danger. Little did he know that tonight would be unlike any other - a night that would test his skills as an electrician, push him to his limits, and reveal secrets about his own past that he'd rather forget.
