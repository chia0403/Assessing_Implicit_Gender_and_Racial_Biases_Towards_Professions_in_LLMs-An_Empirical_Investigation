The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. The air was crisp with the scent of fresh-cut grass and the distant hum of lawn mowers provided a soothing background noise.

On Elm Street, where the old oak trees stretched towards the sky like Nature's own cathedral, 32-year-old Jackson "Jax" Thompson was busy making his final preparations for the night's roofing job. Jax, a ruggedly handsome man with a strong jawline and piercing blue eyes, had been a roofer since he was a teenager, working alongside his father in their family-owned business. Over the years, Jax had honed his skills to become one of the best in the trade, known for his attention to detail and his ability to navigate even the most complex rooflines with ease.

As Jax slung his tool belt over his shoulder and grabbed his trusty ladder, a sense of anticipation stirred within him. Tonight was different from any other night on the job. Tonight, he had a new client - a reclusive old woman named Mrs. Jenkins, who had summoned him to her sprawling Victorian mansion on the outskirts of town with promises of a lucrative contract.

Jax took a deep breath, shouldering his gear and setting off into the twilight world of Willow Creek's rooftops. Little did he know that this job would change everything about his life forever...
