The cold wind outside howled through the narrow alleys of the old town, causing the windows of Axel's workshop to rattle like fragile glass. The sound was familiar, yet unsettling, like an old friend who had wandered off and left behind a faint memory that refused to fade away.

He was lost in thought, his mind whirling with the intricacies of radio theory and old broadcasts that had captivated him since childhood.
