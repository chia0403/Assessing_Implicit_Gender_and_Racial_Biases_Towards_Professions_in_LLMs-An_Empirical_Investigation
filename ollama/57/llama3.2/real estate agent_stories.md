The sun was setting over the sleepy coastal town of Crescent Bay, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint shops and restaurants that lined the streets. Amidst the peaceful evening atmosphere, real estate agent Alexandra Joy Reynolds stood outside her office, sipping on a cup of coffee as she gazed out at the ocean.

Alexandra, or Alex to those who knew her, had spent years building a reputation as one of Crescent Bay's top agents. She knew every nook and cranny of the town, from the best spots for surfers to the most charming boutiques in the art district. But despite her success, Alex couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A sense of unease had been growing inside her for weeks, and she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was.

As she stood there, lost in thought, a sleek black car pulled up beside her office, sending a ripple through the quiet evening air. The driver's door swung open, revealing a tall, imposing figure with piercing blue eyes that seemed to bore into Alex's very soul. "Alexandra Reynolds," he said, his voice low and commanding. "I'm here to discuss the sale of Willow Creek Estates."
