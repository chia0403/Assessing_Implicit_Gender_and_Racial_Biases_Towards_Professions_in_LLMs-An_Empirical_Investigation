---

The rain-soaked streets of downtown Tokyo gleamed like polished onyx as Aurora Ryujin stepped out of her office building, squinting through the misty veil that shrouded the city. As one of the top salespeople at Nakamura Inc., a leading Japanese tech firm, Aurora was no stranger to the cutthroat world of business, but today she felt like she was on the line.

Aurora's bright pink hair - a trademark of her youth as a punk rock enthusiast - had been styled neatly into a sleek bob by her company's stern CEO, Mrs. Matsumoto. The tailored suit and designer heels that accompanied it only added to the sense of professional polish that Aurora wore like a mask.

She took a deep breath, feeling the cool night air fill her lungs as she gazed out at the towering skyscrapers of Shinjuku. Her latest sales target was looming - a meeting with a potential client in the financial district who could make or break Nakamura's chances at landing the massive AI contract that had been on everyone's mind for months.

With a practiced smile, Aurora pulled her phone from her purse and checked the time. She still had 20 minutes before her appointment, plenty of time to grab a quick coffee at the Starbucks across the street and collect her thoughts.

But as she walked towards the convenience store, her eyes caught sight of something that made her heart skip a beat - a flyer plastered on the window with a message that sent shivers down her spine: "Meet me at the old clock tower at midnight. Come alone."

Aurora's instincts immediately kicked in, warning her that this was not a coincidence. And yet...
