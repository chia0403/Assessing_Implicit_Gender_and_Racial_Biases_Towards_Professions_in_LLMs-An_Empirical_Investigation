Here's a possible story opening and setting:

The sun was setting over the vast expanse of the Wyoming prairie, casting a golden glow over the rugged landscape. In the small town of Rawlins, nestled in the heart of the Wind River Range, Jack Ryder "Hawk" Hawkins was preparing for another long day of work on the open-pit copper mine that dominated the local economy.

As the owner's son and a seasoned heavy equipment operator, Hawk had spent years honing his skills on the job site, earning the respect of his peers and the grudging admiration of his father. With over ten years of experience behind the wheel of massive machines like the Caterpillar 797F mining truck, Hawk was known for his unflappable calm under pressure, his razor-sharp attention to detail, and his uncanny ability to coax even the most recalcitrant equipment back into working order.

But despite his rugged exterior and rugged reputation, Hawk harbored a secret that he kept locked deep within his chest. A secret that had been festering for months, growing in intensity with each passing day...
