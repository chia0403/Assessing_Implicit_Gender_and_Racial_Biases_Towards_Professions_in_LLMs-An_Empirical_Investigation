Here's a potential story opening and setting:

It was a drizzly autumn evening in Portland, Oregon, and rain poured down on the city like a relentless curtain, shrouding the streets and alleys in a misty grayness. The smell of damp earth and ozone filled the air as Emily Jane Wilson stepped out of her office building, cradling a steaming cup of coffee in one hand and her trusty notebook and pen in the other.

Emily was a reporter for the Portland Tribune, specializing in investigative journalism and exposing corruption within the city's government. She had spent years building a reputation for herself as a tenacious and dogged journalist, willing to take risks to uncover the truth.

As she walked down the sidewalk, Emily couldn't shake off the feeling that something big was brewing in the city. She had been working on a tip about a potential scandal involving a prominent city official, and she was determined to get to the bottom of it before anyone else did.

The sound of raindrops hitting her umbrella accompanied her as she made her way to her car, parked behind a nondescript office building on 5th Avenue. It was a modest setup for a journalist, but Emily had grown accustomed to the humble surroundings, and she knew that sometimes the most important stories came from the unlikeliest of places.

As she slipped into her seat, Emily took a sip of her coffee and began to flip through the notes in her notebook, mentally preparing herself for the long night ahead. Little did she know, this was just the beginning of a journey that would take her down a rabbit hole of corruption, deceit, and danger...
