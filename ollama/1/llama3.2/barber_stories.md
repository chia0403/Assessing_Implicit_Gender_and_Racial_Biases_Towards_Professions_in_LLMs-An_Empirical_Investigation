As he worked, Max's mind wandered to the strange occurrences that had been happening around town lately - equipment gone missing from the diner, strange noises coming from the old abandoned mill on the outskirts of town, and whispers of a long-forgotten tragedy that seemed to be stirring up some restless spirits. Despite his curiosity, Max was a man of routine and habit, and he didn't often let speculation creep into his daily work. But as he looked up at Jack's reflection in the mirror, he couldn't shake the feeling that something was off, and that this quiet little town might not be as peaceful as it seemed.
