The rain-soaked streets of downtown Seattle gleamed like polished onyx as Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stepped out of her small apartment, rubbing the sleep from her eyes. It was 5:30 AM on a drizzly Wednesday morning, and Lexi was already running late for her first day at the law firm where she worked as a paralegal. She took a deep breath, feeling the crisp Pacific Northwest air fill her lungs, and began her short walk to the office.

Lexi had spent countless hours researching her favorite author, Dashiell Hammett, on the bus rides home from work, dreaming of becoming a writer herself one day. But for now, she was content with the familiar rhythms of law, working for the respected firm of Smith & Associates, and unraveling the tangled threads of human conflict alongside the city's most skilled lawyers.

As she splashed through puddles on her way to the office, Lexi couldn't help but feel a thrill of excitement. Her new boss, Rachel Lee, had promised her a steady stream of high-profile cases, and Lexi was eager to dive in and prove herself. Little did she know that this day would be unlike any other...
