Rain lashed against the windows of Ms. Elara Montgomery’s office, mimicking the storm brewing inside her. Stacks of college applications teetered precariously on her desk, each one representing a student's hopes, fears, and dreams – all piled onto her already overflowing plate. The fluorescent lights hummed overhead, casting a sterile glow over the posters lining the walls, each featuring inspirational quotes about perseverance and achieving your goals. 

But Elara couldn’t help but feel like a hypocrite staring at them. Her own aspirations seemed to be fading faster than the autumn leaves outside her window.
