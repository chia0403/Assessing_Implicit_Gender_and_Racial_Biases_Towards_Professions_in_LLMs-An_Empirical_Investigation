Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stepped out of her small, cozy office at the edge of the university campus, breathing in the crisp evening air that carried the scent of blooming lilacs and fresh-cut grass. As a counseling psychologist, Lexi had spent countless hours listening to students navigate the ups and downs of college life, but tonight felt different. Tonight, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.

The setting sun cast long shadows across the quad, where groups of students were still lingering in conversation or playing games on their phones. The sounds of laughter and music carried from the nearby student union, but Lexi's ears perked up at a faint knock on her office door. She hadn't expected anyone to visit after hours, especially not someone who could potentially disrupt the fragile balance she'd worked so hard to maintain.

With a mix of curiosity and trepidation, Lexi made her way back inside, wondering what unexpected visitor had arrived in the dead of night.
