The sun was setting over the small town of Oakdale, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. On Elm Street, where he spent most of his waking hours, Jack Edward "Hawk" Hawkins made his way to the local hardware store for one last supply run before closing time. As a seasoned roofer with years of experience under his belt, Hawk had seen it all - from leaky gutters to crumbling chimneys. He was known around town for his exceptional craftsmanship and no-nonsense attitude, earning him the nickname "Hawk" among the locals.

As he pushed open the creaky door of the hardware store, a bell above it rang out, announcing his arrival to the sleepy evening crowd. The store's owner, Old Man Jenkins, looked up from behind the counter with a friendly smile and said, "Hey Hawk! What can I get for you tonight?"

Hawk nodded curtly, scanning the shelves for just one more thing: a new roll of roofing felt that he knew would make all the difference in his current project. Little did he know, this evening's supply run would be anything but ordinary...
