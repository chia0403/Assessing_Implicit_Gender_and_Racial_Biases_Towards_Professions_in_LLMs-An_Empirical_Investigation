As she sipped her lukewarm coffee, Emily Anne Wilson gazed out the rain-soaked window of the office building, watching as the droplets slid down the pane like tears on a worn face. The fluorescent lights above hummed softly, casting an eerie glow over the drab gray walls and rows of cubicles. It was 8:45 AM on a typical Monday morning at Smith & Co., a mid-sized insurance firm in downtown Los Angeles.

Emily Anne, or Em as she was known to her coworkers, had been working as the administrative assistant to the company's CEO for three years. Her days blended together in a haze of paperwork, phone calls, and the occasional meeting with a client who seemed more interested in her hairstyle than her work. Despite the monotony, Em took pride in her job, knowing that without her, the office would grind to a halt.

As she began to sort through the stack of papers on her desk, Em's mind wandered to the strange phone call she had received the night before from an unknown number. The voice on the other end had been cryptic, but the words "Meet me at the coffee shop on 5th and Main" still echoed in her ears, leaving a shiver down her spine. Who could be calling her so early, and what did they want?
