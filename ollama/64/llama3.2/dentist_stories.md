Dr. Emmeline "Evie" Thompson wiped the sweat from her forehead as she gazed out at the small, seaside town of Tidal Cove. The salty air filled her lungs as she stood on the edge of the oceanfront pier, watching the sun set over the waves. It was moments like these that reminded Evie why she had left the hustle and bustle of city life behind to start anew in this quiet coastal community.

As a dentist with a passion for restoring smiles and healing lives, Evie had always been drawn to the personal connection she made with her patients. There was something special about fixing a crooked tooth or whitening a smile that brought a sense of satisfaction and fulfillment to her work. But it wasn't just the dental practice that had led her to Tidal Cove – it was also the promise of a simpler, more peaceful life.

Evie's eyes scanned the bustling pier, where fishermen were hauling in their daily catch and children laughed and played in the sand. She felt a sense of peace wash over her as she took in the familiar sights and sounds of this place she now called home. Little did she know, however, that her life was about to take a dramatic turn – one that would test her skills as a dentist, but also push her to confront secrets and lies that had haunted her for years.
