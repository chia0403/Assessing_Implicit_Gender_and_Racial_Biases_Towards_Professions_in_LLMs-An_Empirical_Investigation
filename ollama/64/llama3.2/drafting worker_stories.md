The dimly lit office was a far cry from the bright lights and bustling energy of the construction site where Emilia Alexandra "Mia" Thompson spent most of her days as a drafting worker. The smell of stale coffee and worn leather wafted through the air, mingling with the faint scent of graphite and ink on Mia's hands.

It had been a long day, filled with endless rows of blueprints and tedious revisions for one of the city's largest real estate developments. As she packed up her things to head home, Mia couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The project manager's latest changes had thrown the entire timeline into chaos, and Mia's usually reliable computer had decided to take a break, refusing to boot up until further notice.

As she trudged through the drizzly evening air, the neon lights of the city's nightlife flickered like fireflies on the horizon. The rain-soaked streets were eerily quiet, save for the distant hum of a lone motorcycle and the soft crunch of gravel beneath her boots. Mia's thoughts turned to the mystery that had been plaguing her all week: strange symbols etched into the blueprints, seemingly appearing out of nowhere.

She quickened her pace, wondering if she could make it home before the rain really started coming down. Little did she know, tonight was going to be one night like any other...
