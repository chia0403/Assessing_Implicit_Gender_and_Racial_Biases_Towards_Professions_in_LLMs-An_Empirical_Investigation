The storm had rolled in like a black cloud on wheels, shrouding the small town of Ravenswood in a damp, eerie gloom. Rain lashed down on the streets, drumming a relentless beat against the pavement, while the wind howled through the deserted main street like a chorus of lost souls.

Inside the cramped, cluttered workshop of "Tech's Repair" - the ramshackle shop that served as both residence and workplace for Jack Sebastian Thornton III - the air was thick with the smell of ozone and burnt circuits. Jack, a ruggedly handsome young man with a mop of unruly brown hair and a scattering of freckles across his nose, hunched over a tangled mess of wires and electronics, his eyes narrowed in concentration.

As a skilled radio technician, Jack had spent countless hours diagnosing and repairing faulty communications equipment for the town's residents. But tonight, as he worked to fix a particularly tricky problem with the local sheriff's department's dispatch system, he couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A nagging sense of unease had been building all day, fueled by the increasingly ominous weather and the strange, disconnected calls that had been flooding in from outlying areas.

Just as Jack was starting to make progress on the repair, his phone rang, shrill and insistent. He hesitated for a moment before answering, his heart sinking as he heard the voice on the other end - a low, gravelly tone that sent a shiver down his spine...
