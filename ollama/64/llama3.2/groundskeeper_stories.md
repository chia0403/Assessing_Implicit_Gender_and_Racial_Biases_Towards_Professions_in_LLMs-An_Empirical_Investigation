The misty dawn broke over the sleepy town of Willow Creek, casting a mystical gloom over the crumbling Victorian homes and the worn, dirt streets that wound like a serpent through its heart. Amidst this eerie silence, Emilia Mae Taylor stepped out into the chill morning air, her breath visible in the faint breeze that rustled the leaves of the ancient trees lining the garden paths.

As she made her way to the old mansion on Elmwood Drive, the sound of gravel crunching beneath her boots was the only disturbance to the stillness. The once-grand estate had been abandoned for years, its grandeur and beauty slowly being reclaimed by nature as the overgrown gardens and crumbling walls seemed to whisper secrets to the wind.

Emilia Mae, or Mia as she liked to be called, took pride in her work as the groundskeeper of Willow Creek's most iconic property. She knew every hidden corner, every creaky wooden plank, and every secret garden path like the back of her hand. And yet, despite her deep connection to this place, Mia couldn't shake the feeling that something was off, that a subtle shift in the natural balance had taken hold, leaving her with an inexplicable sense of unease.

As she approached the mansion's massive wooden door, a faint hum began to build in the air, like the distant thrumming of a harp string. Mia's skin prickled with gooseflesh as she pushed open the creaking door and stepped into the musty darkness within, her lantern casting eerie shadows on the walls...
