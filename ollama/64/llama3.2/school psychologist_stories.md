Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood at the edge of the playground, watching as the last bell of the day rang out across the emptying hallways of Oakdale High School. The warm sunlight cast long shadows across the asphalt, and the sound of children's laughter echoed through the air. Lexi took a deep breath, feeling the familiar mix of relief and exhaustion that came with the end of another chaotic school day.

As the school psychologist at Oakdale High, Lexi had seen it all - the triumphant tears of students who'd overcome bullies, the anguished faces of those struggling to connect with their peers, and the quiet desperation of teachers at the breaking point. She'd spent years building a network of relationships that went beyond mere therapy sessions, working tirelessly to create a safe and supportive environment for every student.

But as she gazed out across the playground, Lexi's mind was elsewhere - on the mysterious letter she'd received that morning, its words cryptic and unsettling: "The darkness is growing. Come find me in room 214."

Lexi felt a shiver run down her spine as she wondered what secrets Oakdale High might be hiding, and why this particular message had been addressed to her alone. Little did she know, the shadows that lurked in every corner of the school were only just beginning to stir...
