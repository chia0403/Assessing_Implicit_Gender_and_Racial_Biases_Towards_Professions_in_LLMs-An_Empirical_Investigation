As the sun rose over the small coastal town of Tidal Cove, Exercise Instructor Alexandra Ruby Waters began her day with a quiet moment of reflection on the weathered wooden deck of her oceanfront apartment. The salty sea air filled her lungs as she gazed out at the turquoise waters of the cove, watching the sailboats bob gently in the harbor below.

Alexandra, or Alex to those who knew her, took a deep breath, feeling the tension from the previous day's yoga class still lingering in her muscles. She had been teaching for over a decade, and while she loved helping her clients achieve their fitness goals, lately, she'd been struggling with her own sense of purpose.

Just as she was about to start her morning routine, Alex's phone buzzed on the small table beside her. It was a text from an unknown number: "Meet me at the old pier at low tide if you want to know the truth."

A shiver ran down Alex's spine as she stared out at the water, wondering what secrets Tidal Cove held that could be hidden in plain sight. Little did she know, her life was about to take a dramatic turn, one that would challenge everything she thought she knew about herself and her beloved town.
