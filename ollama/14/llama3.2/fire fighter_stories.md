The sun had just set over the small town of Oakdale, casting a warm orange glow over the bustling streets of Main Street. Firefighter Ethan Ryder Jackson stood outside the local diner, sipping on a cup of black coffee and watching as the patrons streamed in for dinner. It was 7:45 PM on a Friday night, and the diner was already starting to clear out.

Ethan, a tall and imposing figure with broad shoulders and a strong jawline, checked his watch for what felt like the hundredth time that evening. He had just gotten off a 12-hour shift at Station 17, and he was eager to get home and put his feet up. But as he scanned the parking lot, his eyes landed on a familiar figure - a sleek black SUV pulled up to the curb outside the diner, its tinted windows reflecting the glow of the streetlights.

Ethan's instincts immediately went on high alert. He knew that look - it was the same one his partner, Lieutenant Mike Reynolds, had been sporting for weeks now. It meant trouble, and Ethan could feel it in the pit of his stomach. Little did he know, tonight was going to be a night that would test everything he thought he knew about Oakdale...
