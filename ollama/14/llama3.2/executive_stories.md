A mysterious email had been sent to her just hours before, containing a cryptic message that had left her both intrigued and unsettled. The words "Project Nightshade" were typed in bold, black letters, accompanied by an attachment that read "Confidential".

Curiosity getting the better of her, Ava decided to head straight to the office to dig up more information about this enigmatic project. Little did she know, her life was about to take a drastic turn, one that would challenge everything she thought she knew about power, loyalty, and the true nature of ambition.
