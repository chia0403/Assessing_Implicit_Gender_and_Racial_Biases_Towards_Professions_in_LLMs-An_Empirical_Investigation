The rain-soaked streets of Seattle glistened like polished onyx as August "Augie" Thompson stepped out of his office building, shaking the water from his tailored navy suit. The misty veil that shrouded the city seemed to match the melancholy mood that had been settling in Augie's stomach all day.

As an insurance agent with Thompson & Associates, Augie was used to navigating the complexities of risk and reward, but nothing could have prepared him for the eerie feeling that something was off. It started with a mysterious phone call from an unknown number, claiming to be from an old acquaintance who'd been involved in some shady dealings back in his younger years.

The message had been cryptic, but one phrase had stuck with Augie: "Meet me at the Ace Hotel at midnight if you want to know what's really going on." Now, as he stood on the sidewalk, gazing up at the towering skyscrapers of downtown Seattle, Augie couldn't shake the feeling that his life was about to take a drastic turn.

The wind whipped through his hair, carrying with it the whispers of the city's secrets. Augie took a deep breath, shouldering his umbrella and heading into the unknown, ready to face whatever lay ahead.
