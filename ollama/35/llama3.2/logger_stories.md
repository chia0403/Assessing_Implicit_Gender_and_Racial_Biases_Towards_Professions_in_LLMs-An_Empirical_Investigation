Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a golden glow over the dense forest that lay just beyond its borders. The air was crisp and cool, filled with the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves. It was October 15th, and the logging season was in full swing.

In the heart of this forest, where the trees grew tall and the underbrush thick, lived a man named Jedediah "Jed" Everett Flynn III. A logger by trade, Jed had spent his entire life working on the woods, learning the ins and outs of the industry from his father and grandfather before him. He was a rugged, weathered man in his mid-40s, with a strong jawline and piercing blue eyes that seemed to bore into those he met.

Jed's cabin stood at the edge of the forest, surrounded by towering trees that seemed to stretch up to the sky. A small clearing in front of the cabin was filled with the familiar sounds of laughter and music, as Jed sat on his porch, strumming a gentle melody on his old guitar. The fireplace crackled behind him, casting a warm glow over the scene. But despite the peaceful atmosphere, Jed couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A sense of unease had been growing inside him all day, like a slow-burning fire that threatened to consume him whole.

As he played his final chord, a loud crack echoed through the forest, followed by an eerie silence. Jed's eyes narrowed, and he set his guitar aside, his hand instinctively reaching for the axe that hung from his belt. Something was out there, watching him...
