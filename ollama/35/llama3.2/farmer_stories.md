---

The sun was just starting to rise over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quiet streets and homes. In a small farmhouse on the outskirts of town, Elijah Jasper Wellesley stirred in his bed, rubbing the sleep from his eyes. He lived off the land, literally – his family's farm had been in operation for generations, and Elijah spent every waking moment tending to the crops, livestock, and trees that provided for his community.

Elijah was a hardworking man, with calloused hands and a weathered face that spoke of years spent under the sun. He loved the land, and it loved him back – but lately, he'd been feeling a growing sense of unease. Crops were failing, livestock were falling ill, and strange occurrences had begun to plague the farm at night.

As he swung his legs over the side of the bed and planted his feet firmly on the ground, Elijah felt a shiver run down his spine. He knew that he wasn't alone in this feeling – the entire town seemed to be on edge, and whispers of dark magic and ancient curses were starting to circulate.

Elijah took a deep breath, shouldering his worn denim jacket and padding over to the window to gaze out at the dawn-lit fields. The wind was blowing strong, carrying the scent of damp earth and new life. It was going to be a long day – but Elijah was ready for whatever challenges lay ahead.
