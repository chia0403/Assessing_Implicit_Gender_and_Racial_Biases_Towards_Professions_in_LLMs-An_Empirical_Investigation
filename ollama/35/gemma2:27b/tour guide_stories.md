The humid air hung thick in Charleston's historic district, carrying with it the scent of jasmine and saltwater.  Eleanor "Ellie" Beaumont adjusted her straw boater hat, its blue ribbon fluttering gently in the breeze that snaked down Rainbow Row. She smiled brightly at the small group gathering before her, their faces a mix of anticipation and slight weariness from the morning's walking tour.

"Welcome, everyone," Ellie chirped, her voice ringing with infectious enthusiasm. "I'm Ellie Beaumont, your guide for this afternoon's journey into Charleston's spooky side."


She gestured towards the pastel-colored houses lining the street. "Behind these charming facades lie centuries of secrets, whispered legends, and perhaps even a ghost or two..."
