The air hung thick with humidity, clinging to Elara Solstice like a second skin as she surveyed her outdoor bootcamp class. Palm trees swayed lazily in the gentle breeze, their fronds casting dappled shadows on the manicured lawns of the beachfront park. The Pacific Ocean shimmered beyond, a cerulean jewel framed by white sand and impossibly blue sky. 

It was paradise, Elara thought wryly, if you enjoyed being drenched in sweat before noon. Her class, a motley crew of tourists and locals, shuffled impatiently, their faces painted with a mixture of apprehension and determination.

Elara grinned, her own face flushed from the pre-class warm-up. She knew what they were thinking - that this fiery redhead with muscles sculpted from granite was about to make them regret signing up for an hour of "fun" in the sun.
