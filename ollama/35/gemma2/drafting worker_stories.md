The air hung thick with the scent of stale coffee and graphite dust, a familiar aroma to Elara Beatrice Thorne. She hunched over her drafting table, the worn surface scarred with countless revisions and erased lines. Outside, the sun was setting, casting long shadows across the bustling shipyard where she worked, turning the metallic skeletons of half-finished ships into monstrous silhouettes against the dying light. The rhythmic clang of hammers and the shouts of foremen echoed through the open-air workshop, a constant backdrop to Elara’s meticulous work.  

Elara, her brow furrowed in concentration, meticulously etched lines onto a blueprint with her silver stylus, the tip scratching softly against the parchment. This wasn't just another day at the office for her; she was finalizing the design for the "Sea Serpent," a revolutionary new vessel commissioned by Lord Alastor himself.  The pressure was immense – failure meant jeopardizing not only her own career but also the prestige of House Thorne, renowned for their engineering prowess.
