Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun was setting over the bustling city of Tokyo, casting a warm orange glow over the crowded streets of Shibuya. Among the sea of pedestrians rushing to and from work, one person stood out - Takashi Katsuragi, a charismatic salesperson with a quick wit and an even quicker tongue. With his tailored suit and confident stride, he navigated the throngs of people with ease, his eyes scanning the crowds for potential clients and opportunities.

Takashi was a top performer at his company, Morita Industries, and had made a name for himself as one of the most skilled salesmen in the industry. He had a gift for understanding his customers' needs and tailor-made solutions to meet them. But despite his success, Takashi couldn't shake the feeling that something was missing - a sense of purpose, perhaps, or a connection to the people he sold to.

As he walked down the street, Takashi's phone buzzed with an incoming call from his boss, Mr. Iwata. He hesitated for a moment before answering, knowing that this conversation could change everything.

"Good evening, Takashi," Mr. Iwata said, his voice low and serious. "I have some news that may interest you."

Takashi's heart skipped a beat as he listened to the words that would alter the course of his life forever...
