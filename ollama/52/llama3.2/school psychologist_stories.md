The rain-soaked streets of Oakwood City gleamed like polished onyx, reflecting the dim glow of streetlights that cast an eerie pallor over the deserted sidewalks. Inside the Oakwood High School, Dr. Emilia Victoria Patel navigated the dimly lit hallways with ease, her worn sneakers making barely a sound as she moved. As the school's psychologist, Emilia had seen her fair share of tears and troubles, but tonight felt different - like the storm outside was mirroring the turmoil brewing within the students' minds.

Emilia stopped in front of the classroom door to Room 214, a small space that doubled as her office. She took a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for the evening's session with one particularly troubled student. The clock on the wall read 3:47 PM - just ten minutes until the bell would ring, signaling the end of yet another long day at Oakwood High.

As she pushed open the door to Room 214, a sense of déjà vu washed over her. This was a space she'd spent countless hours in, listening to students' stories and offering guidance whenever they needed it most. But tonight felt different - as if the very fabric of her work was about to unravel, revealing secrets and lies that would change everything Emilia thought she knew about Oakwood High, its students, and herself.

"Welcome back, Dr. Patel," a soft voice whispered from behind the door.
