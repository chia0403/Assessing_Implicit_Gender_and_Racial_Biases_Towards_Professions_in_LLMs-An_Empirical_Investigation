As the sun set over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quiet streets and homes, Jack Alexander "Alex" Thompson made his way through the darkening alleys of downtown. A local electrician with years of experience, Alex had spent countless nights like this, juggling tools and circuitry as he worked to keep the town's lights on.

With his worn leather gloves and faded denim jeans, Alex looked every bit the part of a seasoned tradesman. His bright blue eyes scanned the streets, taking in the familiar sights of the town's eclectic mix of shops and eateries. The smell of fresh coffee wafted from the early morning café, mingling with the scent of freshly cut grass from the nearby park.

It was moments like these that Alex cherished – the stillness of the night, the feeling of being connected to something bigger than himself as he worked to keep the town's infrastructure humming. And yet, despite his love for the job, Alex couldn't shake the feeling that tonight would be different. A sense of restlessness had been building inside him all day, and it seemed to be growing stronger by the minute.

As he turned a corner onto Main Street, Alex spotted a small, flickering light in the distance – the iconic lantern outside the old Miller's Hardware store. It was usually extinguished at night, but tonight it seemed to be burning brighter than usual...
