It was an email with no sender or subject line, just a single sentence typed in all caps: "THE BUG YOU'RE LOOKING FOR IS JUST THE TIP OF THE ICEBERG." Max's heart rate had spiked as he read those words, and now his mind was racing with possibilities.

The rain continued to fall outside, drumming out a steady beat that seemed to match the pulse of Max's own anxiety. He rubbed his tired eyes, trying to shake off the feeling of unease that had settled over him like a shroud. But as he gazed around his cluttered apartment, he couldn't help but feel that something was off, that there were secrets lurking just beneath the surface, waiting to be uncovered.

Little did Max know, his life was about to take a dramatic turn, one that would throw him headlong into a world of intrigue and danger.
