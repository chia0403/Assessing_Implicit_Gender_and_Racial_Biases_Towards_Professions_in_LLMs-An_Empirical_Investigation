The rain poured down on the small town of Willow Creek like a relentless curse, casting a misty veil over the quaint streets and worn buildings that lined its Main Street. Inside the cozy office of "Numbers & Co.", bookkeeper Emilia Rose Thompson sat hunched over her desk, surrounded by stacks of dusty financial ledgers and crumpled up receipts.

As she pored over the accounts for the local diner, where she worked part-time to supplement her income as a freelance bookkeeper, Emilia's mind wandered to the mysterious letter that had arrived at her office earlier that morning. The envelope was plain white with no return address, but the typed message on the inside read: "Meet me at the old oak tree in Oakwood Park at midnight. Come alone."

Emilia had tried to brush it off as a prank from one of her coworkers, but something about the formal tone and the specific location sent a shiver down her spine. She had always been a numbers person, not a detective, but a part of her was curious about what this enigmatic message might portend.

As she finished up the diner's accounts for the day, Emilia pushed the letter to the back of her mind and focused on wrapping up the day's work, but she couldn't shake the feeling that something - or someone - was waiting for her in the darkness of Oakwood Park that night.
