The air hung thick with the scent of lavender shampoo and burnt coffee. Outside, the relentless August sun beat down on Bleecker Street, turning the sidewalks into shimmering pools of heat. Inside "Curl Up & Dye," however, Amelia Rose Cartwright felt a familiar calm settle over her as she swept stray hairs from the floor. 

Her salon was small, cozy, and cluttered with vintage mirrors, whimsical posters, and enough plants to rival a greenhouse. Sunlight streamed through the front window, illuminating dust motes dancing in the air and highlighting the fiery red streaks Amelia had expertly woven into Mrs. Goldstein's hair earlier that morning. 


Amelia hummed along to the jazz playing softly from her record player as she prepared for her next client. Today was shaping up to be another busy day - a testament to her skill with scissors and her uncanny ability to make anyone feel like they were the only person in the room. Little did she know, however, that a walk-in customer was about to change everything, bringing with him a tangle of secrets Amelia wouldn't soon forget.
