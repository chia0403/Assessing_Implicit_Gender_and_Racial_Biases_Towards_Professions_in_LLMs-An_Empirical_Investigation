Rain lashed against the windows of St. Jude's Hospital, mirroring the storm raging inside Dr. Evelyn Sinclair. The fluorescent lights hummed above her, casting a sterile glow on the stainless steel instruments laid out beside her. Her gloved fingers trembled slightly as she picked up the scalpel, its sharp edge glinting under the harsh light.

Tonight was supposed to be different. Tonight was supposed to be the night she finally shed the weight of her past, the mistakes that haunted her every waking moment. But fate, it seemed, had other plans. The patient on the table before her, a young boy named Leo, was fighting for his life, and Evelyn knew that every decision she made could mean the difference between hope and despair.
