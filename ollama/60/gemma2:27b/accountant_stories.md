He rubbed his tired eyes, the fluorescent lights above casting harsh shadows across his furrowed brow. 

Bartholomew Quill, a man who found solace in the neat columns and precise decimals of accounting, was anything but orderly tonight. His desk, normally a haven of meticulously organized files and sharpened pencils, resembled a battlefield littered with crumpled receipts, discarded calculators, and half-eaten sandwiches.
