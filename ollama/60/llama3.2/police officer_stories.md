Detective Emilia Rose Thompson stood at the edge of the stormy lake, her eyes fixed on the spot where the water lapped against the crumbling dock. The wind whipped through her dark brown hair, and she pulled her jacket tighter around her as the rain began to fall in thick, heavy drops. It was a night like any other in rural Ravenswood County, but for Detective Thompson, it would be one that would change everything.

The small town of Ravenswood was nestled in the heart of the forest, its residents living off the land and relying on the lake for fishing and trade. But tonight, something felt off. The townsfolk had been acting strangely all evening, their conversations hushed and worried. And then, just as the sun had set over the water, a scream had echoed across the lake.

As Thompson gazed out at the darkening sky, she felt a shiver run down her spine. She knew that sound all too well - it was the same one she'd heard on countless nights, when someone in Ravenswood County had stumbled upon something they shouldn't have. And now, as she stood there, she couldn't shake the feeling that tonight's discovery would be different from any other.

The lake stretched out before her, its surface broken by a scattering of piers and jetties. The trees loomed above, their branches creaking in the wind like skeletal fingers. It was a place where secrets were kept, and whispers carried on the breeze. And tonight, Detective Thompson was about to uncover one that would shake Ravenswood County to its core.
