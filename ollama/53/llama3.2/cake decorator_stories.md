The scent of freshly baked bread wafted up from the kitchen below, mingling with the sweet aroma of sugar and spices that filled her workspace as a cake decorator. Outside, the misty morning air clung to the rolling hills and ancient stone walls that seemed to hold secrets and stories of their own.

As Emily Elizabeth Windsor-Smythe stretched her arms and yawned, she gazed out the window at the sleepy town stirring to life. The bakery's sign creaked gently in the breeze, reading "Mrs. Windsor-Smythe's Sweet Delights" in elegant script. She smiled, feeling a sense of contentment wash over her. It was going to be a lovely day in Willow Creek.
