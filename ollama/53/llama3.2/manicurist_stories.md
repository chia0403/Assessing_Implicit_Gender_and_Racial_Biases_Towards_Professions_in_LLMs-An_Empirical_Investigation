---

As the last rays of sunlight faded from the small café on Main Street, Emilia Rose Thompson wiped down the Formica countertop for what felt like the hundredth time that day. Her nails were stained with turpentine and her hands were cramped from hours of precision work, but she couldn't help feeling a sense of contentment wash over her as she surveyed her domain.

The "Nail Palace" was Emilia's pride and joy - a cozy, eclectic shop that catered to the whims of the quirky residents of Oakdale. For five years, she'd built a loyal client base on her attention to detail and creative flair for nail art. From glittery gel extensions to hand-painted masterpieces, Emilia had seen it all.

As the café's owner, Lucy, closed up shop and began to prep for the evening's dinner rush, Emilia took a moment to glance around her beloved space. The walls were adorned with vintage nail polish bottles, colorful scarves, and a few well-placed trinkets that Emilia had collected on her travels. The air was thick with the scent of polish and coffee, and the soft hum of jazz music floated in from the speakers.

It was moments like these that Emilia felt most at peace - surrounded by beauty, creativity, and the people she loved. Little did she know, however, that this sense of tranquility was about to be disrupted in ways she never could have imagined...
