The fluorescent lights overhead cast an unforgiving glare on the sterile walls of New Haven General Hospital's hematology department. Amidst the hum of beeping machines and the gentle murmur of patients' conversations, Phoebe Alexandra Wellesley expertly navigated the maze of medical carts and wheelchairs with ease.

As a certified phlebotomist with five years of experience, Phoebe had developed an uncanny ability to find the veins even the most reluctant subjects seemed determined to hide. Her long, curly brown hair was tied back in a practical ponytail, and her bright green scrubs were stained with tiny droplets of patient blood, a badge of honor in her line of work.

It was just another Monday morning at New Haven General, and Phoebe was about to face the day's first batch of challenging patients. Little did she know, this ordinary day would soon become anything but when a cryptic message scrawled on a discarded lab slip set off a chain reaction that would change her life forever.

As Phoebe began to prepare for the morning's first draw, her eyes landed on the familiar scribble: "Project Erebus: Patient 314 - Confidential". A shiver ran down her spine. What secrets lay hidden in those numbers? And what did they have to do with her own troubled past?
