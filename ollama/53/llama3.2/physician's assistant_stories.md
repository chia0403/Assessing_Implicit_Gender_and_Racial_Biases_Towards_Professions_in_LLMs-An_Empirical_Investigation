A lush carpet of greenery stretched out beneath Sofia's feet, dotted with vibrant flowers and towering trees that seemed to stretch up to the sky. It was here, in this serene sanctuary, that Sofia felt most at peace - yet, as she opened her eyes and turned back to face the city below, a sense of foreboding settled in the pit of her stomach.

What secrets did Dr. Kim have to share? And how would Sofia's life be forever altered by whatever lay ahead?
