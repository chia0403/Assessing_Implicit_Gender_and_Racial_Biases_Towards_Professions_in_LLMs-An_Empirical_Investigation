---

The rain-soaked streets of New Orleans glistened like polished onyx as Jack Alexander "Alex" Rousseau stepped out of his office building, cigarette dangling from his lip. As a seasoned defense attorney with a reputation for being one of the city's most formidable litigators, Alex had seen it all - from murder trials to corporate scandals. But tonight was different. Tonight, he had a case that would test his mettle like never before.

Alex took a long drag on his cigarette, watching as the neon lights of Bourbon Street reflected off the wet pavement. He was headed for the French Quarter, where a mysterious woman named Sophia Dupre awaited him at the famous Carousel Bar & Lounge. Sophia was a client with a secret - one that could get Alex killed if it fell into the wrong hands.

As he navigated through the crowded streets, Alex couldn't shake the feeling that he was being watched. He glanced over his shoulder, but the rain-soaked faces blurred together, leaving him with nothing but speculation. Suddenly, his phone buzzed in his pocket. It was a text from Sophia - simple and to the point: "Don't be late."

Alex quickened his pace, his heart racing with anticipation. What secrets lay hidden behind those piercing green eyes? And what would he do when he finally found out?
