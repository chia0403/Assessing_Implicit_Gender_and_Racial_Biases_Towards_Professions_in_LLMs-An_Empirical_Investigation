Rain hammered against the grimy windowpane, mirroring the relentless pounding in Elara Finch's head. Her apartment, a cramped studio overlooking a graffiti-splattered alleyway in Seattle's Belltown district, felt like it was shrinking around her. Lines of code swam before her eyes, each character blurring into an indistinguishable mess.  She hadn't slept in 36 hours, fueled solely by lukewarm coffee and the flickering hope that she could crack this algorithm before sunrise.

Elara, a programmer known for her unorthodox solutions and relentless tenacity, was facing her biggest challenge yet: deciphering a cryptic code hidden within an ancient artifact unearthed from the ruins of a lost civilization. The artifact, a shimmering obsidian pyramid, pulsed with an otherworldly energy that both fascinated and unnerved Elara.
