Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain-soaked streets of Ashwood City gleamed like polished stone under the faint moonlight, casting long shadows across the deserted sidewalks. Inside the cramped, yet meticulously organized walls of Thompson & Co., Pharmacy, Emily Theresa Winston stood amidst the rows of neatly stacked bottles and jars, her eyes scanning the shelves with a practiced gaze.

As she worked, the soft hum of the refrigerators and the occasional beep of the cash register provided a soothing background melody to her task. Emily's mind was elsewhere, lost in thought as it often was when the pharmacy grew quiet after hours. She had always found solace in the precise routine of dispensing medication and advising patients on their treatments - it was a small sense of control in a world that seemed increasingly unpredictable.

Tonight, however, Emily's thoughts were preoccupied with an unusual request from one of her regular customers, Mrs. Jenkins, who had phoned earlier to ask for a batch of prescription pills she hadn't collected in months. The elderly woman's voice had been laced with a mixture of anxiety and urgency that had lingered long after the call ended.

As Emily worked, the phone on her desk rang out, piercing the silence like a shout from the unknown. She hesitated for a moment before answering, her heart skipping a beat as she waited to hear who was on the other end...
