Here's a potential story opening:

The fluorescent lights overhead cast an eerie glow over the deserted corridors of Oakwood High School, where Emily Mae Taylor scrubbed away at a stubborn patch of sticker residue on her lunchroom cart. It was 7:45 PM on a Tuesday evening, and the school was quiet, except for the occasional creak of the old building settling into its foundations.

Emily had been working as a janitor here for three years, ever since she'd graduated from high school herself. She took pride in keeping the halls clean and the classrooms tidy, even if no one really noticed her efforts. It was a solitary existence, but it suited Emily just fine. She liked being alone with her thoughts, and the routine of cleaning and disinfecting provided a comforting sense of structure.

As she finished up her tasks for the day, Emily gazed out the window at the darkening landscape beyond the school's parking lot. The sky was a deep shade of indigo, with stars just beginning to twinkle like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse. It was moments like these that Emily felt most alive, when the world outside seemed to be holding its breath in anticipation of something – anything – to break the stillness.

Little did she know, tonight would be different. Tonight, something was waiting for her in the shadows...
