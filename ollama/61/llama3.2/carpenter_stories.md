The misty dawn broke over the small town of Ravenshire, casting a damp veil over the narrow streets and weathered buildings that seemed to lean in like old friends. In the midst of this quiet morning, Emrys Fawcett stirred from his slumber, rubbing the fatigue from his eyes as he swung his legs over the side of the creaky bed frame. A carpenter by trade, Emrys had spent years honing his skills in the workshops and construction sites around Ravenshire, but it was here, in this sleepy coastal town, that he'd finally found a sense of belonging.

As he rose from bed, the chill of the morning air seeped into his bones, but Emrys barely noticed. He'd been up all night, lying awake as the wind howled outside and his mind wandered to the strange letter that had arrived at his workshop just hours before. The message was cryptic, its words scrawled on a worn piece of parchment in a handwriting that seemed almost...familiar.

The fire crackled in the hearth, casting a warm glow over Emrys as he began to get dressed for another long day of work. He pulled on his thick woolen apron, cinched it tight around his waist, and headed downstairs to face whatever challenges the day might bring.

But little did Emrys know, this morning was going to be different from all the others. The wind that had been howling outside had died down, leaving an eerie silence in its wake. And as he stepped out into the misty dawn, he felt a shiver run down his spine...
