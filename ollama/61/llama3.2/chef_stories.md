As one of the youngest chefs to ever earn the coveted Michelin star at Le Coeur de la Vie, Élise had built a reputation for her innovative, farm-to-table cuisine that showcased the rich flavors and traditions of her native Provence. But tonight was different - tonight, she had been tasked with recreating a legendary dish from her childhood, one that held a special significance in her family's history that went far beyond mere culinary nostalgia.
