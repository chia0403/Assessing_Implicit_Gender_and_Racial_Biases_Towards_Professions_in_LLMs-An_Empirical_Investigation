The clinic was understaffed, and the patients were starting to show signs of neglect. Emilia's hands tightened into fists as she thought about the long hours and endless paperwork that would be waiting for her at the office.

Despite her frustration, Emilia felt a sense of pride and purpose as she walked towards her car, parked outside the clinic. She had always dreamed of making a difference in her community, and now, as one of the few physicians in a small town with limited resources, she was determined to provide the best care possible, no matter what obstacles came her way.
