Here's a potential story opening and setting:

Detective Emilia Rose Wilson stepped out of the rain-soaked streets and into the warm glow of the precinct's lobby, shaking the water from her fedora as she made her way to the vending machine. It was 9:45 PM on a drizzly autumn evening in Seattle, and the streets were already emptying out for the night. The only sound was the hum of fluorescent lights overhead and the soft murmur of colleagues sharing stories over their morning coffee.

As a homicide detective with the SPD's East Precinct, Emilia had seen her fair share of late nights and gruesome crime scenes. But tonight felt different. Tonight, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off about the case they'd been working on for weeks - the body of 25-year-old college student found on the waterfront, with no signs of forced entry or struggle.

Emilia slid a bag of peanuts into her pocket and began to make her way to the break room, where she knew a cup of hot coffee and a quiet corner would help clear her mind. Little did she know, that was exactly what she needed - because as she poured herself a steaming cup, her phone would ring with an incoming call from dispatch...
