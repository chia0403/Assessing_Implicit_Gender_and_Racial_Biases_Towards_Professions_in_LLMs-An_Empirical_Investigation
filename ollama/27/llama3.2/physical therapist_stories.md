Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain-soaked streets of Seattle gleamed like polished onyx as Dr. Ava Elizabeth Thompson stepped out of her small office building, gazing up at the towering skyscrapers that seemed to touch the grey sky. The sound of raindrops pattering against the pavement created a soothing melody that normally calmed her nerves, but tonight it only made her feel restless.

As she walked down the sidewalk, Ava's eyes scanned the crowded streets, taking in the familiar sights and sounds of the city she loved. She had spent countless hours as a physical therapist at the nearby Swedish Hospital, helping patients regain their strength and mobility after injury or illness. But tonight, she felt a sense of discontent that lingered long after her shift ended.

Ava quickened her pace, the cold rain seeping into her bones as she made her way to the coffee shop on the corner. She ordered a hot cup of coffee and settled onto a stool at the counter, letting the warmth seep into her chilled hands as she gazed out at the rain-drenched world.

As she sipped her coffee, Ava's mind began to wander back to the patient who had been occupying her thoughts all day - a young woman named Sarah, who had been involved in a severe car accident and was struggling to regain control of her left leg. Ava's gut told her that there was more to this case than met the eye, but she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was...
