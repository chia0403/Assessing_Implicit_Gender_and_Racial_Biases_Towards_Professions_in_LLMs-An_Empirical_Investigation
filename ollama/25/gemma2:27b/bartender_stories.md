The man, a hulking figure shrouded in shadows, nursed a glass of whiskey like it held the cure to all his troubles. Bart had seen his type before - weary souls seeking solace at the bottom of a bottle. He wasn't judgemental; he knew everyone had their demons. But this one, with his haunted eyes and clenched fists, seemed different.
