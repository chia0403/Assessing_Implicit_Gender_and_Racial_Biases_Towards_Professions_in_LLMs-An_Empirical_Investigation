The fluorescent lights overhead cast an unforgiving glow over the checkout lines at Sam's Supermarket, illuminating the tired faces of the evening crowd. Behind the register, cashier Emilia Elena Vasquez scanned groceries with a practiced ease that belied her own exhaustion. It was 7:45 PM on a Wednesday, and the dinner rush had just ended.

Emilia took a deep breath as she processed the next customer's purchases - a quick grab for milk, bread, and eggs before heading home to cook dinner for herself after another long day at work. As she handed over the change, Emilia's gaze drifted out the window, where the late summer sky was a deep shade of indigo, with stars beginning to twinkle like diamonds in the darkness.

It had been a good week so far - her coworker had called in sick and Emilia had gotten to work early to cover the shift. But as she tallied up the till and began to restock the shelves, Emilia couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. It started with a weird phone call earlier that day from an unknown number...
