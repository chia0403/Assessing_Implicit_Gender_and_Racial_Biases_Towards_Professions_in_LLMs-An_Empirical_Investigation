Here's a potential story opening and setting:

---

The snowflakes danced in the flickering light of the old-fashioned street lamps, casting a mystical glow over the narrow streets of Ravenshire. It was a winter evening like any other in this quaint English village, where time seemed to stand still and the air was sweet with the scent of woodsmoke and freshly cut meat.

In his small shop on High Street, Emrys Ffynnon ap Rhys was hard at work, expertly trimming joints and arranging the day's special cuts for the evening's customers. As a butcher, Emrys took pride in his craft, selecting only the finest meats from the local farms and raising them to perfection. His shop, 'Ffynnon's Fine Cuts', had been a staple of Ravenshire for generations, and Emrys was next in line to carry on the family tradition.

At thirty-five years old, Emrys had already made a name for himself in the village as a master butcher, but he knew that his true test lay ahead. A mysterious letter had arrived at the shop earlier that day, bearing an invitation from the enigmatic Lord Harrington, who claimed to have a special task requiring Emrys' unique skills. The letter was cryptic, but one thing was certain: Emrys' life was about to take a dramatic turn.

As he wrapped a perfectly glazed pork loin in paper and tied it with a string, Emrys couldn't shake the feeling that his humble life as a butcher was about to become something much more...
