As the last rays of sunlight faded from the small town of Willow Creek, Emma Elizabeth Thompson made her way through the quiet streets, her feet crunching on the fallen leaves that carpeted the sidewalk. The air was crisp and cool, carrying the scent of wood smoke and damp earth, and Emma breathed it in deeply as she walked towards the old oak tree at the edge of town.

She had lived in Willow Creek all her life, and as an elementary school teacher, she knew every inch of this tiny community. She worked at Oakdale Elementary, a small, rambling school on the outskirts of town that was home to over 300 students from kindergarten to fifth grade. Emma loved her job, teaching subjects like science, social studies, and language arts to kids who were just beginning to explore the world beyond their classrooms.

As she approached the oak tree, Emma noticed something strange: a piece of paper stuck in the trunk, fluttering gently in the breeze. She picked it up, smoothing out the creases, and saw that it was a note, scribbled in hasty handwriting:

"Meet me at Oakdale Elementary tonight at midnight. Come alone."

Emma's heart skipped a beat as she read the words. Who could have written this? And what did they want from her? She felt a shiver run down her spine as she looked around, wondering if anyone was watching her...
