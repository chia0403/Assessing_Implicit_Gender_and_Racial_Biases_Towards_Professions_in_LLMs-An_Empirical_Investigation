The rain-soaked streets of downtown Portland glistened like polished onyx as Amelia Rose Thompson stepped out of her small apartment and onto the narrow balcony. The cool misty air enveloped her like a damp shroud, carrying the faint scent of wet earth and decaying leaves from the nearby Willamette River. Amelia took a deep breath, feeling the tension in her shoulders begin to ease as she gazed out at the city below.

As a paralegal at the law firm of Johnson & Associates, Amelia was no stranger to stress. She spent most of her days pouring over contracts and court documents for one of Portland's most prominent family law firms. But tonight, after a particularly grueling day, she had decided to take a break from it all and clear her head.

With a sigh, Amelia wrapped her arms around herself and leaned against the railing, watching as the raindrops slid down the glass like tiny tears. She felt a sense of disconnection from the world around her - as if she was just going through the motions, without any real purpose or direction. Little did she know, that feeling would soon be interrupted by an unexpected phone call from an old friend...
