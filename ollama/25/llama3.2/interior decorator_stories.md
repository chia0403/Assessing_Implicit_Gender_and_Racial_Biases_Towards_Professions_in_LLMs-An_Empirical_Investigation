The rain pattered against the large windows of Emma Rose Thompson's design studio, casting a rhythmic melody that seemed to match the beat of her own restless heart. As an interior decorator with a passion for restoring old spaces to their former glory, Emma had always felt most alive when surrounded by the quiet intimacy of her clients' homes.

Tonight was no exception. Emma stood at the front of her studio, surveying the sprawling layout she had carefully crafted over the past decade. The walls were lined with rows of sleek, minimalist shelves, packed tightly with an assortment of decorative treasures and design inspiration books that had been collected from her travels around the world. In the center of the room, a stunning chandelier hung from the ceiling, casting a warm glow over the space.

As she gazed out at the sea of creativity before her, Emma's mind began to wander to the latest project on her plate: the restoration of Willowbrook Manor, a sprawling estate nestled deep in the heart of the countryside. It was a job that had been months in the making, and one that held a certain allure for Emma - not just because of its grand scale, but also because of the secrets it seemed to hold.

A shiver ran down her spine as she turned to make her way to the desk, where a stack of documents and sketches awaited. She couldn't shake the feeling that there was something about Willowbrook Manor that called to her, something more than just the thrill of a challenging project.

Little did Emma know, this job would be the one that changed everything...
