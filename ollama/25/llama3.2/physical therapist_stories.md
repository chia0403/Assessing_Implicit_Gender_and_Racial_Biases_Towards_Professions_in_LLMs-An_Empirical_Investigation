The rain pattered against the windows of Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson's office, casting a soothing melody over the bustling streets of downtown Portland. Lexi gazed out at the grey sky, her mind still reeling from the news she'd received just that morning - her mentor and close friend, Dr. Rachel Kim, had been in a serious car accident on her way home from work.

As a physical therapist with a passion for helping patients overcome even the most daunting injuries, Lexi had always admired Rachel's tenacity and dedication to her craft. And now, as she sat in Rachel's office, going over the plans for their upcoming sports medicine conference, Lexi couldn't help but feel a sense of unease wash over her.

The conference was just a week away, and Lexi had spent countless hours preparing her presentation on advanced injury rehabilitation techniques. But with Rachel out of commission, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off - that this accident might be more than just a coincidence.

Just then, the phone rang, shrill in the silence. Lexi picked up, answering with a cheerful "Hello, Thompson Physical Therapy." But her smile faltered as she heard the voice on the other end of the line...
