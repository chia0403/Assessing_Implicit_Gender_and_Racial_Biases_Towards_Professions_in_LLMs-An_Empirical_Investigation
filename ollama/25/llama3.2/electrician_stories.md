Here's a story opening and setting:

The neon lights of "ElectriFy" flickered above the bustling streets of downtown Seattle, casting a vibrant glow over the rainy night. Inside the shop, 32-year-old Julian Andrew Thompson hunched over his workbench, sweat dripping down his forehead as he troubleshooted a faulty circuit breaker for a local restaurant.

The hum of the fluorescent lights overhead cast an eerie blue hue on Julian's rugged features, accentuating the sharp lines of his jaw and the wisps of dark brown hair that framed his pale skin. His hands, stained with grease and sweat, moved deftly as he worked, a testament to his years of experience as an electrician.

The sound of rain pattering against the shop's windowpane created a soothing melody, but Julian's mind was elsewhere. He had received a cryptic message earlier that day from an old acquaintance, hinting at a mysterious job opportunity that required his unique skillset. The pay was too good to pass up, and Julian's curiosity was piqued.

As he finished up the restaurant's circuit breaker, Julian's phone buzzed with another text from the unknown sender: "Meet me at the waterfront at midnight. Come alone." A shiver ran down Julian's spine as he wondered what this mysterious encounter might hold...
