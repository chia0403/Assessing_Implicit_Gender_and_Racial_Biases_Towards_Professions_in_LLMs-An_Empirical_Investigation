The small town of Willow Creek, nestled in the heart of rural America, was bathed in an eerie autumnal glow as night began to fall. The streets were empty, save for the occasional passing car or the soft hum of a lawnmower cutting through the darkness. In a cozy cottage on Elm Street, registered nurse Emilia Rose Thompson sat at her kitchen table, sipping a lukewarm cup of coffee and staring out the window as the sun dipped below the horizon.

Emilia, with her long, curly brown hair and bright green eyes, was a fixture in Willow Creek's tight-knit community. She had grown up here, raised by her loving parents who still owned the local diner where she had spent countless hours serving coffee to the townspeople. After completing nursing school, Emilia had returned home to care for her ailing mother and help with the family business. Now, as she waited for the sun to rise on another ordinary day in Willow Creek, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.

Just then, her phone rang, shrill and insistent. Emilia's heart skipped a beat as she hesitated before answering, wondering who could be calling at such an early hour...
