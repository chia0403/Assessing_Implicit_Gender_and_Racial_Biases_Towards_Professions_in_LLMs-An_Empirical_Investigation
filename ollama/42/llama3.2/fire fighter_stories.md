Evie took a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for whatever the night might bring. She checked her gear one last time, made sure her phone was fully charged, and headed out to meet up with her crew at the fire station's dispatch center. As she stepped into the cool night air, she felt a shiver run down her spine - a sense of unease that had been building inside her since she arrived on duty.

Little did Evie know, tonight would be one of those nights that tested everything she thought she knew about courage, loyalty, and the very fabric of her community.
