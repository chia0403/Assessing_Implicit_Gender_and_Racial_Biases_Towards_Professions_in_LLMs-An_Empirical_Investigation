The rain-soaked streets of Ashwood City were always at their most unforgiving on Wednesdays, but for Emily Anne Windsor, it was just another day in the books. A small, cluttered office with a worn wooden desk and a faded sign that creaked in the gentle breeze outside read "Windsor & Co." - her modest bookkeeping practice.

As she poured herself a lukewarm cup of coffee from the thermos on her desk, Emily Anne's gaze wandered to the stacks of dusty ledgers and dog-eared financial reports that seemed to stretch up to the ceiling. The quiet hum of the office computer and the occasional rustle of papers as she worked were familiar comforts, but today felt different - almost...electric.

It had been a slow week so far, with only a handful of clients to keep her busy. But Emily Anne's attention was drawn to a small, unmarked envelope on her desk that had arrived in yesterday's mail. The plain white paper and the single, elegant letter "W" stamped on it seemed out of place among the stacks of bills and invoices.

She picked up the envelope, feeling an inexplicable thrill run through her fingers as she turned it over. Who could be sending her something mysterious like this? And what did they want from her?

With a sense of trepidation, Emily Anne Windsor reached for her pen and carefully opened the envelope...
