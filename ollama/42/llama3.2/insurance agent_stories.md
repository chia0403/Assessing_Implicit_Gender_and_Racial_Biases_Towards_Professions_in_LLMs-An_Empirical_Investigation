The rain poured down on the small town of Oakdale like a relentless curtain, casting a grey mist over the streets and homes. It was the kind of day that made you want to stay inside with a warm cup of coffee and forget about the world outside. But for Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, insurance agent at Thompson & Associates Insurance Agency, it was just another day in the trenches.

Lexi stood at her desk, staring out the window at the droplets sliding down the panes like tears on a sad face. Her eyes were red-rimmed from lack of sleep and too much stress. It had been a long week, with client meetings and paperwork piling up faster than she could keep up.

As she rubbed the tiredness from her eyes, Lexi's gaze fell on the old-fashioned clock on the wall. 8:47 AM. She was supposed to have a meeting with a new client at 9 AM sharp. The prospect of another tedious sales pitch was already weighing her down like a boulder in her stomach.

Little did she know, this meeting would be the least of her worries...
