The sun was setting over the Manhattan skyline, casting a golden glow over the steel and glass towers that seemed to stretch on forever. It was a chilly autumn evening in October 2023, and the streets were still buzzing with the remnants of a long day of trading.

Inside the high-rise office building where he worked, stock broker Reginald "Reg" Thompson sat at his desk, staring out the window as he sipped on a lukewarm cup of coffee. His eyes scanned the chaotic streets below, taking in the sea of suits and briefcases hurrying to and from their destinations.

Reg was a top performer at his firm, known for his razor-sharp instincts and uncanny ability to sniff out hot stocks before they went public. But despite his outward success, Reg couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A nagging sense of unease had been building in him all week, ever since he'd received a mysterious phone call from an unknown number on Wednesday evening.

The voice on the other end had been low and gravelly, speaking only three words: "Meet me tonight." Reg had tried to brush it off as a prank, but something about those simple words had stuck with him. Now, as he gazed out at the glittering lights of the city, he couldn't help but wonder what lay in store for him come nightfall.

Reg took another sip of his coffee, his mind racing with possibilities. He was about to head home when his phone rang, breaking the spell. It was his boss, calling to brief him on a major announcement that would shake the very foundations of Reg's world...
