The misty dawn crept over the sleepy coastal town of Ravenswood-by-Sea, casting a mystical veil over the quaint streets and homes. Amidst the tranquility, Dr. Sophia Isabella Thompson stirred in her small cottage overlooking the ocean, the sound of seagulls and waves crashing against the shore lulling her into a state of relaxation.

As a registered dietician specializing in functional medicine, Sophia had spent years studying the intricacies of human nutrition and its impact on overall well-being. Her gentle approach to health, coupled with her empathetic nature, had earned her a loyal following among Ravenswood's residents. With her curly brown hair tied back in a loose bun and a warm smile etched on her face, Sophia embarked on another day of helping people transform their lives through the power of food.

Little did she know, this particular morning would bring more than just a gentle ocean breeze to shake up her routine...
