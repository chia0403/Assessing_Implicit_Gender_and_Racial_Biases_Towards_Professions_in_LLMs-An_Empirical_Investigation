Here's a potential story opening:

The rain-soaked streets of Seattle glistened like polished onyx as Registered Nurse, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, trudged through the downpour towards the hospital entrance. Her bright yellow raincoat and matching hat seemed out of place among the drab gray buildings that lined the sidewalk, but Lexi didn't let that faze her. She had always been a beacon of sunshine in the midst of chaos.

As she pushed open the heavy glass doors, a warm blast of air enveloped her, carrying with it the scent of disinfectant and the faint tang of saltwater from Puget Sound. Lexi smiled wistfully, feeling the stress of the long commute melt away. She loved this job – not just because she got to help people, but also because every day was different.

Tonight, however, felt like a particularly tough night. A string of violent altercations had left the ER team on high alert, and Lexi's shift was about to get underway at 8 PM. As she made her way through the crowded waiting room, exchanging warm smiles with familiar faces, she couldn't shake the feeling that tonight would be different.

Little did Lexi know, her evening was just around the corner – one that would test every ounce of skill and compassion she possessed as a nurse...
