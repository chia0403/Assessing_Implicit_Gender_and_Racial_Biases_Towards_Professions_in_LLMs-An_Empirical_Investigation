He took pride in his land, which had been passed down through generations of his family, and he worked hard to ensure that it remained productive and profitable.

The air was thick with the scent of creosote and mesquite, and the sound of coyotes echoed through the valley as Alex breathed deeply, feeling a sense of contentment wash over him. It was moments like these, when the sun dipped below the horizon and the stars began to twinkle in the sky, that he felt most at peace with himself and his place in the world.
