The sun had just set over the bustling streets of Tokyo, casting a warm orange glow over the neon-lit skyscrapers of the Shinjuku district. In a small, cramped office nestled between a ramen shop and a used bookstore, Kazuo Nakamura sat hunched over his desk, staring blankly at the rows of sales data spread out before him.

As one of the top salespeople for the company's Tokyo branch, Kazuo had spent years honing his skills in the cutthroat world of high-stakes electronics sales. He was known for his razor-sharp instincts and ability to read people like a book - but despite his success, he couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.

Kazuo's eyes wandered to the worn leather briefcase by his side, its contents a carefully curated selection of company-approved gadgets and gizmos designed to impress even the most discerning clients. But as he gazed at the sleek, silver smartphone nestled in the case, he couldn't help but feel a pang of dissatisfaction.

This was it - the moment of truth. His latest client, the enigmatic billionaire entrepreneur Ryota Matsumoto, was due to arrive within the hour, and Kazuo knew that one misstep could spell disaster for his already-tenuous grip on the company's top sales position.

With a deep breath, Kazuo straightened his suit jacket, mentally rehearsed his opening lines, and steeled himself for what promised to be the most high-pressure sales meeting of his career. Little did he know, this would be just the beginning of a journey that would take him from the cutthroat world of corporate sales to a dark, twisted game of cat-and-mouse with a sinister force beyond his wildest imagination.
