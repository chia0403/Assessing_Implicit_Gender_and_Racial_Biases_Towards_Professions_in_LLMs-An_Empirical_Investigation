Dr. Emilia Sophia Thompson stepped out of her office and into the crisp autumn air, gazing up at the towering trees that lined the campus quad. As a professor of English literature at Oakwood University, she had spent countless hours lecturing on the classics, but tonight she was here to attend a more...unconventional gathering.

A small, mysterious invitation had arrived in her mailbox earlier that day, bearing no return address and only two words: "Meet me." The message had piqued her curiosity, and Emilia's detective instincts were now itching to be satisfied. She tucked the crumpled note into her pocket and made her way across the quad, her eyes scanning the shadows for any sign of movement.

The moon was full overhead, casting an eerie silver glow over the deserted campus. The only sound was the rustling of leaves beneath Emilia's feet as she approached the ancient oak tree that stood sentinel at the center of the quad. It was here, under the branches of this very tree, that her mysterious summons promised to reveal a secret that would shake the foundations of Oakwood University forever.

As she reached the base of the tree, a figure emerged from the darkness. Emilia's heart quickened as she recognized the stranger, their features illuminated by the soft moonlight. It was Dr. Henry Lee, a former colleague who had disappeared under mysterious circumstances five years ago...
