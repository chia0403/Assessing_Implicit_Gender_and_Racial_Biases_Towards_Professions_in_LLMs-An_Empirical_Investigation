Here's a potential story opening and setting for a tale featuring a physical therapist:

The sun had just begun to set over the small coastal town of Ravenswood, casting a warm orange glow over the worn wooden pier that stretched out into the sea. The sound of seagulls filled the air as Emily Anne Thompson, a physical therapist with a passion for helping others heal and recover from injury, made her way along the pier, taking in the salty scent of the ocean and the cries of the gulls.

Emily had just finished a long day at the Ravenswood Rehabilitation Center, where she worked alongside an interdisciplinary team to help patients regain mobility and strength after surgeries, accidents, or illnesses. As she walked, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off - perhaps it was the way the shadows cast by the setting sun seemed to stretch and twist on the water, like dark fingers reaching out from the depths.

As a physical therapist with a specialized interest in aquatic therapy, Emily had spent countless hours on this very pier, watching patients float and move through the water, their bodies healing at an incredible rate. But tonight, something felt different. The air seemed charged with an anticipation that made her skin prickle with unease.

And then, just as she turned a corner, Emily caught sight of it - a figure standing at the far end of the pier, its back to her. The figure was shrouded in shadows, but there was something unmistakably familiar about the way it moved, like a ghostly echo of someone who had once been close to Emily herself.

Without thinking, Emily picked up her pace, her heart pounding in her chest as she quickened her stride towards the mysterious stranger...
