---

The fog rolled in off the Pacific Ocean, shrouding the small coastal town of Tidal Cove in a damp, grey mist. It was the kind of day that made you want to stay inside with a warm cup of coffee and a good book, but for Emily Joy Wilson, it was just another typical Wednesday morning at her travel agency.

Emily's eyes scanned the rows of brochures and posters on her walls, each one advertising a different destination - from sun-kissed beaches in Hawaii to bustling city streets in Tokyo. She loved being a travel agent, taking people on adventures they'd never forget. But today, she wasn't feeling inspired.

As she sipped her coffee and checked the morning's bookings, Emily couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A recent string of cancellations had left her agency feeling light on business, and she was starting to worry about the future. She needed a break from the stress and uncertainty of it all - and fast.

Just then, the doorbell above the entrance to her agency jangled, signaling the arrival of the day's first customer. Emily smiled and made her way to answer the door, ready to face whatever adventure came her way next...
