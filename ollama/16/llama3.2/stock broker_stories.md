The sun had long since set over the bustling streets of Manhattan, casting a golden glow over the towering skyscrapers of Wall Street. Inside the gleaming glass and steel walls of Harrison & Co., a prestigious investment firm on 22nd Street, stock broker Ethan Alexander Wynter sipped his scotch on the rocks as he stared out at the city's twinkling lights.

Ethan, a man in his late thirties with piercing blue eyes and chiseled features, had spent years climbing the ranks of Harrison & Co. He was a rising star in the world of high finance, known for his razor-sharp instincts and uncanny ability to sniff out hot stocks before they became the next big thing. But as he gazed out at the glittering lights of New York City, Ethan couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The market had been on a wild ride lately, with sudden dips and dives sending shockwaves through the financial world.

As he turned back to his desk, Ethan's phone buzzed with an incoming call from his boss, Marcus Harrison. "Ethan, I need to see you in my office ASAP," the voice on the other end said, its tone dripping with urgency. Ethan felt a flutter of unease as he set his glass down and began to make his way towards the elevator.

Little did he know, this call would set off a chain of events that would change his life forever...
