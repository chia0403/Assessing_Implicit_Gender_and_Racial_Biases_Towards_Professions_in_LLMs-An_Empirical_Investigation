The fog rolled in off the canal, shrouding the narrow streets of Ravenswood in a damp, grey mist. It was a familiar sight for Emilia Rose Thompson, owner and operator of Thompson's Plumbing Co., but it never lost its ability to chill her bones. As she trudged through the fog, her worn wellies squelching in the damp ground, she couldn't help but feel a sense of unease. It had been three years since her father's passing, and the business they'd built together was still struggling to find its footing.

Emilia's thoughts were interrupted by the phone call from her right-hand man, Tony. "Hey, Em, we got a weird one here," he said, his voice low and serious. "Customer claims their toilet's possessed."

Emilia raised an eyebrow as she climbed the steps of the old Victorian house. This was going to be an interesting day.

The house loomed above her, its once-grand facade now cracked and faded, like the dreams that had been lost within its walls. Emilia took a deep breath and pushed open the creaking front door, ready to face whatever lay ahead. Little did she know, this job would be more than just another fix for a faulty pipe...
