Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun had long since set on the crumbling city of Ashwood, casting a dark and foreboding shadow over the narrow streets and alleyways. The once-thriving metropolis was now a mere ghost town, its inhabitants either fled or fallen victim to the ravages of time.

In the midst of this desolate landscape stood the imposing walls of Ravenshire Keep, the seat of power for the city's last remaining guard: Emilia Greywood-Blackthorn. As a member of the esteemed Blackthorn family, Emilia had always felt a sense of duty to protect the keep and its surroundings from any threats that may arise.

With her piercing emerald eyes and raven-black hair tied back in a tight braid, Emilia stood watch at the entrance of Ravenshire Keep, her hand resting on the hilt of her sword. The wind howled through the deserted streets like a chorus of restless spirits, causing the old wooden signs to creak and groan in protest.

It was on this particularly eerie evening that Emilia sensed something amiss. A faint scent of smoke wafted on the breeze, carrying with it whispers of an unknown presence lurking just beyond the keep's walls. As she scanned the horizon, her instincts told her that tonight would be a night like no other in Ravenshire Keep...
