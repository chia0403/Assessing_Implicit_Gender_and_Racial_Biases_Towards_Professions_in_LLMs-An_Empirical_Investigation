Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stepped out of her small apartment and into the crisp morning air, gazing out at the bustling streets of Portland, Oregon. As a registered dietitian with a passion for nutrition education, Lexi had spent countless hours exploring the city's food scene, from artisanal bakeries to street vendors serving up authentic Korean BBQ.

Lexi worked at a community health clinic in the city's oldest neighborhood, where she spent most days counseling patients on everything from mindful eating habits to meal planning strategies. Her specialty was working with clients who struggled with disordered eating, and her approach focused on promoting self-care and body positivity.

As she sipped her coffee on the balcony of her apartment building, Lexi gazed out at the willow trees swaying in the breeze, their delicate branches etched against a sky that seemed to be slowly awakening from its winter slumber. It was April 10th, and spring was finally starting to make its presence known in the city.

But despite the promise of new life and renewal, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. She had been receiving strange messages on her phone for weeks, anonymous notes and cryptic warnings that seemed to be hinting at a deeper conspiracy lurking beneath the surface of Portland's seemingly idyllic food scene...
