The sun was setting over the small town of Ravenshire, casting a warm orange glow over the cobblestone streets and half-timbered buildings. In the heart of the town square, Emrys ap Rhys stood at his workbench, his hands moving deftly as he shaped and polished the rough granite that would soon become the centerpiece of the local lord's new chapel.

Emrys, a skilled stone cutter with a reputation for precision and patience, had spent the better part of his life honing his craft. His fingers were stained with the cool grey dust of the quarry, and his eyes, like the worn leather of an old glove, seemed to hold a thousand stories of the stones he'd worked with over the years.

As the last rays of sunlight faded beyond the town's wooden palisade, Emrys stepped back from his workbench, his eyes scanning the square with a practiced gaze. The evening air was alive with the sounds of laughter and music drifting from the local tavern, where travelers and locals alike gathered to share tales of their adventures. But Emrys' attention was focused on the task at hand: crafting a masterpiece that would be remembered for generations to come.

Little did he know, however, that his latest commission would set him on a path that would change the course of his life forever...
