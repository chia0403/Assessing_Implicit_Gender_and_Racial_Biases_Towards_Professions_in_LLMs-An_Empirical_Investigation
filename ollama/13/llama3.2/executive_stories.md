She had spent countless hours poring over financials and crunching numbers, but a nagging sense of unease lingered at the back of her mind. Something didn't add up.

The lights of the Chrysler Building cast an otherworldly glow on the darkening streets as Lexi turned to head back into the building, her eyes fixed on the elevator button that would take her to the 23rd floor and the waiting chaos of a high-stakes meeting with her team. Little did she know, this was just the beginning of a night that would test everything she thought she knew about power, loyalty, and the true cost of success.
