**Setting:** The small coastal town of Ravenswood-by-Sea, nestled between rugged cliffs and the vast expanse of the North Atlantic Ocean. The town's economy has long relied on fishing and tourism, but in recent years, it's struggled to find its footing as the industry has declined. As a result, many residents have turned to alternative sources of income, including small businesses and odd jobs.

**Main Character:** Axel "Ax" Thompson, 35-year-old electrician with a passion for restoring old buildings and fixing anything with a wire. Ax grew up in Ravenswood-by-Sea, the youngest of three siblings in a family of craftsmen. He inherited his love of electronics from his father, who was an electrician before him, but has always had a knack for getting his hands dirty and figuring out how things work.

Ax has spent years working on odd jobs around town, including fixing the old lighthouse at the edge of the cliff, which now serves as a popular spot for tourists. Despite the town's struggles, Ax is proud to call Ravenswood-by-Sea his home and is determined to make a name for himself in the community.

As the stormy night wore on, casting an eerie glow over the small town, Ax received a mysterious phone call from the local councilor, asking him to meet at the old clock tower on the outskirts of town.
