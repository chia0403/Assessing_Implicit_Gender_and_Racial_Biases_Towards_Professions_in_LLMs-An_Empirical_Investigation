The incessant drone of cicadas filled the humid air, a thick, green blanket clinging to everything in sight.  Sweat trickled down Detective Alistair "Ali" Blackwood's neck, dampening the collar of his starched white shirt.  He stood at the edge of the overgrown sugarcane field, its rustling stalks whispering secrets he couldn't decipher. 

The stench of decay was unmistakable - a sickly sweet odor that mingled with the earthy scent of freshly turned soil. It led him further into the field, towards a gnarled banyan tree draped in Spanish moss. Beneath its sprawling branches lay the body, sprawled awkwardly amidst a tangle of roots.
