"Welcome to Snips & Style," Mia said with a warm smile, her hands pausing mid-snippet as she extended a hand. "What can I get for you?"

The stranger's gaze lingered on hers before he shook her hand, his voice low and rough as he replied, "Just a trim, please."

Mia raised an eyebrow, sensing there was more to this stranger than met the eye. Little did she know, their chance encounter would set off a chain of events that would change the course of her life forever.
