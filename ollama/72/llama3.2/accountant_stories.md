It was a chilly autumn morning in late October when Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson trudged into her small office in downtown Portland, Oregon. The rain-soaked streets reflected the grey sky above, casting a somber mood over the entire city. Lexi shivered as she pushed open the door to her accounting firm, a tiny space above a vintage bookstore on Hawthorne Boulevard.

She was a numbers person, through and through - an accountant with a keen eye for detail and a love of order and precision. Her clients were mostly small businesses and individuals, all of whom relied on Lexi's expertise to navigate the complex world of taxes and finance.

As she hung her coat on the rack by the door, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. She had been noticing strange occurrences around town - a string of break-ins at local shops, rumors of a new gang operating in the area, and an unexplained sense of unease that seemed to permeate every corner of Portland.

Lexi's own life wasn't much more exciting than her work, but she was determined to keep things that way. She had given up on love and relationships years ago, content to focus on building a successful career and enjoying the quiet comfort of her small apartment in the Pearl District.

But as she began to sort through the day's mail and respond to messages from clients, Lexi couldn't help but wonder if her careful world was about to be turned upside down.
