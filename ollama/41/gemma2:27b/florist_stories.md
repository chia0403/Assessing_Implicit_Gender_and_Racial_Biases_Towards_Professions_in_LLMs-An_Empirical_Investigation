The scent of lilies hung heavy in the air, mingling with the sweet perfume of roses and the earthy aroma of lavender. Emilia Bellweather delicately arranged a bouquet of sunflowers, their golden heads tilting towards the morning sun filtering through the shop window. Outside, cobblestones glistened with dew, reflecting the vibrant colours of the flower stalls lining the charming street in Bath. Emilia hummed softly to herself, her nimble fingers weaving magic between the petals, transforming ordinary stems into expressions of joy, sorrow, and everything in between. 


Today, however, a knot of anxiety tightened in her stomach.  A grand order awaited her: the floral arrangements for Lady Cavendish's notorious Midsummer Night masquerade ball. This wasn't just any event; whispers of intrigue and scandal trailed Lady Cavendish like perfume. Emilia knew she had to create something spectacular, something that would not only capture the magic of the night but also withstand the scrutiny of Bath's high society.
