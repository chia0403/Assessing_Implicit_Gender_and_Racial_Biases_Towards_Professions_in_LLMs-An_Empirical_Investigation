The moon cast its silvery glow over the crumbling stone walls of Ravenshire Prison, the wind whispers secrets through the broken windows like a mournful sigh. Within the fortress-like structure, the shadows danced with an air of malevolence, as if the very darkness itself was alive and watching.

Kaelin "Kae" Blackwood, a grizzled and battle-hardened guard, stood at attention before the imposing stone door of his watchtower. His rugged features were etched with the lines of countless nights spent on duty, his piercing blue eyes scanning the horizon for any sign of trouble. The wind ruffled his dark hair, and his thick beard was flecked with threads of silver, a testament to the years he'd served Ravenshire's brutal regime.

As Kae gazed out into the night, his thoughts were drawn to the rumors that had been circulating among the prison's inhabitants - whispers of a plot to break free from their oppressive chains. He had always prided himself on being loyal to the Ravenwood family, who ruled Ravenshire with an iron fist, but the seed of doubt had begun to take root in his mind.

Little did Kae know, the night was about to bring him face-to-face with a reality that would shake the very foundations of his existence...
