It was a chilly autumn evening in the small town of Oakwood, nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains. The leaves had long since fallen from the trees, leaving behind a carpet of golden brown and crimson hues that seemed to glow in the fading light. In a small office building on Main Street, Systems Analyst Emily JoAnne Wilson sat hunched over her computer screen, sipping lukewarm coffee as she pored over lines of code.

As she worked, the sounds of the town filtered in through the open window: the distant rumble of the creek, the occasional bark from a passing dog, and the faint hum of the evening news on the radio. It was a peaceful atmosphere, one that Emily had grown to love over the years. She had spent countless hours in this very office, tapping away at her keyboard as she worked to fix the technical problems that plagued Oakwood's residents.

Despite her dedication to her work, Emily couldn't shake the feeling that something was off tonight. The town's mayor, a man named Jedediah Flynn, had called an emergency meeting for 8pm, and there was no mention of what it was about on the agenda. As she worked, Emily found herself glancing at the clock, her mind racing with possibilities.

Little did she know, this was just the beginning of a night that would change everything.
