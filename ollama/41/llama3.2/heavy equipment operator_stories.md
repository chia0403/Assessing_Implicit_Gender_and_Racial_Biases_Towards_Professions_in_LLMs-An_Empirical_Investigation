Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun had just begun to set over the vast expanse of the Montana countryside, casting a warm orange glow over the dusty fields and rugged hills. The air was cool and crisp, carrying the scent of sagebrush and fresh earth.

In the small town of Willow Creek, nestled in the heart of the Beaverhead-Deerlodge National Forest, 32-year-old Ryder Jackson "Jax" Thompson revved up his engine, preparing for another long day of work as a heavy equipment operator on the local ranch. Jax had spent countless hours behind the wheel of his CAT 350B excavator, hauling bales of hay and fencing posts to remote corners of the property.

As he finished fueling up at the local convenience store, Jax rubbed the fatigue from his eyes, his rugged features creased by a day's worth of work. He had been working for rancher Jack McCade for three years now, learning the ins and outs of the land and the equipment that kept it running smoothly.

Little did Jax know, today would be different. Today, he'd receive an unexpected phone call from his estranged father, a message that would set him on a course to confront the dark secrets of his past and the true reason he'd left Willow Creek in the first place...
