Rain lashed against Detective Elias Thorne's trench coat, each drop exploding in a miniature burst of spray on the grimy asphalt. The neon glow from the "Lucky Clover" diner across the street flickered through the deluge, casting an unsettling green sheen over the alleyway where he stood. A guttural cough erupted from the shadowed figure slumped against the overflowing dumpster. 

Thorne, his face weathered and grim under the brim of his hat, cautiously approached. The man, a young kid barely out of his teens by the looks of it, was dressed in a threadbare hoodie, his face pale and drawn. Blood seeped from a jagged gash on his forehead, staining the concrete crimson.
