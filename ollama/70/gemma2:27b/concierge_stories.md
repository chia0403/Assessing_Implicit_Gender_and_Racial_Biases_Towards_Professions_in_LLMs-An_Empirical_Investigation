The air in the opulent lobby hummed with suppressed excitement. Guests flitted about like exotic birds, their tailored suits and jewel-toned dresses shimmering under the crystal chandeliers.  Eleanor Beaumont, Chief Concierge of The Grand Meridian Hotel, surveyed the scene with a practiced eye. She knew each guest by name, their preferences, their eccentricities. It was her job to anticipate their needs before they even realized they had them.

Tonight was different. Tonight, a new guest was arriving - a renowned archaeologist rumored to have unearthed an artifact of immense historical significance. Eleanor straightened her immaculately pressed uniform and adjusted the pearl brooch at her collar, bracing herself for the arrival that promised to be anything but ordinary.
