The sun had just begun to set over the small coastal town of Tidal Cove, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and shops that lined its Main Street. In a quiet office above the local surf shop, Dr. Sofia Elena Vasquez was finishing up her shift as a dental hygienist at the town's sole family practice. She wiped down the last of the equipment and stacked her supplies into the cleaning cart, taking a moment to glance out the window at the sound of seagulls wheeling overhead.

Sofia had always loved Tidal Cove - its laid-back vibe, its friendly residents, and its stunning natural beauty made it the perfect place to call home. As she locked up the office and headed downstairs to grab her jacket, Sofia couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over her. It was days like this, with the sun setting over the ocean and the smell of saltwater filling the air, that reminded her why she loved being a dental hygienist in the first place: to help people smile, and see their faces light up when they left the dentist's chair.

Little did Sofia know, however, that today was going to be anything but ordinary...
