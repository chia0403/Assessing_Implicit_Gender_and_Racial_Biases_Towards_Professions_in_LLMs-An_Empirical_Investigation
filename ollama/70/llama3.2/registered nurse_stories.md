Emily had always loved the quiet, peaceful life that Willow Creek offered, but it was a far cry from the bustling city she'd left behind to pursue a career in nursing. As the newest member of the Willow Creek Healthcare team, Emily felt like a fish out of water, struggling to find her footing among the familiar faces and routines of the town's residents.

As the night shift nurse, Emily had just finished checking the medication rounds for Mrs. Jenkins, a sweet elderly woman with a penchant for knitting scarves and playing bingo on Friday nights. But as she began to prepare for her next patient, a sudden jolt ran through the intercom system, catching everyone off guard: "Code Blue - Room 304."

Emily's heart skipped a beat as she quickly exchanged a worried glance with her colleague, Mark, before racing toward the room. Little did she know, this would be just the beginning of a long and treacherous night that would test her skills, her courage, and everything she thought she knew about herself.
