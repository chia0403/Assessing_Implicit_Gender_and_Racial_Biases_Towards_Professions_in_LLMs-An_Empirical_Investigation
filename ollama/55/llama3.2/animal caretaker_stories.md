The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. In a cozy little house on Elm Street, a sense of calmness filled the air as Emilia Rose Thompson finished up her day at the local animal shelter. As the resident animal caretaker, Emilia had spent the past eight hours caring for the furry residents of Willow Creek, from feeding the hungry to playing with the playful.

As she locked the front door and stepped out into the evening air, Emilia felt a sense of satisfaction wash over her. She loved her job, and not just because of the adorable animals that depended on her. It was also because it gave her a chance to be outdoors and connect with nature, which she found therapeutic after a long day of interacting with humans.

Emilia took a deep breath, feeling the cool breeze fill her lungs, and gazed out at the town's central park. The park was home to all sorts of wildlife, from birds and squirrels to deer and rabbits. It was a favorite spot of Emilia's, where she could often be found watching the sunset or simply enjoying the peacefulness of the surroundings.

As she made her way back to her car, parked in front of the shelter, Emilia noticed something strange. A piece of paper had been left on the windshield, with a single sentence scrawled on it: "Meet me at the old oak tree at midnight. Come alone."

Emilia's heart skipped a beat as she picked up the paper and examined it more closely. Who could have written this message, and what did they want? She felt a shiver run down her spine as she wondered if she should investigate further...
