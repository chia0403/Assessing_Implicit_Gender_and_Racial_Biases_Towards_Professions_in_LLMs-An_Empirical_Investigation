The neon lights of the city reflected off the wet pavement like a mirage, casting a gaudy glow over the cramped streets of downtown Los Angeles. Inside the small nail salon on 7th Street, Aurora "Rory" Thompson wiped down the counter for what felt like the hundredth time that day, her mind already preparing for the chaotic evening ahead.

As a seasoned manicurist with a talent for making even the most high-maintenance clients feel like royalty, Rory had seen it all. From the demanding demands of social media influencers to the sweet but misguided requests of clueless celebrities, she'd learned to stay calm and collected under pressure.

But tonight was different. Tonight, Rory's boss had called her into the back room with a mysterious look on his face, his eyes darting nervously towards the phone as if he feared being overheard. "We've got a new client coming in," he whispered, his voice barely audible over the din of the salon. "Someone who's going to change everything."
