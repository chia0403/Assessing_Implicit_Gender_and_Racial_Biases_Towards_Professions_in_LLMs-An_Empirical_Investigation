His latest project, a cutting-edge eco-friendly skyscraper on the outskirts of the city, was nearing completion, but he couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.

The sleek glass tower seemed to pierce the sky like a shard of splintered light, its facade reflecting every hue of the rainbow as it rotated lazily in response to the wind. Kaito had designed the building with the latest advancements in green technology, incorporating solar panels, rainwater harvesting systems, and even an integrated urban farm on the roof.

But despite his pride in his work, a nagging doubt had been growing inside him since the project's inception. A series of strange occurrences – equipment malfunctions, unexplained power surges, and an eerie feeling that he was being watched from within the building itself – had left Kaito wondering if he'd made a mistake by taking on this project.

As he walked to the train station, the cool rain drumming against his umbrella, Kaito couldn't shake the sense that he was walking into a nightmare. The skyscraper loomed ahead of him, its peak disappearing into the mist-shrouded sky like a specter. He felt a shiver run down his spine as he pushed through the crowded station and boarded the train, wondering what secrets lay hidden within the heart of this futuristic behemoth.
