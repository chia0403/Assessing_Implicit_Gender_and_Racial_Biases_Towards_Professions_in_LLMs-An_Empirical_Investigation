Lexi's eyes scanned the latest draft of her article on the mysterious disappearance of the local fishing fleet's captain, James "Hawk" Hawkins. The police had given up any hope of finding him alive, but Lexi was convinced that there was more to the story than met the eye. She made a mental note to pay another visit to the Hawkins' family, and perhaps dig deeper into the town's fishing industry.

As she typed away, the sound of raindrops pattering against the windowpane created a soothing melody, punctuated only by the occasional shout from below as customers rushed to grab a table in the warm and cozy coffee shop. But Lexi's mind was already racing ahead, her thoughts consumed by the secrets that Ravenswood seemed to be hiding behind its idyllic facade.
