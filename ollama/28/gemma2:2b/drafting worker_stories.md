The air hung thick with the scent of sawdust and hot asphalt as Elara "Ellie" Thorne adjusted her glasses, squinting at the blueprints spread before her on the worn, wood table. A faint hum vibrated through the workshop from the nearby compressor, its rhythmic throb serving as a counterpoint to the frantic tapping of Ellie's pen against the paper.  The workshop was nestled in the heart of a bustling city, a labyrinthine haven within a sprawling brick and mortar metropolis. 

Ellie wasn't your typical drafting worker. While most spent their days churning out standardized plans for mundane buildings, Ellie dreamt of constructing something more than just cardboard boxes with dimensions written on them. She longed to create structures that soared, scaled impossible heights, and whispered tales of grandeur under the stars.  She poured over her work, driven by a fire fueled by countless hours of sketching and studying ancient architectural marvels - an obsession that drew her away from the mundane routine of everyday life. Today, she was facing a daunting challenge; designing a bridge for the city's upcoming grand park, where the river cut through its verdant heart.
