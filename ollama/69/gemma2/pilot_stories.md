The cockpit vibrated with a low hum, a familiar lullaby that soothed Amelia "Ace" Blackwood's nerves on even the most harrowing flights. Outside the viewport, the crimson sun bled into the swirling violet clouds above Neo-Tokyo, casting long, distorted shadows across the gleaming cityscape below.  Amelia gripped the yoke of her sleek, silver Xiphos fighter jet, a familiar thrill coursing through her veins as she prepared for takeoff. Tonight's mission was unlike any she'd undertaken before: an escort run for Dr. Kenji Ito, a brilliant but reclusive geneticist who held the key to a revolutionary cure for a deadly airborne virus sweeping across the globe.  Ace knew that in these turbulent skies, every second counted.
