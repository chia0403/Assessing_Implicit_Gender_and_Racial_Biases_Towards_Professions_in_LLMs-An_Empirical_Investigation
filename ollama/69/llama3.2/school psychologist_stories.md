A string of disturbing incidents had been plaguing the school in recent weeks - pranks gone too far, aggressive outbursts, and whispered rumors of a mysterious student who seemed to be hiding behind the mask of an average teenager.

Emily's instincts told her that there was more to this than just your typical high school drama. She had seen it before - the warning signs of bullying, social isolation, or even worse - but something about these incidents felt different. And as she took a deep breath and stepped into the crowded hallway, Emily couldn't help but wonder what secrets lay hidden beneath the surface of Oakdale High's seemingly idyllic facade.
