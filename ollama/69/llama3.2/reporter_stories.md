Tonight, however, was different. Tonight, she stood at a crossroads, facing a story that threatened to upend everything she thought she knew about the people and institutions she had sworn to protect. The phone in her pocket buzzed softly, its vibrations like a gentle warning: this was it – the moment of truth.
