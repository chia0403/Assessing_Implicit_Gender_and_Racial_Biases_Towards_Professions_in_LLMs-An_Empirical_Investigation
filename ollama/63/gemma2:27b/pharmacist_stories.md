Rain lashed against the windowpanes of "The Apothecary's Den," blurring the streetlights outside into shimmering halos. Inside, Amelia Cartwright meticulously counted out tiny blue capsules, her brow furrowed in concentration. The scent of lavender and chamomile wafted from jars lining the shelves, a comforting counterpoint to the storm raging beyond.

Amelia was known in the quaint seaside town of Oakhaven for her gentle touch and uncanny ability to conjure the perfect remedy for any ailment. From soothing coughs to easing anxieties, she dispensed her knowledge with a warm smile and a listening ear. Tonight, however, a knot of unease tightened in Amelia's stomach. The storm felt ominous, like a prelude to something unsettling. 

A sudden clang from the back door startled her. It swung open violently, revealing a shadowy figure drenched to the bone.
