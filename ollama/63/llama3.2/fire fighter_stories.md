---

The sun had just begun to set over the small town of Oakdale, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint Main Street. The smell of freshly brewed coffee wafted from the local diner, mingling with the acrid scent of smoke that still lingered in the air. It was a stark reminder of the fire that had ravaged the old mill on the outskirts of town just hours before.

In the midst of this chaos, Captain Ryder Jackson stood tall, his bright red helmet and heavy coat a stark contrast to the exhaustion etched on his face. A seasoned firefighter with over a decade of experience under his belt, Ryder had seen it all - from the smallest kitchen fires to the most devastating industrial disasters. But nothing could have prepared him for what lay ahead.

As he surveyed the damage at the old mill, Ryder's mind wandered back to the events that had led up to this moment. A mysterious arsonist had struck again, leaving a trail of destruction in their wake. The police were stumped, and the community was on edge. Little did they know, the real challenge was only just beginning.

With a deep breath, Ryder shouldered his pack and began to survey the scene, his trained eyes scanning for any sign of what might have started the fire. It was going to be a long night...
