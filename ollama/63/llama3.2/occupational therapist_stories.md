The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. In a cozy office nestled between a vintage bookstore and a bakery, Dr. Astrid Helena Andersdotter sat at her desk, sipping on a cup of steaming coffee as she prepared for another long day of working with her clients.

As an occupational therapist, Astrid had dedicated herself to helping people overcome physical and emotional challenges, whether it was recovering from an injury or coping with the aftermath of trauma. Her small practice, nestled in the heart of Willow Creek, was a haven for those seeking support and guidance on their journey towards healing.

With her bright blue eyes shining behind a pair of wire-rimmed glasses, Astrid smiled to herself as she began to review her schedule for the day. She had sessions lined up with everything from children struggling with sensory integration issues to adults recovering from strokes or joint replacements. But amidst the chaos and uncertainty of her work, Astrid felt a sense of calm and purpose that she couldn't find anywhere else.

Little did she know, today would be different. Today, a mysterious phone call would change everything...
