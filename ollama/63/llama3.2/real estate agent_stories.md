---

The sun had barely cracked the horizon over the sleepy coastal town of Crescent Cove, casting a warm golden light over the quaint streets and bustling beachside boardwalks. It was a crisp autumn morning in late October, and the smell of saltwater and freshly brewed coffee filled the air.

In her sleek black sedan, parked at the edge of the Cove, real estate agent Ruby Mae Reynolds sipped on a lukewarm cup of coffee as she gazed out at the endless stretch of ocean. Her eyes scanned the horizon, taking in the sailboats bobbing gently on the waves and the seagulls wheeling overhead.

With her curly brown hair tied back in a ponytail and her bright blue eyes squinting against the morning light, Ruby Mae was a familiar sight around town. She'd lived in Crescent Cove all her life, working for her family's real estate firm since she was old enough to hold a pen. Over the years, she'd built a reputation as one of the most knowledgeable and enthusiastic agents in town, with a knack for sniffing out hidden gems and connecting clients with their dream homes.

But today was different. Today, Ruby Mae had a feeling that something big was about to change everything. She couldn't quite put her finger on it - maybe it was the strange look from the old sea captain who owned the shop next door, or the cryptic message scrawled on her coffee cup: "Be careful what you wish for." Whatever it was, Ruby Mae knew that she needed to be ready for anything. Little did she know, the day was just beginning...
