The rain hammered against the panoramic windows of Amelia Cartwright's corner office, mimicking the relentless drumming in her chest. Thirty-two stories below, the city lights blurred into an impressionist painting, but Amelia couldn’t appreciate the view. Not tonight. Tonight, she was staring down the barrel of a corporate gunfight. The quarterly earnings report was due tomorrow, and Cartwright Industries, once a titan of the tech world, was bleeding red ink.

Amelia, with her perfectly coiffed auburn hair and crisp Armani suit, exuded an aura of controlled power. But beneath the polished veneer, anxiety gnawed at her. She’d climbed the corporate ladder relentlessly, fueled by ambition and a fierce desire to prove herself in a male-dominated world. Now, facing the imminent collapse of the company she'd poured her heart and soul into, she felt a terrifying pang of doubt. Could she save Cartwright Industries? Or would this be the fall that finally brought her down?
