As a professor of environmental science at the local university, Eli had spent countless hours studying the effects of human activity on this very ecosystem. But as she gazed out at the lake's tranquil surface now, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A nagging sense of unease had been growing inside her for weeks, and she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was.
