Elara's mind wandered to the upcoming event as she carefully labeled bottles of cough syrup and handed out samples of her homemade skincare products. The town fair had been a staple of Willow Creek's social calendar for decades, and Elara was proud to be one of its many contributors.

As the door swung open, signaling the arrival of the evening crowd, Elara's thoughts turned back to work. She smiled warmly at the familiar faces of the townspeople, exchanging pleasantries as they came in to stock up on essentials or simply chat about their day. Little did she know, tonight would be anything but ordinary...
