Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun had long since set on the concrete jungle of Lower Manhattan, casting a golden glow over the towering skyscrapers of Wall Street. The neon lights of the trading floors reflected off the wet pavement, creating a dizzying display of color and energy.

In a small, high-rise office nestled between two giants of finance, Alexander "Xander" Thompson sat hunched over his computer, staring intently at the screen as he watched his portfolio fluctuate with each passing moment. As one of the top stock brokers on Wall Street, Xander had made a name for himself as a master strategist and risk-taker.

With his razor-sharp mind and quick wit, he had built a reputation as a go-to expert in the high-stakes world of finance. But despite his success, Xander couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A nagging sense of unease had been growing inside him all day, fueled by a cryptic message from an anonymous source that seemed to hint at a catastrophic event on the horizon.

As he typed out a series of rapid-fire orders, trying to make sense of the jumbled signals emanating from his screens, Xander's mind wandered back to the phone call he had received earlier that day. The low-pitched voice on the other end had spoken only two words: "Trust no one." Suddenly, Xander felt like he was trapped in a game of cat and mouse, with the fate of his career hanging precariously in the balance.

The fluorescent lights above Xander's desk seemed to flicker ominously, as if warning him that something was indeed amiss. With a sense of growing trepidation, he leaned back in his chair and let out a deep breath, preparing himself for what was to come...
