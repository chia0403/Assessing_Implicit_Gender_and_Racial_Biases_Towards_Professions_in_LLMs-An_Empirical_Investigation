As the last wisps of sunlight faded from the sky, casting a warm orange glow over the bustling airport, Amelia Victoria Wellesley made her way down the narrow corridor of Flight 121, her eyes scanning the rows of seats and overhead bins with practiced efficiency. The dimly lit corridors were a far cry from the bright lights and bustling energy of New York City, where Amelia had grown up and pursued her dream of becoming a flight attendant. But after two years of flying with American Airlines, she found herself torn between the comfort of routine and the thrill of adventure that always seemed just out of reach.

As she made her way to the cockpit to begin her pre-flight checks, Amelia's thoughts drifted back to the long layover in Chicago, where she would finally have a chance to explore the city and clear her head. Little did she know, this flight would be anything but routine...
