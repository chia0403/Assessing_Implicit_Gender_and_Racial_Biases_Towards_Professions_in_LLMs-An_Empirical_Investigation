Emily rolled her eyes good-naturedly; she knew it was probably just another misplaced prescription or a misfiled lab result. But something about the tone of the voice sent a shiver down her spine, and for once, even her practiced skepticism couldn't shake off the feeling that this was more than just a routine medical mix-up.

At 25, Emily Mae Wilson had already accumulated a decade of experience as a Physician's Assistant in Willow Creek, and she knew its rhythms better than anyone. Yet, on days like today, when the morning light seemed to hold secrets hidden behind it, she couldn't help but wonder if her small town was hiding more than just the usual medical mysteries.
