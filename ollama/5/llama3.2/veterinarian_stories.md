Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood at the edge of the barn, her eyes scanning the rows of stalls as she made her way through the pre-dawn mist that clung to the fields like a damp shroud. The sun was just starting to rise over the horizon, casting a golden glow over the landscape and illuminating the dew-kissed grass. Lexi took a deep breath, savoring the scent of fresh hay and damp earth, and began her rounds.

As a veterinarian at Green Meadow Farm, a thriving horse farm on the outskirts of town, Lexi was no stranger to early mornings. But tonight felt different, somehow. The air seemed charged with anticipation, as if something - or someone - was waiting in the wings.

Lexi's gaze landed on the stall door next to her own, and she felt a sudden jolt of recognition. It had been three years since she'd last seen him, and yet the memory of his piercing blue eyes and chiseled jawline still lingered, like an open wound. Jake McCoy, former racing star turned rehoming farmhand, was back in town, and Lexi couldn't help but wonder what brought him to Green Meadow Farm.

The familiar routine of her job washed over her like a warm bath, soothing her frazzled nerves as she began her examination of the horses' stalls. But beneath the surface, Lexi's mind was already racing with possibilities - and old feelings that refused to be buried.
