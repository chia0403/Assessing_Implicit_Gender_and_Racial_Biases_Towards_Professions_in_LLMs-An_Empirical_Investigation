---

The sweet scent of vanilla and sugar wafted through the air as Emilia Rose Thompson carefully piped intricate borders onto the wedding cake in front of her. The warm glow of the studio lights highlighted the delicate swirls of frosting, making them look like delicate snowflakes on top of the towering masterpiece.

It was a typical Saturday morning at Emily's Bakehouse, the small cake decorating studio that Emilia had founded with her best friend and business partner, Olivia. The phone rang, shrill in the quiet of the morning, breaking the spell of concentration that Emilia had worked so hard to achieve. She smiled as she picked up the receiver, hoping it was another wedding booking or a new client eager to try out her custom cake designs.

But as she listened to the voice on the other end of the line, Emilia's smile faltered. It was from Mrs. Jenkins, an old family friend who had always been kind but also brutally honest. "Emilia, I'm so sorry to call you at such a bad time," Mrs. Jenkins said, her tone tinged with worry. "But I need to talk to you about something important."

Emilia's heart skipped a beat as she wondered what was wrong. Had someone broken into the studio? Or was there a problem with one of her cakes?

"Of course, Mrs. Jenkins," Emilia replied, trying to sound calm. "What's on your mind?"

But as she listened to Mrs. Jenkins' words, Emilia realized that this call might just change everything...
