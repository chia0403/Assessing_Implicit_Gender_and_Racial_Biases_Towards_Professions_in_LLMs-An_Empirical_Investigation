The wind howled like a banshee outside Professor Eleanor Alistair's office window, rattling the panes with icy claws. Rain lashed against the glass, blurring the already faded lights of the city below. Eleanor, her grey hair pulled back in a tight bun, barely noticed the storm raging around her. Her focus was entirely on the ancient parchment spread across her desk, its faded ink whispering secrets from centuries past.  

The setting: A gothic university library nestled high atop a windswept hill overlooking a sprawling, perpetually overcast city. The building itself is a labyrinth of dark corridors and towering shelves groaning under the weight of countless forgotten tomes. 


Eleanor Alistair, renowned for her expertise in arcane languages and forbidden lore, felt an undeniable tremor of excitement course through her as she deciphered the cryptic symbols on the parchment. This was no ordinary manuscript; it hinted at a long-lost civilization, a whispered legend dismissed by most scholars as mere fantasy.
