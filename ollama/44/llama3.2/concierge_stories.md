---

The rain-soaked streets of Manhattan gleamed like polished onyx as Emilia "Mia" Thompson, Concierge at the luxurious Bellwether Hotel, gazed out the window of her office. The soft glow of the city lights danced across her face, casting an ethereal sheen over her features. With a cup of lukewarm coffee in hand, Mia surveyed the chaos unfolding before her - the throngs of tourists swarming the lobby, the harried look on the eyes of the front desk staff, and the anxious tone that always seemed to emanate from the hotel's wealthy patrons.

As she sipped her coffee, Mia's mind wandered to the peculiar requests she'd received over the past few weeks. Guests whispering about "the package" in hushed tones, employees disappearing for hours at a time without explanation, and an air of unease that seemed to permeate every corner of the hotel. Despite the whispers, Mia remained resolute - she had a job to do, and that was ensuring the Bellwether's esteemed reputation remained intact.

Just as she was about to dismiss her doubts, a knock at the door interrupted her reverie. It was one of the maids, her face tense with concern. "Mia, sir? There's something you need to know."

Mia's instincts told her that this could be the start of something - something big. She leaned forward, her eyes locking onto those of her maid, and whispered a single word: "What?"
