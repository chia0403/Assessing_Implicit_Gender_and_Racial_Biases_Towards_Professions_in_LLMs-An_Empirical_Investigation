The misty dawn broke over the sleepy town of Ravenswood, shrouding its quaint streets in a damp, emerald veil. In the heart of the town square stood the imposing Victorian mansion of Ravenswood Manor, its stone façade weathered to a soft grey that seemed to blend seamlessly into the surrounding landscape. Within the manor's walls, a quiet figure stirred.

Emmett Thaddeus Finch, a ruggedly handsome groundskeeper with calloused hands and eyes as green as the mist-shrouded trees he tended, made his way down the winding driveway, his worn boots crunching on the gravel beneath. The morning air was crisp with the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves, and Emmett breathed it in deeply, feeling alive as he began another day's work.

As a groundskeeper for Ravenswood Manor, Emmett had spent years tending to its meticulously manicured lawns, trimming hedges, and pruning flowers. But today was different. Today, he sensed that something was amiss – a subtle tension hung in the air like an unspoken promise of change.

And then, as he stepped out into the misty dawn, Emmett caught sight of a figure watching him from behind the manor's imposing stone walls...
