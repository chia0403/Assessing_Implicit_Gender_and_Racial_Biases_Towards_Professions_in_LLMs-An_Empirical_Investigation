Oakdale was never going to be a bustling metropolis like Manhattan, where his medical school days had been spent studying under some of the city's top dentists. Here, the most exciting thing that ever happened was the annual Harvest Festival – and Thompson couldn't help but feel a twinge of restlessness as he pondered whether this quiet life was truly what he was meant for.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, casting Oakdale in an even deeper shade of twilight, Thompson knew it was time to face whatever challenges lay ahead.
