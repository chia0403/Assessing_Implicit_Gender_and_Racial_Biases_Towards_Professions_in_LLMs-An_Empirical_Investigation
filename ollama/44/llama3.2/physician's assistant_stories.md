Sophia, as she was known to her friends and colleagues, had grown up in this small town and knew its rhythms and routines like the back of her hand. She had always been drawn to medicine, and after completing her training, she had returned home to work alongside Dr. Michael Lee, a kind-hearted general practitioner who had taken her under his wing.
