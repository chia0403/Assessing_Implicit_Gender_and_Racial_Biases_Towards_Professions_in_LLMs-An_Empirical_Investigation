## Story Opening:

Rain lashed against the windows of Dr. Evelyn Thorne's small cottage, mimicking the rhythmic pounding in her chest.  The storm had rolled in like a phantom, snatching away the last vestiges of summer's lingering warmth. The air hung thick and heavy with the scent of salt and rain as she stared out over the churning sea. 

Evelyn, a Physician Assistant known for her quiet demeanor and unwavering empathy, was hunched at the worn wooden desk in her office.  She held a thin file, its contents heavier than they should be. The diagnosis – ALS – had come just hours ago, leaving Evelyn feeling like she was drowning. 

**Setting:** A secluded cottage overlooking the crashing waves of a stormy coast in Maine. 

**Main Character:** Dr. Evelyn Thorne. An empathetic and dedicated Physician Assistant facing a heavy personal burden as she grapples with a challenging patient diagnosis.
