The sirens wailed outside, piercing the crisp evening air as Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a seasoned emergency medical technician (EMT), stepped out of the ambulance and onto the damp pavement. The bright lights of the hospital's Emergency Department cast an otherworldly glow over the scene, illuminating the chaos that had unfolded on this fateful night.

It was 10:45 PM on a Friday, and the small town of Willow Creek was bracing for its annual summer festival, which usually drew in crowds from all over the region. But tonight, something felt off. The streets were eerily quiet, except for the sound of sirens and muffled voices coming from within the hospital walls.

Lexi's eyes scanned the area, taking in the sight of paramedics swarming around a stretcher, while others rushed to secure the perimeter. She knew this drill all too well – emergencies often unfolded under the guise of celebration. Her heart rate quickened as she mentally prepared for whatever lay ahead.

As an EMT with over five years of experience, Lexi had seen her fair share of traumas, from car accidents to cardiac arrests. But something about tonight's scene felt different. The patients were all young adults, and the nature of their injuries was peculiar – it seemed as though they'd been attacked by...something.

"Lexi, we need you to assess Patient 3!" one of the paramedics yelled over the din. Lexi nodded, her eyes locking onto the stretcher, where a young woman lay motionless, her skin deathly pale and her eyes frozen in terror.

With a deep breath, Lexi sprang into action, ready to face whatever horrors this night had in store for her. Little did she know that the true horror was only just beginning...
