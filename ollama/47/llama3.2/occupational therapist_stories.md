The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint Main Street. The sound of children's laughter carried on the breeze as they played tag in front of the old-fashioned ice cream parlor. Inside, occupational therapist Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson sat at her desk, sipping a cup of coffee and staring out the window at the bustling street.

Lexi had just finished a long day at the local children's hospital, where she worked with kids who had been injured in an accident involving a runaway truck that had crashed into the town's annual summer festival. The memory of the chaotic scene still lingered in her mind, but she pushed it aside as she focused on the stack of papers in front of her.

She was about to leave for the day when her phone rang, shrill in the quiet evening air. Lexi answered, expecting it to be a colleague or perhaps a patient's family member checking in. Instead, she heard a hesitant voice on the other end of the line.

"Dr. Thompson? It's Karen from Social Services. I hope I'm not interrupting anything... I was hoping to speak with you about something rather urgent."
