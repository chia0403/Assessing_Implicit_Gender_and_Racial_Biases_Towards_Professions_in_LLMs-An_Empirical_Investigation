It was a drizzly autumn evening in the small town of Willow Creek, where the smell of freshly baked pastries wafted from the local bakery and the sound of jazz music drifted from the vintage cafe on Main Street. Inside "Locks & Co.", a cozy hair salon nestled between a used bookstore and a boutique clothing store, 27-year-old hairdresser Emilia Rose Ellis was prepping for another long day of styling and chatting with her clients.

As she steamed hot water in the sink and rummaged through her supply cabinet, Emilia's mind wandered to the peculiar phone call she'd received earlier that morning. The cryptic message from an unknown number had simply read: "Meet me at the old oak tree in the town square at midnight. Come alone."

The hairs on the back of Emilia's neck stood on end as she tried to shake off the feeling of unease that lingered long after she'd hung up the phone. What could this mysterious stranger want from her? And what did it have to do with anything, anyway?

Little did Emilia know, her life was about to take a dramatic turn, one that would lead her down a twisted path of secrets and lies, hidden histories and unexpected revelations...
