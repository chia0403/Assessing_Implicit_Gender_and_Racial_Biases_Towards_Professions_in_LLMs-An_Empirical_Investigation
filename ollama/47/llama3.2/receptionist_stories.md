The message sent a shiver down her spine, and for the first time in years, Emily felt like something was off in Willow Creek.

Emily took a deep breath, trying to shake off the feeling of unease. She had lived in this small town her entire life, and she knew its rhythms better than anyone. But as she glanced out the window, watching the townspeople hurry to and fro, she couldn't help but wonder: what was really going on behind the friendly facades? And why did that letter feel like a warning from someone who knew her secrets?
