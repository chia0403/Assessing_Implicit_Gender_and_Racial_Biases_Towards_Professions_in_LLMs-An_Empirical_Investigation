Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain-soaked streets of New Haven City were always at their most unforgiving on nights like this. The storm that had been brewing all day finally unleashed its fury, casting a veil of darkness over the city's already seedy underbelly.

At the heart of this darkness stood the imposing structure known as the Umbra Facility, a maximum-security prison for the most violent and notorious offenders in the country. It was here that Dr. Elijah Jaxon Wynter, a grizzled and seasoned corrections officer with over a decade of service, stood watchful guard.

Elijah, or "Eli" to those who knew him, had seen it all - from the brutal riots that had ravaged the prison in his early years to the countless escape attempts and attempted murders. But tonight was different. Tonight, Elijah felt a growing sense of unease, as if something was off, lurking just beyond the periphery of his vision.

As he made his rounds, checking on the various tiers of the prison's crumbling infrastructure, Eli couldn't shake the feeling that the storm outside was mirrored by some deeper tempest within the walls. And it was this sense of foreboding that would prove to be his downfall...
