Rain lashed against the windowpanes of The Nourishing Nook, blurring the view of the quaint cobblestone street outside. Inside, Emilia Cartwright, a nutritionist with auburn curls escaping her bun and eyes that sparkled like emeralds, was hunched over a countertop covered in vibrant vegetables. She meticulously chopped kale and red onion, her mind swirling with recipes for a new client struggling with chronic fatigue.

The bell above the door jingled, breaking Emilia's concentration. A gust of wind followed the newcomer, momentarily extinguishing the warm glow from the antique chandelier hanging above. Emilia looked up, her smile widening as she recognized the woman who hesitantly stepped inside.
