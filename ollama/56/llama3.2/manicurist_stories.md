The sun had just begun to set over the bustling streets of Tokyo's Shinjuku district, casting a warm orange glow over the crowded sidewalks. In a small, unassuming nail salon nestled between a used bookstore and a 24-hour ramen shop, Emiko Nakahara was busy prepping for the evening rush.

As the owner and head manicurist at Sakura Beauty Spa, Emiko took pride in her work, meticulously crafting intricate designs on clients' fingers and toes with precision and care. She had spent years honing her skills, learning from her mother, a skilled Japanese artist who had taught Emiko the ancient art of traditional nail styling.

With her signature silver-pink hair styled in a neat bob, Emiko's bright eyes sparkled as she expertly mixed colors on her palette, preparing for the evening's appointments. She was particularly excited tonight, as one of her most high-profile clients – the famous pop star, Rina Tanaka – would be stopping by for a special pre-show manicure.

As the salon began to fill with customers, Emiko's hands moved swiftly and surely, her fingers dancing across the nail surface with the precision of a surgeon. But despite the chaos that surrounded her, Emiko felt a growing sense of unease, a nagging feeling that something was off in the usually predictable rhythms of her life...
