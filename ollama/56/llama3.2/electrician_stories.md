The phone on Emily's workbench rang out, shrill in the quiet atmosphere. She picked it up, answering with a practiced efficiency that belied the faint hint of tension in her voice. "Jenkins Electric Co., this is Emily."

But as she listened to the caller on the other end – a worried-sounding receptionist from the office building's management company – Emily's eyes narrowed further, her mind already racing with possibilities.
