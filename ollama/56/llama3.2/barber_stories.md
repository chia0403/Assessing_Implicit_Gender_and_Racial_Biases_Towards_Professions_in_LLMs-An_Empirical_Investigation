Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun had just begun to set over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint Main Street. The air was filled with the sweet scent of freshly cut grass and the sound of children's laughter carried from the park where they were playing tag.

Inside the old-fashioned barber shop on Main Street, "Cutting Edge" Barbershop, 32-year-old Julian Edward Thompson was busy prepping for the evening rush. Julian had spent years honing his craft as a barber, and had built a loyal following of clients who appreciated his attention to detail and warm bedside manner.

As he expertly trimmed a customer's nose hairs with his trusty scissors, Julian couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over him. This was his happy place - surrounded by the familiar sounds and smells of the shop, with his regulars chatting and laughing in the chairs. It was where he felt most at home.

But despite the tranquility of the moment, Julian's mind kept wandering back to the strange occurrence that had been plaguing him all day. A mysterious customer, who had walked into the shop earlier that morning, had left a cryptic message on his mirror with a single word: "Watch". Julian had tried to brush it off as mere small talk, but the words lingered in his mind like a ghostly whisper.

As the evening wore on and the shop grew quiet, Julian couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. And he wasn't alone...
