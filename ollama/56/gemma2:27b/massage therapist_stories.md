The bell above the door jingled, startling her. A figure shrouded in the shadows of the awning shuffled in, shaking droplets onto the welcome mat. Eloise plastered on her best smile, hoping it masked the tremor in her hands. "Welcome to Zenith Massage," she greeted, her voice a touch too high. 

"Got an opening?" The figure stepped forward, revealing a man with rain-soaked hair plastered to his forehead and eyes that seemed to hold the weight of the world.
