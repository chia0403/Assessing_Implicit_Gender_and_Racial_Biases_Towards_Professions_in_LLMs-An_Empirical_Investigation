As the last wisps of sunlight faded from the sky, casting a warm orange glow over the sleepy coastal town of Tidal Cove, real estate agent Emilia Mae Thompson stood outside her office, sipping on a lukewarm coffee and gazing out at the ocean. The salty spray carried the scent of seaweed and saltwater, mingling with the faint hint of freshly cut grass from the beachside gardens. It was moments like these that Emilia loved most about her job – the peacefulness of the morning, the sound of seagulls crying overhead, and the promise of a new day full of possibilities.

Emilia's eyes wandered to the small, weathered sign above her office door, creaking gently in the gentle breeze: "Mae's Marvelous Homes". She smiled wryly, thinking about how far she'd come since those early days as a young agent with a passion for finding the perfect home. Now, at 32, Emilia was one of Tidal Cove's top agents, known for her encyclopedic knowledge of the local market and her uncanny ability to sniff out hidden gems.

As she took another sip of her coffee, Emilia's phone buzzed in her pocket. She pulled it out, eyeing the screen with a mixture of excitement and trepidation. A message from a potential client – a reclusive millionaire looking for his dream home in Tidal Cove...
