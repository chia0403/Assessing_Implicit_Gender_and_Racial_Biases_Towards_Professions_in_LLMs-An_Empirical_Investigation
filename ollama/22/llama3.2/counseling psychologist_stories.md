Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stepped out of her car and onto the rain-soaked streets of downtown Seattle, the grey sky mirroring her own somber mood. It had been three months since she'd returned to her hometown after a decade away, fleeing the stress and pressure of her high-profile job at a prestigious university counseling center in New York City. Lexi's decision to leave had come after a particularly grueling case left her questioning whether she was truly making a difference in people's lives.

As she gazed out at the towering skyscrapers and bustling streets, Lexi couldn't help but feel a sense of unease. She'd always loved this city, but now it seemed too big, too loud, and too overwhelming. The rain dripped off her umbrella like a steady heartbeat, and Lexi felt her own pulse quicken in response.

She took a deep breath, letting the cool air fill her lungs, and began to walk towards her small office building on Capitol Hill. Her phone buzzed with an incoming text from her best friend and colleague, Rachel: "Hey girl, what's the plan for tonight? We need to grab drinks and catch up."

Lexi smiled wryly to herself as she walked; it had been too long since she'd let loose and let someone else hold her together. Tonight was exactly what she needed.

But little did Lexi know, her life was about to take a dramatic turn...
