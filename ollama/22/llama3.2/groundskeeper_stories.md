The misty dawn air clung to the dew-kissed grass like a damp shroud as Emilia "Mia" Thompson stepped out of her small cottage and into the stillness of the gardens at Ravenswood Manor. As the groundskeeper, Mia had spent countless mornings tending to the manicured lawns, pruning the roses, and coaxing new life from the ancient trees that stood sentinel around the estate.

With a worn pair of gloves and a faded denim jacket slung over her shoulder, Mia surveyed the grounds with a practiced eye. The mist swirled around her ankles as she made her way along the winding paths, her boots leaving faint impressions in the damp earth. The air was heavy with the scent of damp soil and decaying leaves, but beneath it all lay the sweet, heady aroma of blooming flowers – the first hints of spring's arrival.

As she walked, Mia's thoughts turned to the events that had led her to this quiet corner of the English countryside. She had left behind a life in the city, one marked by noise and chaos, in search of a simpler existence. And yet, as she breathed in the misty air and listened to the birdsong, she couldn't shake the feeling that she was being watched – that unseen eyes were trained on her from the shadows of Ravenswood Manor's ancient walls.
