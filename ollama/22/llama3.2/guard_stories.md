The night air was heavy with the scent of blooming jasmine as Sergeant Elianore "Eli" Thompson stepped out of the flickering torchlight of the castle walls and into the shadows of the abandoned gardens. The moon cast an eerie glow over the overgrown fountains and crumbling statues, making it seem like the very spirits of the ancient estate were watching him from the darkness.

As a member of the Night Guard, Eli's duty was to patrol the perimeter of the kingdom in the dead of night, ever vigilant for any signs of disturbance or danger. It was a solitary task, one that he had grown accustomed to over the years, but tonight felt different. Tonight, he had received a cryptic message from an unknown sender, warning him of a potential threat to the royal family.

Eli's eyes scanned the horizon, his gaze lingering on the familiar landmarks of the castle and its surroundings. He knew every inch of this land, every hidden path and secret passage. But tonight, something felt off. The trees seemed to be swaying in an unnatural rhythm, and the wind carried a faint whisper that sounded almost like a name: "Arachne".

With a sense of growing unease, Eli drew his sword and began to move cautiously through the darkness, ready to face whatever dangers lay ahead.
