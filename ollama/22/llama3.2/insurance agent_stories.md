Here's a potential story opening and setting:

---

As the sun set over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes, Emily Mae Jenkins stepped into her office, the worn wooden floor creaking beneath her feet. She had just finished a long day of navigating the complex world of insurance policies for her clients, and was looking forward to unwinding with a cup of coffee and some quiet time.

Emily's office, nestled above her family's bakery on Main Street, was a cozy space filled with the sweet scent of freshly baked goods and the sound of gentle chatter from the townspeople below. She had always been proud to carry on her family's tradition, just as her parents and grandparents before her had done. But it wasn't all sugar and spice - insurance sales could be tough, especially in a small town where everyone knew each other's business.

As she poured herself a cup of coffee, Emily gazed out the window at the quiet streets below, feeling grateful for this life she had built for herself. It was a life that, on the surface, seemed perfect - but beneath the surface, Emily couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A nagging sense of unease had been growing inside her all year, and she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was.

Just then, the phone on her desk jolted her out of her reverie, shrill and insistent. "Jenkins Insurance," Emily answered, her voice crisp and professional. Little did she know, this call would be the one that changed everything...
