The air hung thick with the scent of dust and aged paper in Agnes Thistlewick's cramped office. Sunlight filtered weakly through the grimy windowpanes, casting long shadows across the cluttered desk overflowing with ledgers, invoices, and half-empty teacups. Agnes, her spectacles perched precariously on the bridge of her nose, meticulously tallied figures in a tattered account book. Each scratch of her quill against parchment was accompanied by the rhythmic tick of the grandfather clock in the corner, a constant reminder of time's relentless march. 

Agnes Thistlewick wasn't known for adventure or excitement. Her world revolved around numbers, balances, and the comforting predictability of her routine. But today, a single, smudged envelope nestled amongst the usual bills threatened to disrupt that carefully curated order. It bore no return address, only a cryptic message scrawled across the front: "The truth lies buried beneath." 


Agnes sighed. The last thing she needed was another mystery to unravel.
