The scent of burnt toast hung heavy in the air, mingling with the faint aroma of cinnamon from Amelia Rose Davies' homemade granola bars. Outside her window, the San Francisco fog was rolling in, casting an ethereal grey veil over the Golden Gate Bridge.  Amelia sighed, pushing back a stray curl that had escaped her messy bun. Another Monday morning, another onslaught of desperate clients pouring into her small wellness practice nestled on bustling Fillmore Street. This one, however, promised to be different. His name was Lucas Hawthorne, and his dietary restrictions were... unusual, to say the least. Amelia wasn't sure what she'd find in that man's refrigerator.  Perhaps a bowl of unicorn tears? Or maybe just plain old kale.
