The sun had just begun to set over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. The air was filled with the sweet scent of blooming lilacs and the distant chatter of children playing in the park.

Inside the offices of Oakwood High School, guidance counselor Dr. Emily Joanne Wilson sat at her desk, sipping on a lukewarm cup of coffee as she stared out the window. It had been a long day already, with meetings and parent-teacher conferences weighing heavily on her mind. As she gazed out at the peaceful evening scene, she couldn't help but feel a sense of restlessness.

Dr. Wilson's thoughts were consumed by the upcoming school year, which promised to be filled with its own set of challenges and triumphs. She had spent countless hours preparing lesson plans and making phone calls, trying to anticipate the needs of her students. But as the summer drew to a close, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.

As she turned back to her desk, her eyes landed on a piece of paper slipped under her door. It was an anonymous note, scribbled in hasty handwriting:

"Meet me at the old oak tree in the park at midnight if you want to know the truth about what's really going on at Oakwood High."

Dr. Wilson's heart skipped a beat as she read the cryptic message. What could this be about? And who would dare leave such an ominous warning? She felt a shiver run down her spine as she folded the note and tucked it into her pocket, wondering if she was ready to uncover secrets that might upend everything she thought she knew about her school, her students, and herself.
