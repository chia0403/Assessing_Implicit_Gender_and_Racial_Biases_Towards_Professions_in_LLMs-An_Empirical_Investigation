The sun had just set over the bustling streets of Tokyo, casting a warm orange glow over the neon lights of the city. In a small salon nestled between a vintage clothing store and a ramen shop, Emiko Nakahara was hard at work, her skilled fingers moving with precision as she shaped and painted a delicate design onto a client's fingernails.

It was a Friday evening, and the salon was bustling with last-minute appointments before the weekend. The air was thick with the scent of nail polish and the soft hum of chatter from the clients. Emiko, or "Miko" to her friends, had been working as a manicurist for over five years, ever since she'd graduated from beauty school and moved back to Tokyo after a stint in New York City.

Despite the long hours and early mornings, Miko loved her job - there was something soothing about transforming someone's look, making them feel confident and beautiful. As she worked on the final details of the client's manicure, Miko couldn't shake the feeling that tonight was going to be different. She had a sense of restlessness, a feeling that the city outside was watching her, waiting for something to happen.

Little did she know, the night would bring more than just the usual Tokyo nightlife - it would bring secrets, surprises, and a transformation that would change her life forever.
