The misty dawn air clung to Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson like a damp shroud as she made her way through the quiet streets of Willow Creek, her eyes fixed on the old Victorian house in front of her. The once-grand home now stood as a testament to the town's fading glory, its ornate facade covered in ivy and moss, the windows like empty eyes staring back at Lexi.

As a physical therapist, Lexi had seen her fair share of injuries and ailments, but there was something about this house that drew her in. Maybe it was the sense of foreboding that seemed to emanate from its crumbling walls, or perhaps it was the whispered rumors of strange occurrences that had long been a part of Willow Creek's folklore. Whatever the reason, Lexi felt an inexplicable pull to this place, as if some unseen force was calling her inside.

She pushed open the creaky front door and stepped into the foyer, her eyes adjusting slowly to the dim light within. A chill ran down her spine as she heard a faint whisper in her ear, "Welcome home, Lexi."
