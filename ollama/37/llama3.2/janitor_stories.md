The fluorescent lights overhead cast an unforgiving glare over the worn linoleum floor, illuminating the scattered crumbs and dust bunnies that called the old school library home. It was 3:45 AM on a Wednesday morning, and Emilia "Mia" Thompson was already knee-deep in the stacks, her mop slung over her shoulder like a makeshift tool belt.

As a janitor at Lincoln High School, Mia knew every nook and cranny of the crumbling building by heart. She'd spent countless nights sweeping away discarded textbooks and mopping up spills from forgotten cafeteria food. It was a thankless job, but one that gave her a sense of order in a world that seemed to be constantly spinning out of control.

Mia's mind wandered as she worked, lost in the familiar rhythm of her cleaning routine. She'd been feeling restless lately, like there was something missing in her life - a nagging feeling she couldn't shake no matter how hard she tried to focus on the task at hand. Little did she know, tonight would be different from all the others...
