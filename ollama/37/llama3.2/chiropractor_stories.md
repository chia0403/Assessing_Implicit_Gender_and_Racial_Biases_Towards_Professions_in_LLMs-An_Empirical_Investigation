The sun had long since set on the small town of Oakdale, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a successful chiropractor with a passion for holistic health, stood outside her office building, sipping a cup of coffee as she gazed out at the quiet evening scene. The trees surrounding the property were ablaze with color, their leaves rustling softly in the breeze.

Lexi took a deep breath, feeling the stress and tension of the day melt away. She loved her job, but it could be emotionally draining at times - especially when dealing with patients who had been injured or afflicted by chronic pain. As she walked back into her office, the sound of gentle music and the scent of essential oils enveloped her, providing a sense of calm and tranquility.

Despite the peaceful atmosphere, Lexi's mind was already racing ahead to the busy day that lay ahead. She had a full schedule of patients waiting, and she needed to make sure everything was in order before they arrived. As she began to tidy up her office and prepare for the morning, a sense of unease crept over her. It wasn't just the usual worries about meeting deadlines or managing patient expectations - something else was on her mind, something that had been nagging at her all week.

Little did Lexi know, this was going to be a day like no other...
