Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The misty dawn crept over the small town of Ravenswood, shrouding its narrow streets in a damp veil that seemed to cling to everything like a damp blanket. The air was heavy with the smell of coal smoke and damp earth, carrying on it the faint scent of blooming lilacs that clung tenaciously to the crumbling brick facades of the old buildings.

In a small workshop nestled between a vintage bookstore and a greasy diner, the soft glow of a single overhead light cast an eerie pallor over the cluttered workbench. Amidst the scattered tools, scraps of metal, and yellowed drafting papers, Arthur Edwin Fletcher hunched over his latest project - a delicate model of a historic steam locomotive.

As he carefully measured and marked the intricate curves of the locomotive's frame, Arthur's mind wandered to the upcoming town council meeting. Rumors had been circulating about a proposed new development on the outskirts of town, one that would see the displacement of several long-time residents and small businesses in favor of a gleaming glass-and-steel complex. The prospect sent a shiver down Arthur's spine - he'd lived in Ravenswood all his life, and the thought of losing its quiet, rustic charm was unbearable.

With a sigh, Arthur put aside his drafting pen and reached for the cup of cold coffee that sat on the edge of his workbench, lost in thought as the morning sun slowly climbed higher over the rooftops. Little did he know, this ordinary day would soon become anything but...
