Rain lashed against the grimy windows of "Justice For All," the flickering neon sign outside struggling valiantly to pierce the gloom. Inside, Amelia Harding, her brow furrowed in concentration, tapped furiously on her keyboard. Stacks of legal documents threatened to topple from her cluttered desk, each one representing another desperate soul seeking solace in the labyrinthine corridors of the law.  

Amelia wasn't a lawyer, just a paralegal, but she carried the weight of their hopes and fears with a quiet intensity. Tonight, though, her usual focus was fractured. A call from her estranged father earlier that day had thrown a wrench into her carefully ordered world. He needed her help - not legal, but something far more personal and potentially dangerous.  The case files blurred before her eyes as Amelia wrestled with a decision that could change everything.
