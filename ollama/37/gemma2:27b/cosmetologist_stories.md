The fluorescent lights buzzed overhead, casting a sickly yellow glow over Esmeralda "Esme" Diaz's workstation. The air hung heavy with the scent of perming solution and hairspray, a familiar perfume Esme had grown to love over her ten years at Salon Rouge.  

Today, however, the usual symphony of blow dryers and chatter was replaced by an uneasy silence. A storm raged outside, mirroring the turmoil brewing in Esme's heart. Rain lashed against the windows, obscuring the view of bustling Fifth Avenue just beyond. The power flickered ominously, threatening to plunge them all into darkness.


Esme nervously twisted a strand of auburn hair around her finger, staring at the reflection staring back from the mirror. A woman with eyes the color of warm honey and a smile tinged with worry. She hadn't expected this call, hadn't expected this sudden upheaval in her carefully constructed life.
