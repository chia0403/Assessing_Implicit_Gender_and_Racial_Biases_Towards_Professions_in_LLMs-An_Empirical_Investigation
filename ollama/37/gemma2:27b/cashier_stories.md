The flickering fluorescent lights buzzed overhead like agitated wasps, casting a sickly yellow pallor over everything in the convenience store. Amelia "Amy" Cartwright sighed, her breath fogging the plexiglass shield separating her from the endless stream of weary travelers and late-night snack seekers. Outside, the wind howled, whipping rain against the grimy windows and turning the desolate highway into a river of shimmering asphalt. Amy longed for the warmth of her tiny apartment, a steaming mug of chamomile tea, and the comforting silence that only solitude could provide. But duty called, and the endless beep of the register was her unwelcome symphony.

Just then, the bell above the door chimed, announcing the arrival of a new customer. Amy braced herself, plastering on her best customer service smile. Little did she know this encounter would shatter the monotony of her night and send ripples through her otherwise ordinary life.
