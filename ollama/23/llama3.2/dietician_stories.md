Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun had just set over the bustling streets of downtown Portland, casting a warm orange glow over the crowded sidewalks and trendy cafes. In a small office above a used bookstore on NW 23rd Avenue, Dr. Sophia Elizabeth Thompson, a registered dietitian with a passion for holistic nutrition, sat at her desk, sipping a cup of tea as she reviewed her schedule for the evening.

Sophia, or Sivi to those who knew her best, was known for her no-nonsense approach to healthy eating and her uncanny ability to connect with clients from all walks of life. Her practice, Natural Nourish, had grown steadily over the past five years, as word of her innovative approach to nutrition spread through the city's health-conscious community.

As she scanned her schedule, Sivi noticed that tonight was a particularly packed one, with back-to-back appointments at 6 PM and 8 PM. She took a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for a night of listening ears and expert advice. Little did she know, this evening would bring more than just the usual clients seeking guidance on everything from sugar addiction to gluten intolerance.

Just as Sivi was about to start her first appointment, her phone rang, shrill and insistent. She hesitated for a moment before answering, wondering who could be calling at 5:45 PM on a Tuesday night. As she listened to the voice on the other end of the line, a shiver ran down her spine...
