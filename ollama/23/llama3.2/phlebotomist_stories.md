The fluorescent lights hummed overhead, casting an eerie glow over the small medical laboratory in the heart of downtown Los Angeles. The sign above the counter read "Medical Analysis Solutions" in faded letters, and the walls were adorned with dusty framed certificates and worn photographs of happy patients.

Amidst the sterile chaos, Phoebe Elizabeth Thompson stood tall, her bright blue eyes scanning the rows of humming machinery as she prepared for another day of pricking veins. As a skilled phlebotomist, Phoebe took pride in her work, ensuring that each sample was collected with precision and care. Her long, curly brown hair was tied back in a loose ponytail, and a small tattoo of a snake coiled around her right wrist – a symbol of her rebellious past.

Phoebe's gaze lingered on the calendar hanging above the counter, where today's date read March 15th. It was supposed to be a quiet day at Medical Analysis Solutions, with only a handful of patients scheduled for blood draws. But as she began to prep her equipment, Phoebe couldn't shake off the feeling that something was off.

A faint noise in the back of the lab caught her attention – a low creaking sound, followed by the soft rustle of paper shuffling. It sounded like someone was trying not to be heard, but still making themselves known. Phoebe's curiosity piqued, she set aside her thoughts and turned to face the unknown presence...
