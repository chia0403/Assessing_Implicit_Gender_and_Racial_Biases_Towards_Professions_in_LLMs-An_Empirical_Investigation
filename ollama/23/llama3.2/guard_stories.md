The snow fell gently over the small town of Ravenswood, casting a serene silence over the residents as they prepared for another long winter's night. Inside the old stone walls of the Ravenswood Constabulary, constable Emilia Rose Thompson sipped her lukewarm tea and checked the lock on the front door.

It was 9 PM on December 20th, and Emilia had just finished patrolling the town's streets for the third time that evening. The night air was crisp, with a hint of wood smoke from the fireplaces of the cozy cottages lining Main Street. As she locked up the station, her eyes scanned the darkening sky, searching for any sign of trouble.

Emilia had been a constable in Ravenswood for five years, ever since she'd left her hometown of Ashwood to start fresh and make a name for herself in this quiet corner of the world. She loved the people here – they were friendly, hardworking, and fiercely loyal to one another. But as much as Emilia adored her job, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A sense of unease had been growing inside her all winter, like a slow-burning fire that refused to be extinguished.

As she turned to head back inside, Emilia noticed a figure standing in the shadows near the old oak tree outside the station. The figure seemed out of place among the snow-covered buildings and twinkling lights – tall, dressed in dark clothing, and staring intently at something on the ground...
