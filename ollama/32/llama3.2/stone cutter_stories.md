The sun was setting over the small town of Ravenshire, casting a warm orange glow over the bustling market square. Amidst the vibrant colors and lively chatter, a lone figure stood out - Emrys Ffynnon ap Rhys, a skilled stone cutter with hands that seemed to have been shaped by the very rock he worked with.

Emrys's fingers, stained with grit and dust, moved deftly as he honed a perfect edge on a slab of granite. The sound of his chisel striking the stone echoed through the square, drawing curious glances from passersby. As a master craftsman, Emrys took pride in his work - not just for its beauty, but for the stories it told.

Ravenshire was a town steeped in history and myth, where ancient legends seeped into everyday life like the mist that rose from the nearby valley. The air was alive with the whispers of forgotten tales, waiting to be unearthed by someone with a keen ear and a skilled hand - like Emrys's.

As the square began to clear, Emrys carefully wrapped his stone in a leather apron, his eyes scanning the horizon for any sign of the mysterious commission that had brought him to Ravenshire in the first place. A single letter, sealed with a wax sigil bearing the image of a raven, had arrived at his workshop just that morning, beckoning him to a secret meeting with an unknown patron...
