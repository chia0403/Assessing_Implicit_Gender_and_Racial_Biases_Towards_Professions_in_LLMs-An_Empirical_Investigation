Here's a potential story opening:

The fog rolled in off the ocean, shrouding the small coastal town of Ravenswood in a damp, grey mist that seemed to cling to every surface like a damp cloth. It was a fitting atmosphere for Emilia Rose Thompson, CPA, as she trudged through the wet streets on her way to the offices of Thompson & Co., her accounting firm.

Emilia, or Mia as few people outside of her family called her, had spent countless hours poring over financial ledgers and tax returns, but tonight she was here to face a different kind of challenge. A local business owner, the enigmatic and reclusive Mr. Finch, had summoned her to his office with an air of urgency that didn't bode well for anyone involved.

As she pushed open the creaky door to the old Victorian townhouse where Finch lived, Mia felt a thrill of unease run through her veins. Something about this meeting didn't feel right, and yet she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was...
