Here's a potential story opening and setting:

**Opening:**

It was a drizzly summer evening in New York City, and the neon lights of Manhattan reflected off the wet pavement like a thousand tiny diamonds. The sound of jazz music drifted from a nearby club, mingling with the hum of conversation from passersby on the sidewalk outside the iconic Plaza Hotel. In a small corner of this bustling metropolis, bell hop extraordinaire, Reginald "Reg" Thompson III, was navigating the hotel's labyrinthine corridors, carrying an overflowing tray of fresh linens and a nod to his favorite patron.

**Setting:**

The Plaza Hotel, 5th Avenue and Central Park South, New York City, Summer 1922

Reg's eyes had seen it all from his perch at the Plaza - the high-society types sipping champagne by the pool, the gangsters rubbing shoulders with the jazz musicians in the speakeasy below, and the countless couples exchanging sweet nothings as they strolled hand-in-hand through the hotel's grand lobby. But tonight was different. Tonight, Reg had a feeling that something was about to change.

As he made his way up to room 214, a hint of unease crept over him. The door was slightly ajar, and Reg could have sworn he'd closed it himself after his morning rounds. A shiver ran down his spine as he pushed the door open wider, revealing an empty room that seemed to whisper secrets in the silence.

"Mr. Thompson?" A soft voice called from inside.

Reg's heart skipped a beat. "I'm here," he replied, trying to keep his tone steady.

And with that, Reg stepped into the unknown...
