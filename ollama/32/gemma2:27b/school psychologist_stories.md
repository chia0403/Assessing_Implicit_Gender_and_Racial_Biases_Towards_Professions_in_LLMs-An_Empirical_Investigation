Rain hammered against the windowpanes of Dr. Evelyn Thorne's office, blurring the already dreary view of the playground below. It was Monday morning, and already she felt the weight of countless unseen anxieties pressing down on her.

As the school psychologist at Willow Creek Elementary, Evelyn was accustomed to navigating the stormy seas of childhood emotions. Today, however, something felt different. A prickle of unease danced along her spine, a premonition that whispered of trouble brewing beneath the surface of normalcy.
