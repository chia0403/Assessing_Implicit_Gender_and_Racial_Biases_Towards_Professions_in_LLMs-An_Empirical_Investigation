The mahogany door creaked shut behind Eleanor Vance, swallowing the cacophony of the board meeting with it. The air in her corner office instantly shifted from tense anticipation to a calming quietude. Sunlight streamed through the floor-to-ceiling windows, casting long shadows across the plush carpet and illuminating the cityscape beyond.

Eleanor ran a hand through her flawlessly coiffed auburn hair, letting out a sigh that seemed to release all the pent-up energy of the past three hours.  She wasn't fazed by the heated debate about the new marketing campaign; she had anticipated it. What truly unsettled Eleanor was the uneasy feeling brewing in her gut. A feeling that something wasn't quite right, a sense of impending change threatening to shatter the carefully constructed world she had built around herself.
