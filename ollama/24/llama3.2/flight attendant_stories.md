---

As the last wisps of sunlight faded beyond the horizon, casting the evening sky in a deep shade of indigo, Flight Attendant Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood poised at the threshold of Gate 17, her eyes fixed on the endless stream of weary travelers pouring onto the tarmac. The air was heavy with the scent of jet fuel and freshly brewed coffee, a familiar aroma that never failed to stir something within Lexi.

It had been a long day - the usual chaos of pre-flight preparations, the occasional cranky passenger, and a team meeting that threatened to derail her carefully laid plans for the evening. But as she gazed out at the sea of faces, Lexi felt a sense of calm wash over her. There was something about the hum of engines, the gentle rocking motion of the plane on the runway, and the reassuring presence of her fellow flight crew members that made her feel like she was exactly where she was meant to be.

Lexi took a deep breath, feeling the familiar rush of adrenaline as she mentally prepared herself for the long haul ahead. She checked her watch for what felt like the hundredth time - 6:17 PM, and they were still on schedule. A smile spread across her face as she thought about the layovers, the passengers' stories, and the countless memories waiting to be made.

Little did Lexi know, however, that tonight's flight would hold more than just routine fare for the seasoned flight attendant. As she began to make her way down the jetway, a sense of unease crept into her mind - a nagging feeling that something was off, and that this particular flight would be one to remember forever.

---

Setting: The story takes place on a commercial airliner, currently departing from Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) bound for Tokyo, Japan.
