Here's a potential story opening and setting:

**Opening**

It was a drizzly autumn evening in Seattle, the kind that made you want to curl up with a good book and a warm cup of coffee. But for Private Investigator Julian Alexander "Jules" Thompson, there was no such luxury. He stood outside his office, a small, cramped room above a used bookstore on 2nd Avenue, and lit a cigarette as he surveyed the city's neon-lit underbelly.

**Setting**

Seattle in the late 1990s was a city of contrasts. The gleaming glass towers of downtown Seattle pierced the clouds, while the rain-soaked streets of Pioneer Square were lined with crumbling warehouses and rundown apartment buildings. Jules knew every nook and cranny, every hidden alleyway and backstreet bar, like the back of his hand. He had grown up on these streets, the son of a retired cop who had taught him everything he knew about people and their secrets.

Jules' office was a reflection of his background - cluttered with files, dusty old maps, and a few well-worn detective novels that he'd read so many times they were dog-eared. The air was thick with the smell of stale cigarettes and yesterday's coffee. But despite its rough exterior, Jules' office held a strange allure, like a magnet for those who needed to keep their secrets hidden.

And right now, someone needed Jules' help...
