And when she glanced out at the sky, she could have sworn she saw a shape - a fleeting glimpse of something that looked almost human.

Maya's heart skipped a beat as she quickened her pace, telling herself it was just her imagination playing tricks on her. She had lived in Willow Creek all her life, and she knew its rhythms and moods better than anyone. But today, something felt different.
