The sun had just begun to set over the bustling streets of Tokyo's Shinjuku district, casting a warm orange glow over the neon-lit skyscrapers and crowded alleys. Amidst the chaos, Emiko Nakahara carefully polished the last nail on her latest client's hand, her eyes scanning the small, eclectic salon she had inherited from her grandmother.

The sign above the door read " Sakura Beauty Parlor" in elegant kanji characters, a nod to the cherry blossom trees that bloomed in Japan every spring. Inside, the air was thick with the scent of lotions and oils, and the soft hum of classical Japanese music floated through the ventilation system. Emiko's clients adored her attention to detail and soothing touch, and she took pride in creating a tranquil oasis amidst the city's frenetic pace.

As the evening rush subsided, Emiko began to prep for the next day's appointments, restocking supplies and tidying up the cluttered salon. But as she turned to hang up her apron, her gaze landed on an unexpected delivery: a mysterious package with no return address, wrapped in plain white paper and tied with a faded black ribbon.

A shiver ran down Emiko's spine as she picked up the package, feeling an inexplicable sense of trepidation. Who could have sent this, and what was inside?
