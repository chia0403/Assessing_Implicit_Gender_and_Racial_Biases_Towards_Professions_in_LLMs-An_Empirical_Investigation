The fluorescent lights overhead cast an eerie glow over the abandoned classrooms, the once-vibrant paint now chipped and faded like the dreams that lingered in the air. Amidst the dusty remnants of a bygone era, Emilia Rodriguez pushed her cart filled with cleaning supplies through the empty hallways, her eyes scanning the familiar landscape with a mix of nostalgia and melancholy.

As a janitor at Springdale High School, Emilia had spent countless hours tidying up after students who seemed more interested in their social media feeds than their studies. She took pride in her work, knowing that her diligence kept the school's corridors from descending into complete disarray. But tonight was different. Tonight, something felt off.

The usually deserted halls were eerily still, as if the very students and teachers had vanished, leaving behind only the faint scent of last week's cafeteria food and the echoes of forgotten memories. Emilia shivered, despite the warmth of the summer evening, and quickened her pace, her eyes fixed on the schedule pinned to the staff room door.

According to the clock, she should be done with her rounds by now. But as she approached the door marked "Principal's Office," a sudden sense of unease crept over her, making her hesitate. What was waiting for her inside?
