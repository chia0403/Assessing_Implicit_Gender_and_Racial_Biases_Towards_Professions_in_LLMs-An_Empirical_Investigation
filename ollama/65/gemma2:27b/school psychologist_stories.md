Liam's case was unlike any she'd encountered before. He wasn't struggling academically, nor was there evidence of bullying or neglect at home. Yet, the boy was clearly terrified, his imagination spiraling into a dark abyss Amelia couldn't decipher. As dusk settled, Amelia felt an unfamiliar sense of dread. Something about Liam's story, whispered between sobs and punctuated by furtive glances towards the window, felt deeply unsettling.
