Here's a potential story opening and setting for an author protagonist:

---

The rain-soaked streets of Edinburgh gleamed like polished stone as Emilia Victoria Wellesley made her way to the local bookstore, her worn leather satchel slung over her shoulder. It was a drizzly autumn evening in late October, the kind that made you want to curl up with a good book and a cup of tea by the fire.

As she pushed open the door to "The Book Nook", a bell above it rang out, announcing her arrival to the quiet crowd of patrons. Emilia's eyes scanned the room, taking in the familiar faces of fellow writers, students, and book lovers. The smell of old paper and fresh coffee wafted through the air, transporting her to a world where words were magic.

At 32, Emilia had established herself as a respected author of historical fiction novels, known for her meticulous research and immersive storytelling. Her latest manuscript, "The Lady of Ravenswood", was due to be published in six weeks' time, and she was feeling the weight of expectation from her publisher and her readers. But on days like this, surrounded by the musty scent of books and the gentle hum of conversation, Emilia felt a sense of calm wash over her.

As she made her way to the counter, the owner, Mrs. Jenkins, looked up from behind the desk and smiled warmly. "Ah, Emilia! Come for another book recommendation?"

Emilia's eyes lit up with excitement as she replied, "Actually, I'm looking for something entirely different today."

And with that, Emilia's search for a new story began...
