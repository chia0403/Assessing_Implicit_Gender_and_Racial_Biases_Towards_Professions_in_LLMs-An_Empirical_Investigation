The rain poured down on the streets of Ashwood City like a dirty blanket, casting a gloomy mist over the crowded dining room of Bistro Bliss. Inside, 22-year-old Alexis "Lexi" Thompson wiped down the counter for what felt like the hundredth time that night, her mind numb from the endless stream of orders and complaints. As a server at one of the city's trendiest restaurants, Lexi was used to the high-stress environment, but tonight was taking it to a whole new level.

The Bistro Bliss crowd had come out in full force for the annual Wine and Dine gala, and Lexi was stuck dealing with rowdy patrons, fussy diners, and a kitchen that seemed to be on fire. The tension in the air was palpable, and Lexi could feel her shoulders tightening with every passing minute.

As she took a quick glance around the room, her eyes landed on a familiar face - Rachel Kim, the Bistro Bliss manager, hovering over the hostess stand like a hawk. Rachel's usually chipper demeanor had turned frosty tonight, and Lexi knew better than to ask what was bothering her. Tonight, it seemed, the night air was going to be filled with more drama than just the promise of good wine and fine dining.
