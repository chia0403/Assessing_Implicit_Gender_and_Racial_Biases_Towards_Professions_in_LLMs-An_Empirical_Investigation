The rain-soaked streets of Ravenswood were always at their most unforgiving on nights like these, when the darkness seemed to swallow everything whole. But for Emilia "Mia" Rodriguez, a skilled radio technician with a keen ear for signals and a taste for adventure, this was just another evening in her beloved hometown.

Inside the cramped, cluttered workshop of her family's repair shop, "Rodriguez Electronics," the soft glow of fluorescent lights illuminated the rows of dusty wires, blinking meters, and discarded gadgetry that lined the walls. The air was thick with the scent of solder, oil, and ozone, a familiar comfort to Mia's sensitive nose.

As she worked on a particularly stubborn AM radio, its static-filled reception crackling in her earpiece, Mia's mind wandered to the mysterious transmission she'd intercepted earlier that day - a cryptic message that had piqued her curiosity and left her with more questions than answers. Little did she know, this was just the beginning of a journey that would take her beyond the familiar streets of Ravenswood and into the very heart of the city's hidden underbelly.
