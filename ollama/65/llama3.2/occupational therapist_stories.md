The snowflakes danced outside the window of Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson's office, casting a serene silence over the small town of Willow Creek. Lexi sat at her desk, sipping her lukewarm coffee as she gazed out at the winter wonderland. Her eyes, a deep shade of brown, seemed lost in thought, her brow furrowed in concentration.

As an occupational therapist with a specialization in pediatric rehabilitation, Lexi had seen her fair share of triumphs and setbacks. But on days like this, when the snowdrifts reached the windowsills and the world outside felt hushed and still, she couldn't help but feel a sense of peace settle over her.

Lexi's office, tucked away in the corner of the children's hospital, was a cozy sanctuary filled with toys, puzzles, and the soft hum of therapy sessions. She had spent countless hours here, working with children to overcome injuries, develop new skills, and find joy in their own unique ways. And yet, despite her love for this work, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off today.

It started with a mysterious phone call from an unknown number earlier that morning. The voice on the other end had been cryptic, speaking only in hushed tones about a patient that Lexi didn't recognize. The words had sent a shiver down her spine: "They're watching you, Lexi. They want something."

Now, as she sat at her desk, sipping her cold coffee and staring out into the snowy day, Lexi couldn't help but wonder what secrets lay hidden behind the quiet streets of Willow Creek...
