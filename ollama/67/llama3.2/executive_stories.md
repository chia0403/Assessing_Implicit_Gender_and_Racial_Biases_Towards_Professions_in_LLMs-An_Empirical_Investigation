A shiver ran down her spine as she turned away from the window and walked over to her desk, her eyes scanning the room for any signs of surveillance. NovaTech's security team was always on high alert, but something about this message felt off.

With a sense of unease growing in the pit of her stomach, Emilia picked up the phone and dialed a number she hadn't touched in years. It was a call that would change everything.
