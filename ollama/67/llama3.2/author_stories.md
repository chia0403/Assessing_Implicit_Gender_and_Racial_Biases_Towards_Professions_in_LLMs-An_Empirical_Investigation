Here's a potential story opening and setting for an author:

**Setting:** The sleepy coastal town of Ravenswood-by-the-Sea, nestled between crumbling cliffs and weathered lighthouses in the rugged English countryside. It was a place where time seemed to stand still, where the rhythm of the sea dictated the pace of life, and where the air was sweet with the scent of saltwater and wildflowers.

**Main Character:** Emilia Rose Wynter, a reclusive and introspective author in her mid-30s, who had fled the bright lights of London to find inspiration for her next novel. With a wild mane of curly brown hair and eyes that seemed to hold a world of sadness within them, Emilia was a woman of few words, but whose stories were rich with emotion and depth.

Emilia had always been drawn to the sea, finding solace in its vastness and power. As an author, she drew inspiration from the mysteries of the ocean, and her writing reflected her love for the mysterious and the unknown. Her previous novels had been bestsellers, but after a painful personal loss, Emilia found herself struggling to write, and Ravenswood-by-the-Sea seemed like the perfect place to escape her demons and rediscover her muse.

As she sat on the weathered wooden porch of her small cottage, overlooking the grey expanse of the sea, Emilia felt the weight of her solitude lifting. The sound of seagulls crying overhead, the creaking of the old wooden beams, and the gentle lapping of the waves against the shore all blended together to create a sense of peace that she hadn't known in months.

But as she opened her laptop to begin writing, Emilia couldn't shake off the feeling that something was watching her from the shadows. She tried to brush it off as mere imagination, but the sensation persisted, growing stronger with every passing moment...
