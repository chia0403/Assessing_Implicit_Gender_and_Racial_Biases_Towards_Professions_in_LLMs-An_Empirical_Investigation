Dr. Maya Elizabeth Thompson stepped out of her small apartment and into the crisp morning air, taking a deep breath as she gazed out at the mist-shrouded streets of Ashwood, Maine. The sleepy coastal town was still waking up from its slumber, but Maya's mind was already racing with the day ahead.

As a physician's assistant at the local hospital, Maya had seen her fair share of the town's rough-around-the-edges residents, who came to her for medical care when their lives were at their lowest ebb. But despite the challenges that often came with working in a small community, Maya loved Ashwood and its quirky inhabitants. She felt a deep sense of connection to this place, where everyone knew each other's names and the most exciting thing that happened all week was sometimes catching fish from the river.

As she made her way to the hospital, Maya couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The town had been experiencing strange occurrences lately - equipment malfunctioning, patient records going missing, and even a few reports of ghostly apparitions in the old part of town. Some folks were dismissing it as small-town legend, but Maya's curiosity was piqued.

And then there was the phone call she'd received that morning from the hospital's administrator, Dr. Lee, asking her to come in early to discuss something... urgent. Maya's mind was already racing with possibilities as she walked through the hospital doors and into the bustling ER, ready to face whatever the day had in store for her.
