A petite, raven-haired woman with a kind smile and a talent for making people feel at ease, Naomi had spent years honing her craft as a hairstylist in Tokyo's trendy neighborhoods.

As she swept the floor and tidied up the salon, Naomi's mind wandered to the strange occurrences that had been happening in the neighborhood lately. Naomi tried not to let it bother her - she was a simple girl who liked simple things: music, hair, and the quiet rhythms of everyday life.

But as she slipped on her favorite pair of scissors and prepared for another day of cutting hair and listening to stories, Naomi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.
