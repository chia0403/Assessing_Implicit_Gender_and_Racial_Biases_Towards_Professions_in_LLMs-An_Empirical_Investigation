Outside his workshop window, rain lashed against the corrugated iron roof, drumming a relentless rhythm that mirrored the static hiss emanating from a vintage radio receiver perched precariously on a shelf.

Elias, grease smudged across his forehead and calloused fingers nimbly adjusting a miniature screwdriver, was lost in the intricate world of vacuum tubes and cathode rays. He could hear the distant rumble of thunder, but his focus remained solely on the delicate task at hand – coaxing life back into a relic from a bygone era, a Bakelite beauty with faded gold lettering proclaiming it "The Majestic Voice".
