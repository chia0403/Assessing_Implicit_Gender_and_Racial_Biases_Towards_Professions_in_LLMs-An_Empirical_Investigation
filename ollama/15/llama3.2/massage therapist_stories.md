Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The warm glow of sunset cast a golden light over the tranquil streets of Ravenswood, a charming coastal town nestled between rocky cliffs and the vast expanse of the Pacific Ocean. The sound of seagulls filled the air as people strolled along the beach, enjoying the last few minutes of daylight before the stars began to twinkle overhead.

In her small, cozy massage studio above the surf shop, Emilia Ruby Blackwood was preparing for another evening of helping clients relax and unwind. As a licensed massage therapist with a passion for healing and connection, Emilia had built a loyal following in Ravenswood, where everyone knew her name and trusted her skills.

With her fingers moving deftly over the lotion-scented oils on her counter, Emilia's thoughts turned to her own evening plans. She was meeting an old friend, Olivia, at the local café for coffee and gossip after closing time. It had been months since they'd last caught up, and Emilia couldn't wait to hear about Olivia's latest art project or share some juicy news of her own.

As she poured a calming blend of essential oils into a diffuser, Emilia felt a sense of contentment wash over her. This was her happy place – surrounded by the soothing scents and gentle hum of the massage studio, where people came to find peace and tranquility in their chaotic lives. Little did she know, tonight would bring more than just relaxation...
