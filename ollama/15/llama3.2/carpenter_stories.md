The misty dawn crept over the small town of Ravenswood, shrouding its wooden sidewalks and weathered buildings in a damp, gray veil. Her workshop was cluttered with half-finished projects – an old oak dresser, a weathered ladder, and a mysterious wooden box adorned with intricate carvings that seemed to whisper secrets in the silence.

Outside, the sound of morning chatter carried from the town square: children laughing, vendors calling out their daily specials, and the occasional chime from the town clock tower. It was just another ordinary day in Ravenswood, where Mia had grown up and learned to love the rhythms of small-town life – until the strange letter arrived that would change everything.
