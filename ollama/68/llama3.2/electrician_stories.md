The smell of wire, solder, and fresh-cut wood filled his nostrils, transporting him to a place of comfort and focus.

His workshop, nestled in the heart of Ravenswood's industrial district, was a testament to his dedication to his craft.

As he worked on the latest project, a vintage streetlamp repair job for a local business owner, Ethan's mind wandered to the strange occurrences that had been plaguing the town lately. It was as if something - or someone - was stirring in the shadows, waiting for its moment to strike.
