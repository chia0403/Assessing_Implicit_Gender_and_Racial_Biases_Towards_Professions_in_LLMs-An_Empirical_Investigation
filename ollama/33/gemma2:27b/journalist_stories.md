Rain lashed against the grimy windowpanes of The Daily Grind, mirroring the storm brewing inside Amelia "Amy" Thorne. Her fingers drummed a frantic tattoo on her battered notepad, the cheap ink smearing under the pressure. Outside, neon signs flickered in the downpour, reflecting off the slick cobblestones of Old Town, Prague.

The air hung thick with the scent of stale coffee and desperation. Amy, a seasoned investigative journalist known for her tenacious pursuit of truth, was staring down the barrel of her biggest story yet – one that could expose a decades-old conspiracy hidden in the heart of the Czech Republic. But with each passing hour, the walls seemed to close in, the weight of unanswered questions pressing down on her like a physical burden.

She had come to Prague following a trail of cryptic emails and coded messages, whispers of political corruption and silenced voices. Now, she was stuck –  her only lead, a reclusive librarian with a penchant for ancient folklore, was nowhere to be found.
