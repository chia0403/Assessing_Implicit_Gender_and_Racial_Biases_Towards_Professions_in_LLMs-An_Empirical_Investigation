As she sipped her lukewarm coffee, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson gazed out the rain-soaked window of her small office in downtown Portland. The gray sky seemed to match her mood perfectly, and she couldn't help but wonder if this was more than just a coincidence.

A paralegal at the law firm of Smith & Associates, Lexi had spent years perfecting her craft, navigating the complexities of the legal system with ease. But as much as she loved her job, it felt like Groundhog Day - same old cases, same old clients, and the same old monotony.

Tonight, however, something was different. A knock at the door broke the silence, and Lexi's eyes flicked to the clock on her computer screen. It was 8:47 PM, which meant she had a rare window of time before the office closed for the day. She picked up the phone, answered it with a practiced "Smith & Associates", and waited.

The voice on the other end was unfamiliar, but the tone sent a shiver down Lexi's spine. "This is Detective Jameson from Homicide South Division," he said, his words dripping with an air of gravity that didn't belong in a conversation at 8:47 PM. "I'm looking for you, Ms. Thompson."

Lexi's heart skipped a beat as she sat up straighter in her chair. What could the detective want with her? And why was it happening now?

She hesitated, weighing her options, before asking the question that would change everything: "What is it?"
