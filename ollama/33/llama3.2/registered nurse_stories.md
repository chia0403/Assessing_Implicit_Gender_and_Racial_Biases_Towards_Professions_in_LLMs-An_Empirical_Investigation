Registered Nurse Emily Joanne Taylor trudged through the downpour, her worn leather boots splashing in the puddles as she made her way to the local hospital. As she pushed open the door, a bell above it let out a tired clang, and Emily Joanne slipped off her raincoat, revealing a crisp white shirt with the hospital's logo emblazoned on the sleeve. She had been working at Willow Creek Hospital for five years, and in that time, she had seen her fair share of storms - medical emergencies, personal crises, and the occasional small-town drama.

The ER was already buzzing with activity, the hum of machines and the murmur of conversations creating a familiar background noise that Emily Joanne knew like the back of her hand. She took a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for the long night ahead, as she made her way to the nurse's station to review the evening's schedule.
