Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The misty dawn broke over the sleepy college town of Ashwood, casting a mysterious veil over the crumbling brick buildings and overgrown quad. Professor Elara Vex, a renowned expert in ancient languages and mythology, stood at her window, gazing out at the grey sky as if searching for something that wasn't quite there.

She was a tall, imposing figure with long, dark hair and piercing green eyes that seemed to bore into those she met. Her sharp jawline and angular features were softened by a gentle smile, which creased the corners of her mouth and made her appear approachable despite her formidable intellect. Elara's slender fingers trailed along the windowsill, as if she was perpetually poised on the cusp of a great discovery.

As a professor at Ashwood University, Elara had spent years studying the myths and legends of ancient cultures, seeking to unravel the secrets hidden within their stories. Her latest obsession was the myth of the Star-Crossed Ones, an ancient legend that spoke of a group of celestial beings who had been imprisoned by a powerful sorcerer for eternity.

The chill in the air seeped into Elara's bones as she turned away from the window and began to pace across her small office, lost in thought. Her mind was already racing ahead, conjuring up images of dusty tomes and forgotten scrolls, of crumbling temples and hidden artifacts waiting to be uncovered...
