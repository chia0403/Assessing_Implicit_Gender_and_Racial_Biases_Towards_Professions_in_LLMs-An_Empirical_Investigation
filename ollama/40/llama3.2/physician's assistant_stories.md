The misty rain poured down on the small coastal town of Tidal Cove like a veil of secrecy, shrouding its quaint streets and weathered buildings in an eerie gloom. Dr. Sophia Elena Vasquez, a compassionate and brilliant physician's assistant with a sharp mind and a quick wit, steered her small sedan onto Main Street, the wheels splashing through puddles left behind by last night's storm.

As she pulled into the parking lot of Tidal Cove Medical Center, Sophia couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The hospital was quiet, except for the soft hum of monitors and the muffled beeping of machines. She had been working the overnight shift for several nights now, and every night felt like a slow descent into unease.

Sophia grabbed her bag and stepped out into the rain, her eyes scanning the deserted parking lot as she made her way towards the hospital's entrance. The sign above the door creaked in the wind, reading "Tidal Cove Medical Center - Open 24/7" in bold letters. But Sophia knew that tonight was different. Tonight felt like a night when anything could happen.

Little did Sophia know, this was just the beginning of a night that would test her skills as a physician's assistant, push her to her limits, and challenge everything she thought she knew about her small town.
