Here's a story opening and setting:

The misty dawn broke over the small town of Ravenswood, casting a eerie gloom over the narrow streets and weathered buildings that lined its main thoroughfare. The fog swirled around the old clock tower, where it seemed to seep into every crevice and crack, like a living entity.

Inside his cramped but tidy plumbing shop on Elm Street, Jack "Hatchet" McTavish stirred from his slumber, rubbing the fatigue from his eyes. A gruff, no-nonsense plumber with a reputation for being one of the best in town, Hatchet had spent years perfecting his craft, fixing everything from leaky faucets to clogged drains for the residents of Ravenswood.

As he swung his legs over the side of the bed and planted his feet firmly on the floor, Hatchet's gaze fell upon the faded photo on his bedside table - a younger version of himself, grinning from ear to ear alongside his late father, Angus McTavish. It was a reminder that even as a plumber, Hatchet had always been drawn to the mysterious and unknown, much like the old tales of Ravenswood's cursed history.

With a sigh, Hatchet began his morning routine, preparing for another long day of unclogging pipes, fixing broken fixtures, and perhaps - just perhaps - unraveling one of the many secrets that still lingered in this enigmatic town. Little did he know, today would be different from any other day...
