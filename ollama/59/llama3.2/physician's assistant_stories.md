The rain lashed down on the small town of Ravenswood, casting a gloomy veil over its quaint Main Street. Inside the cozy office of Dr. Thompson's medical practice, Physician Assistant Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson sat at her desk, sipping lukewarm coffee and staring out the window as the storm raged on outside. Her bright blue eyes, fringed with thick lashes, seemed to hold a deep sadness, as if the weight of the world was bearing down on her.

At 28 years old, Lexi had always been driven by a desire to help others. She had earned her Master's degree in Physician Assistant Studies from Duke University and had spent the past five years honing her skills under the watchful eye of Dr. Thompson. As she finished up her charts for the day, a sense of restlessness settled over her, like the rain-soaked streets outside were seeping into her very bones.

It was then that Lexi's phone rang, shrill and insistent, shattering the stillness of the office. She picked it up, answering with a hesitant voice: "PA Thompson speaking."

But as she listened to the words on the other end of the line, her expression changed from calm to concerned, her eyes narrowing as a shiver ran down her spine...
