The sun was setting over the rugged coastline of Ravenshire, casting a warm orange glow over the small village where Emrys Ffolkeson, a skilled stone cutter, worked tirelessly in his workshop. The air was crisp and salty, filled with the scent of seaweed and the distant tang of limestone quarries that dominated the surrounding hills.

Emrys, his dark hair tied back in a practical knot, his hands stained with the residue of mortar and dust, paused from his work to gaze out at the sea. He had spent countless hours studying the unique geology of Ravenshire, learning how to coax the most beautiful, intricate patterns from the stone he worked with. It was a talent that had served him well over the years, earning him a reputation as one of the finest stone cutters in the region.

As he stood there, lost in thought, Emrys's mind wandered back to the strange package he had received earlier that day - a small, unmarked box wrapped in plain brown paper and twine, with no indication of who might have sent it or what could be inside. He had been hesitant to open it, sensing that something important was hidden beneath its unassuming exterior...
