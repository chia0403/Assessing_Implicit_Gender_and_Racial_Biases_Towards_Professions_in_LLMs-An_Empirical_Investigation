The air hung heavy with salt and seaweed, clinging to Elara's worn camera like a second skin.  She stood at the edge of the jetty, its wooden planks groaning under her weight as she adjusted the lens for the perfect shot. The setting sun cast an eerie orange glow on the churning waves, turning the harbor into a scene straight out of a forgotten dream. 

**Elara "Ellie" Davenport**, with her unruly brown curls and eyes that held the depth of a thousand stories, had spent years chasing light in this coastal town.  It wasn't just about the vibrant colors or the crashing waves; it was about capturing the soul of place. Tonight, she was determined to find something more, something tangible, before the last sliver of sun sank beneath the horizon and swallowed the world into a velvet darkness.
