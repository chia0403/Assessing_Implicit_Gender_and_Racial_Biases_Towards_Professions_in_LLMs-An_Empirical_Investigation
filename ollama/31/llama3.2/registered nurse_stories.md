The stormy night air clung to the small town of Ravenswood like a damp shroud, casting a gloomy pallor over the deserted streets. In the dimly lit hospital waiting room, Registered Nurse Emily Victoria Reynolds sat hunched over a lukewarm cup of coffee, her eyes fixed on the clock ticking away on the wall with a monotonous drone.

It was 11:45 PM, and the emergency room had just closed for the night, but Emily's shift wouldn't end until she finished reviewing the charts from the previous day's patients. She was a solo nurse that evening, covering for her colleague who had called in sick with a sudden case of food poisoning.

As she scanned the rows of patient records on her laptop, Emily's mind began to wander to the strange phone call she'd received earlier that night from an unknown number. The voice on the other end had been low and gravelly, speaking only two words: "Room 304." Suddenly, the lights in the waiting room seemed to flicker ominously, as if warning her of something lurking just out of sight.

Emily shivered, despite the warmth of the hospital's air conditioning system. She had a feeling that this night was about to get a lot more interesting than she'd bargained for.
