As she trudged through the drizzly London morning, Emily Anne Windsor couldn't help but feel like another day in the office was just around the corner. She lived for those drizzly days - they always seemed to bring out the best in her, and reminded her that even on the greyest of days, there was still beauty to be found.

Emily Anne, or "Evie" as she liked to be called by those close to her, was a hardworking secretary at the prestigious law firm of Harrington & Sons. She had worked her way up from being a bright-eyed new graduate with a degree in English Literature to become one of the most well-respected and efficient secretaries in the firm.

As she walked through the office doors, Emily Anne was greeted by the familiar sight of rows of gleaming desks and the murmur of hushed conversations. She made her way to her own desk, which overlooked the bustling streets of Piccadilly, and began to sort through the day's mail and paperwork.

The office was a large, converted townhouse in a quiet corner of Mayfair, with high ceilings, marble floors, and walls lined with books that Emily Anne had lovingly curated over the years. It was a place where she felt most at home, surrounded by the comforting familiarity of routine and responsibility.

Little did Emily Anne know, however, that today would be different from all the other days - today would bring an unexpected surprise, one that would shake her life to its very foundations...
