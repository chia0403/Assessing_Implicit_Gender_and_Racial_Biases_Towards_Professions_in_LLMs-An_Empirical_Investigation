The relentless Nevada sun beat down on Elias "Eli" Blackwood's neck as he wrestled with a stubborn section of guardrail. Sweat stung his eyes, blurring the already hazy vista of scrubland stretching towards the distant, purple-tinged mountains. The air hung thick and still, punctuated only by the insistent buzzing of cicadas and the mournful cry of a hawk circling high above.

Eli grunted, heaving one last time against the metal behemoth before it finally yielded with a groan. He wiped his brow with a calloused hand, leaving a streak of grime across his forehead. The highway stretched endlessly before him, a ribbon of asphalt snaking through the barren landscape.
