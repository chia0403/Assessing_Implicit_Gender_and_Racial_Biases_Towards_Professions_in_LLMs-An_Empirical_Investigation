Rain hammered against the leaded glass windows of Blackwood Manor, each drop echoing like a mournful drumbeat through the cavernous halls. Amelia Thistlewick sighed, her breath fogging the cold air as she surveyed the drawing-room. The fire, once roaring merrily, had dwindled to embers, casting long, skeletal shadows across the opulent furniture shrouded in dust sheets. A chill snaked down her spine, a familiar sensation within these grand but neglected walls.

For twenty years, Amelia Thistlewick had been the sole custodian of Blackwood Manor, entrusted by the eccentric Lord Blackwood with its upkeep while he travelled the world in pursuit of ancient artifacts and forgotten lore.
