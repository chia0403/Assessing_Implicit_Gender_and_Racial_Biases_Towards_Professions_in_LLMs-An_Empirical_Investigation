The stormy night air clung to the small coastal town of Ravenswood like a damp shroud, casting a gloomy shadow over the sleepy streets and homes. Inside the cramped, white-washed hospital on Oceanview Drive, Nurse Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson scrubbed in for the long shift ahead, her eyes squinting behind her mask as she surveyed the night's schedule.

Lexi, with her sharp jawline and short, dark hair, had spent the last five years honing her skills as a registered nurse at Ravenswood General Hospital. She was well-respected among her colleagues for her calm demeanor and attention to detail, qualities that had earned her the nickname "The Angel of Mercy" among patients and families alike.

As she began to prep for the overnight shift, Lexi's mind turned to her own personal demons – a nagging sense of unease that had been plaguing her since the mysterious disappearance of a young patient earlier that week. The case had gone cold, but Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off about it, and she was determined to dig deeper.

Little did she know, this stormy night would be just the beginning of a night that would test her skills, her compassion, and her very resolve as a nurse – and perhaps even challenge everything she thought she knew about Ravenswood's seemingly idyllic community.
