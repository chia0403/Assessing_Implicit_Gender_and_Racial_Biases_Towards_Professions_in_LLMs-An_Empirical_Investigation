Here's a potential story opening and setting:

As the last wisps of sunlight faded from the horizon, casting a warm orange glow over the small town of Ravenswood, Pennsylvania, Physician's Assistant Emily Anne Jenkins made her way through the bustling streets of downtown. The smell of freshly brewed coffee wafted from the local café, enticing her to pause for a moment before heading into work at the Ravenswood Memorial Hospital.

Emily, a petite woman in her mid-twenties with long, curly brown hair and bright green eyes, had always been drawn to the quiet, rural charm of Ravenswood. She had grown up here, the youngest of three siblings, and had returned after medical school to practice as a physician's assistant in her hometown.

As she walked towards the hospital, Emily felt a sense of comfort and familiarity wash over her. This was where she belonged – surrounded by the familiar faces and rhythms of small-town life. But despite her love for Ravenswood, Emily couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A sense of unease had been building in the back of her mind all week, and she couldn't help but wonder what lay ahead.

Little did she know, this was only the beginning of a journey that would take her to the very heart of Ravenswood's secrets...
