Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain-soaked streets of Ashwood City gleamed like polished onyx, reflecting the faint glow of streetlights that struggled to pierce the gloom. It was a night like any other in this forsaken industrial town, where the only sound was the mournful hum of factories and the distant thrum of the city's water treatment plant.

In her small, cluttered office above the local newspaper's newsroom, reporter Emily Victoria Wellesley poured over a stack of dusty files and faded police reports, her mind racing with the latest lead in a string of bizarre occurrences that had plagued Ashwood City for months. As she sipped cold coffee from a stained mug, her eyes scanned the headlines etched across the office's worn bulletin board: "Mysterious Abductions", "Disappearance of Local Teen", and "Ashwood's Secret History".

Emily's gaze narrowed as she thought back to her own investigation into the city's history. What secrets lay hidden beneath the surface of this seemingly ordinary town? And what was driving these strange events now?

Just then, the phone on Emily's desk jolted her out of her reverie, shrill and insistent. She picked it up, her voice firm as she said, "Wellesley, Ashwood News".

The voice on the other end was hesitant, almost afraid to speak. "Emily... it's Officer Jenkins from the department. We've got another one. Another disappearance..."
