Elliot took a deep breath, feeling the cool mist fill his lungs as he gazed out over the city's twinkling lights. But Elliot had long since grown tired of the area's pretentious vibe - for him, there was no place like home: a small, cluttered apartment above his parents' antique shop on 15th Avenue.

As he trudged through the downpour towards the bus stop, Elliot's mind began to wander to the cryptic email that had arrived in his inbox just hours before. The message was from an unknown sender, with only two words: " Project: Erebus." No further information. Just those three simple letters, sending a shiver down Elliot's spine.

With a practiced eye, he scanned the bus schedule on his phone and spotted the one that would take him to downtown Seattle. The thought of that email lingered in the back of his mind as he boarded the bus, disappearing into the throngs of commuters rushing towards their own destinations. Little did Elliot know, his life was about to take a dramatic turn - one that would lead him down a rabbit hole of conspiracy and intrigue, with Erebus lurking ominously at its center.
