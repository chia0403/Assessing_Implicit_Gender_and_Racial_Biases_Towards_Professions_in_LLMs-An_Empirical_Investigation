Here's a potential story opening and setting:

---

The rain poured down on the small town of Ravenswood like a malevolent spirit, casting a gloomy shroud over the streets and homes. In the midst of this drizzly evening, Electrician Maximilian "Max" Wynter huddled in his workshop, surrounded by tools and wiring that seemed to hum with the energy of the storm outside.

The smell of ozone and burning insulation filled Max's nostrils as he worked on a particularly tricky circuit board repair. He was known around town for his ability to fix anything electrical, from broken streetlights to malfunctioning medical equipment. But tonight, Max had a feeling that this job would be different. The client, a reclusive millionaire named Harrison Grant, had asked him to make some... unconventional repairs.

As the rain drummed against the workshop's roof, Max couldn't shake the feeling that he was being watched. He glanced around the cluttered space, his eyes adjusting to the dim light. The shadows seemed to deepen and twist in the corners, like dark sentinels guarding secrets. Suddenly, a faint creaking sound echoed from the back of the workshop, making Max's heart skip a beat.

"Who's there?" he called out, his voice firm but cautious.

The only response was the continued patter of rain outside, and an unsettling silence within the workshop...
