As a photographer, she had spent countless hours capturing the essence of Tidal Cove's rugged coastline, but it was on days like today that she felt most alive.

Aurora Wynter pushed open the creaky shutters and stepped out onto her small porch, camera slung over her shoulder. The sun was just beginning to peek above the horizon, casting a warm golden light over the village.
