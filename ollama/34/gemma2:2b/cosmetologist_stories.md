The scent of lavender and hairspray hung thick in the air, a familiar aroma that usually brought Amelia "May" Everly solace. However, today, her usual calm was being swallowed by the anxiety-inducing weight of the nearly empty waiting room at "Glam & Glow." Her grandmother's legacy, the salon May had inherited, was teetering on the precipice of collapse. 

The setting sun cast long shadows across the worn vinyl seats and the faded floral wallpaper, illuminating dust motes dancing in the air like miniature stars. Outside, the city hummed with a restless energy, but within these walls, time seemed to stand still.  May ran a calloused thumb over her reflection in the dusty mirror, her tired eyes reflecting her mounting worries about her late grandmother's secret: a hidden stash of vintage and rare hair products that promised untold magic, if only May could learn the how-to's of unlocking it.
