The wind whistled through the broken shingles like a scornful banshee, rattling the metal skeleton of  Old Man Murphy's shed. Rain hammered against its warped wooden frame, creating a symphony of drumming that only intensified the ache in Michael "Mickey" O'Malley's back. He was just about to switch from his trusty ladder to his old, creaky scaffold when a sudden gust of wind ripped off a piece of roofing paper, sending it tumbling towards him like an angry, feathered projectile.

Setting: A dilapidated waterfront town on the coast of Maine, USA

Character: Michael "Mickey" O'Malley -  a weathered roofer who has spent years navigating the harsh elements and unpredictable clientele of his small-town job.
