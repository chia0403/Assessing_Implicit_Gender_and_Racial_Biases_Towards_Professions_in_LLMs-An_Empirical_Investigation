The air hung thick with the scent of beeswax and lavender as Elias Thornweaver threaded his needle through the eye for what felt like the hundredth time. Frustration gnawed at him, a familiar beast these days. The velvet doublet he was crafting for Lord Beaumont refused to cooperate, its rich crimson fabric twisting and puckering under his deft fingers.

Elias's workshop, tucked away on a cobbled side street in the bustling heart of Atheria City, was a haven from the outside world. Sunlight streamed through the tall arched window, illuminating spools of silk thread in every shade imaginable, bolts of luxurious fabrics stacked neatly against the walls, and a half-finished mannequin draped in shimmering gold brocade. Yet, even amidst this familiar comfort, Elias couldn't shake the feeling that something was amiss.
