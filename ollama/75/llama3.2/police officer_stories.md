Detective Emilia "Mia" Thompson stepped out of her car and onto the crumbling asphalt of the deserted highway, the neon lights of the diner in the distance casting an eerie glow over the desolate landscape. It was a chilly autumn night, and the air was heavy with the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves. The only sound was the distant hum of a lone engine, fading into the darkness like a whisper.

Mia pulled her jacket tighter around her shoulders, the worn leather creaking softly as she gazed out at the abandoned stretch of road. It was a place where dreams went to die, where the hopes and aspirations of those who came through never seemed to materialize. But tonight, something felt different. Tonight, Mia had received a call that would change everything.

As she scanned the horizon, her eyes landed on a single headlight, flickering to life in the distance like a firefly on a summer night. The light was weak and erratic, casting an otherworldly glow over the surrounding landscape. Mia's gut told her something was off, but she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was.

She pulled out her phone and dialed the dispatcher, her voice firm as she asked for more information about the call they'd received just moments before. The response that came back was brief and chilling: "Possible homicide, mile marker 27. Requesting backup."
