The sidewalks were already bustling with students, their backpacks bouncing behind them as they hurried to catch the first bell. Alex took a deep breath, feeling a mix of excitement and trepidation wash over her. This was her chance to make a difference in the lives of these children, to help them overcome the challenges that lay ahead.

As she pushed open the door and stepped inside, the familiar sounds of chatter and laughter enveloped her, mingling with the hum of fluorescent lights overhead. Alex smiled to herself, feeling a sense of belonging wash over her. She was home now, at least for the next nine months or so.
