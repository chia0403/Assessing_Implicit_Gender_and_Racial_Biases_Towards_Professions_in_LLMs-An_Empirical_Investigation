Rain lashed against the windows of "Smile Bright Dental," blurring the cheery cartoon tooth on the door into an amorphous blob. Inside, Esmeralda "Esme" Ramirez sighed as she surveyed her schedule for the day. Every slot was filled, and not a single one with a pleasant patient. It seemed like every Tuesday brought a fresh wave of dental dread - from the man who swore he brushed twice daily (clearly a lie) to the teenager whose sole mission in life appeared to be destroying braces.

Esme loved her job. She genuinely enjoyed helping people feel confident about their smiles, but Tuesdays always tested her patience.
