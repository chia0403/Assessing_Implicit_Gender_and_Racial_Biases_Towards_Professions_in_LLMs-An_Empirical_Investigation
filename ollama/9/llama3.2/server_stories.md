As the sun set over the bustling streets of Portland, Oregon, Aurora "Rory" Wynter poured steaming cups of coffee for the early morning crowd at her family's beloved café, The Cozy Cupboard. With her bright blue eyes and spiky blonde hair tied back in a ponytail, Rory had spent years perfecting her craft as a server, learning the intricacies of coffee drinkology and honing her people skills to keep even the most discerning customers happy.

Rory's family had owned The Cozy Cupboard for over two decades, and she took pride in being the fourth generation to serve behind the counter. As she expertly juggled multiple orders at once, Rory's mind began to wander to the upcoming Friday night event – the annual Portland Food Festival, which was expected to draw in a crowd of thousands and put The Cozy Cupboard on the map. Little did she know, this particular evening would bring more than just a surge in customers...
