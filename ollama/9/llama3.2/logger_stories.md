The misty dawn broke over the small town of Raven's Peak, nestled deep in the heart of the Pacific Northwest's ancient forests. The air was crisp and cool, carrying the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves. In a secluded clearing surrounded by towering fir trees, logger and outdoorsman, Ephraim "Eph" Wystan Jensen, stood at the helm of his trusty old logging truck, sipping a steaming cup of coffee.

Eph, with his rugged good looks and worn denim overalls, had spent most of his life living off the land. He was the son of a logging family, and for as long as he could remember, the sound of chainsaws and the smell of freshly cut wood had been his constant companions. As he gazed out at the forest, Eph felt a sense of restlessness wash over him - a feeling that there was more to life than the routine of logging and living in this small town.

As he finished his coffee, Eph's eyes scanned the horizon, taking in the rows of neatly arranged logs, waiting to be transported down the winding mountain roads. He let out a deep sigh, knowing that another day of hard labor lay ahead - but also sensing that something was about to change. Little did he know, a mysterious stranger would soon arrive in Raven's Peak, setting off a chain of events that would shake the very foundations of his life forever.
