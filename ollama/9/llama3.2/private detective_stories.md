The rain-soaked streets of Seattle were always at their most unforgiving on nights like these. The darkness seemed to swallow everything whole, leaving only the faint glow of neon signs and the distant hum of the city's nightlife as reminders that even in the depths of winter, life went on.

In a cramped, smoke-filled office nestled between a vintage bookstore and a Korean BBQ joint, private detective August Ryder stared out into the stormy night, his eyes narrowed against the driving rain. A haze of cigarette smoke curled around him like a shroud, a habit he'd long given up but couldn't shake.

August's latest case had just landed on his desk, an enigmatic package from an unknown client with only one message: "Meet me at Pike Place Market at midnight. Come alone." The note was unsigned, but the single word that followed seemed to sear itself into August's mind - "Evelyn."

Little did he know, tonight would be just the beginning of a journey that would take him down the darkest alleys of Seattle's underbelly and force him to confront secrets he thought were long buried.
