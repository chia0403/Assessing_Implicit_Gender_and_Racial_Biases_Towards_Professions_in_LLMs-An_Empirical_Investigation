As she scrubbed the last remnants of tartar from Mrs. Johnson's teeth, Emily Anne Thompson couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. It wasn't just the usual complaints about sensitive gums or pesky food particles - it was a nagging sense of unease that had been growing inside her for weeks.

The sun-drenched dental office was quiet on this Wednesday morning, except for the soft hum of the drills and the muffled chatter of the occasional patient. Emily Anne's gaze drifted out the window, where the misty dawn light struggled to penetrate the dense fog that shrouded the city. The street below seemed deserted, save for a lone figure standing outside her office building - a tall, imposing man with piercing blue eyes and jet-black hair.

Emily Anne felt a flutter in her chest as she recognized the stranger from her dreams. It had been three years since her younger brother, Max, went missing during a solo hiking trip in the mountains. The police had given up on the case, but Emily Anne's mind refused to let go of the hope that one day, he'd walk through that door, and everything would be okay again.

With a deep breath, she refocused on her patient and smiled warmly at Mrs. Johnson, who was now smiling back at her from beneath a fresh layer of teeth cleaning. Little did Emily Anne know, this ordinary morning in the dental office was about to become anything but ordinary...
