The rain poured down on the small town of Willow Creek like a relentless curtain of grey, casting a gloomy pallor over the quaint Main Street. Inside the local office building, where Elmwood & Co. had been housed for decades, Receptionist Emily Mae Thompson sat hunched over her desk, staring blankly at the clock on the wall as it ticked away with agonizing slowness.

Emily, a petite woman in her mid-twenties with a messy bob of curly brown hair and a scattering of freckles across her nose, had been working at Elmwood & Co. for nearly five years. She was a fixture behind the front desk, answering phones, scheduling appointments, and generally keeping the office running smoothly. But as she gazed out the rain-soaked window, Emily's mind began to wander from the mundane routine of her day job.

A sense of restlessness had been growing inside her for weeks, ever since the arrival of a new client - a mysterious woman with piercing green eyes who seemed to be hiding secrets behind her enigmatic smile. Emily's curiosity was piqued, and she couldn't shake the feeling that there was more to this newcomer than met the eye...
