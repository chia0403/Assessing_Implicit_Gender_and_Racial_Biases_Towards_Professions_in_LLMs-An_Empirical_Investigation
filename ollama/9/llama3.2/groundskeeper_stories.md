The sun was setting over the sprawling estate of Ravenswood Manor, casting a golden glow over the meticulously manicured lawns and perfectly trimmed hedges. Amidst the tranquil atmosphere, Emilia Rose Fletcher moved with precision, her worn gloves protecting her hands as she expertly pruned the last of the autumnal roses.

As the groundskeeper for Ravenswood Manor, Emilia had spent countless hours tending to the estate's gardens, a task she took great pride in. She knew every nook and cranny, every hidden spot where a stray weed might try to make itself at home. It was a job that required patience, attention to detail, and a deep love for the natural world.

The air was crisp and cool, carrying the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves. Emilia breathed it in deeply, feeling a sense of contentment wash over her. This was her domain, and she was its guardian. Little did she know, however, that tonight would bring more than just the usual evening routine...
