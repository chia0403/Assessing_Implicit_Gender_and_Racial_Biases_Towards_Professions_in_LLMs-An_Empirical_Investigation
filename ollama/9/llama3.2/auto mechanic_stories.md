---

The sun had barely cracked the horizon over the dusty desert town of Red Rock, Arizona, casting a warm orange glow over the crumbling adobe buildings and faded neon signs. In a small, cluttered shop on Main Street, a figure emerged from the early morning shadows. It was Axel "Ace" Thompson, a ruggedly handsome 35-year-old auto mechanic with a penchant for fixing anything with wheels.

Ace lived and breathed engines, his hands stained with grease and oil from years of tinkering under the hood of every make and model that had ever crossed his path. His shop, Thompson's Automotive Repair, was the go-to destination for Red Rock's residents when their cars broke down or just needed a good tune-up. With his trusty toolbox by his side and a cup of black coffee in hand, Ace set out to tackle the day's challenges, ready to face whatever mechanical mayhem came his way.

Little did Ace know, however, that today would be different from any other day at the shop. A mysterious stranger was about to walk into his life, one who would challenge everything he thought he knew about Red Rock and his own place in the world...
