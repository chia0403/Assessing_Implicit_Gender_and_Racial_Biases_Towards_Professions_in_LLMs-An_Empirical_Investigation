The aroma of vanilla bean and caramelized sugar hung thick in the air, a comforting shroud against the chill creeping through the bakery windows. Rain lashed against the glass, blurring the neon glow of "Sweet Surrender" across the street. Amelia Rose Carrington sighed, her breath fogging the cool surface as she surveyed her latest creation: a three-tiered masterpiece destined for a society wedding.

Snowflakes dusted the delicate sugar roses adorning its buttercream slopes, each petal painstakingly crafted and hand-painted. The ivory fondant shimmered under the bakery's warm lights, but Amelia couldn't shake the feeling that something was missing.
