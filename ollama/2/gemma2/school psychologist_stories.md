The fluorescent lights hummed above Amelia Grace Hawthorne's head, casting a sterile glow on the faded floral wallpaper in her office at Willow Creek High. A faint scent of burnt popcorn lingered in the air, a remnant of yesterday's impromptu movie night for underachieving seniors held in the common room.  Amelia sighed, smoothing down the wrinkles on her navy blazer as she reviewed Sarah Miller's file.  Sarah, a sophomore with eyes like startled deer and an affinity for disappearing into daydreams, was scheduled to meet Amelia in ten minutes. This wasn't the first time Sarah had been referred, the school counsellor suspecting something deeper than just teenage angst fueling her withdrawal. But Amelia had learned that beneath every child's facade, there were stories waiting to be heard.  And Willow Creek High, nestled in a quiet corner of suburban Ohio, was a town brimming with untold stories.
