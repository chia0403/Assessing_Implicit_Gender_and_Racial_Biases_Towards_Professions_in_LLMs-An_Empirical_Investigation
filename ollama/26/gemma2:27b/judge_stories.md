Rain lashed against the leaded glass windows of Judge Amelia Blackwood's chambers, mirroring the storm brewing inside her. The mahogany desk, usually polished to a gleam, was cluttered with case files, each one representing a life hanging in the balance.

Amelia, her silver hair pulled back in a severe bun and her emerald green eyes filled with weary determination, stared out at the grimy streets of Newhaven. This city, once a beacon of industry, now struggled under the weight of poverty and corruption. Amelia had spent her career fighting for justice within its crumbling walls, but tonight, doubt gnawed at her. The case before her, the trial of young Gabriel Thorne accused of arson, threatened to shatter everything she believed in.
