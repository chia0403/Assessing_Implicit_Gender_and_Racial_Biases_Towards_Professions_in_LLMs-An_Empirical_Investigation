---

The city lights twinkled like stars on the horizon as Regina Evelyn Thompson stepped out of her sleek black sedan and onto the dewy grass of the exclusive Oakwood Estates compound. The crisp night air carried the sweet scent of blooming flowers and the distant hum of the city's nightlife, a reminder that even in this secluded world, the world outside was never far away.

As the CEO of Thompson Industries, Regina was no stranger to power and prestige. Her sharp mind, razor-sharp instincts, and calculated charm had propelled her to the top of the corporate ladder, where she now reigned supreme over a vast empire of tech, finance, and real estate.

But tonight, Regina's thoughts were not on boardrooms or profits, but on the mysterious letter that had arrived at her mansion earlier that day. The words on the page still danced in her mind like fireflies: "Meet me at Oakwood Estates at midnight. Come alone."

Regina's fingers drummed a staccato beat on the car door as she gazed out into the darkness, her eyes narrowing against the stars. Who could have sent this letter? And what did they want from her?

As she took a deep breath and turned to head towards the grand entrance of her estate, a flutter in her chest betrayed a spark of curiosity that had long been dormant beneath her polished exterior.

Little did Regina know, tonight was just the beginning of a journey that would shake her world to its foundations and challenge everything she thought she knew about power, loyalty, and the true cost of success.
