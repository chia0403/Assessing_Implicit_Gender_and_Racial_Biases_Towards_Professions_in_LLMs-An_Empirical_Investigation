The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quiet streets and homes. Inside the cozy community center where Emilia Rose Thompson worked as a part-time childcare worker, the sound of children's laughter and playful chatter filled the air. Emilia, with her bright pink hair tied up in a ponytail and a worn denim jacket slung over her shoulder, was busy preparing for the evening's activities - a game night with the kids. As she poured glasses of juice and handed out snacks, she couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over her. It had been a long week, filled with meetings and paperwork, but moments like these made all the stress worth it. She loved watching the children grow and learn, and being a part of their lives was a privilege she cherished deeply.

As the evening wore on and the kids settled in for games of Simon Says and Red Light, Green Light, Emilia's mind began to wander back to the strange phone call she'd received that morning from an unknown number. The voice on the other end had been low and gravelly, with a hint of menace in its tone. "Meet me at the old oak tree at midnight if you want to know what really happened to Sarah," it had said, before hanging up without another word. Emilia's gut had told her to ignore it - she was just a childcare worker, after all - but now, as she watched the children play and laugh together, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off...
