Here's a story opening and setting for you:

In the quaint town of Ashwood, nestled in the rolling hills of rural England, where the misty dawn often shrouded the thatched roofs of the ancient cottages, and the scent of damp earth and wildflowers wafted through the air. It was here that Emilia Rose Fothergill, a skilled dress maker with a passion for creating exquisite gowns, lived and breathed her craft.

Emilia's small workshop, tucked away on High Street, was a treasure trove of fabrics, threads, and buttons, each one carefully selected to bring the visions in her mind to life. The sunlight streaming through the window highlighted the intricate stitches on the garments that lined the shelves, like a testament to Emilia's dedication to her art.

As she worked on a delicate lace wedding gown, the soft hum of the sewing machine and the rustle of fabric against fabric provided a soothing soundtrack to the morning. The year was 1915, and the world outside seemed to be changing at an alarming rate. But in Emilia's world, time stood still, and the only sound was the rhythm of her needles as she brought beauty and joy to those who wore her creations.

And yet, amidst the tranquility of her workshop, a sense of unease began to stir, like a whisper in the silence. For Emilia had received an unexpected visit from a stranger the previous evening, one who left behind more questions than answers...
