Smoke clawed at Captain Elias Blackwood's throat, acrid and stinging.  He squinted through the haze, searching for any sign of movement beyond the flickering orange dance. The abandoned warehouse groaned under the inferno raging within, its rusted metal bones threatening to buckle at any moment. 

Elias gripped his axe tighter, knuckles white against the worn wood. This wasn't just another routine blaze. There was something different about this fire, a ferocity that gnawed at him like a hungry beast. He couldn't shake the feeling that they weren't alone in the inferno.
