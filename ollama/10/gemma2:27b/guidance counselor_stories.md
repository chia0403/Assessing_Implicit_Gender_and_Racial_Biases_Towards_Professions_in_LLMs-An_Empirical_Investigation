The air hung heavy with the scent of stale coffee and teenage angst. Eleanor "Ellie" Vance sighed, pushing a stray curl behind her ear as she surveyed her domain: the cramped, fluorescent-lit office that doubled as her sanctuary and her battlefield. Posters adorned the walls, offering platitudes like "You Can Achieve Anything!" and "Follow Your Dreams!", starkly contrasting with the anxieties etched on the faces of her students who shuffled in and out. Today was college application deadline day, and Ellie felt the weight of a thousand futures pressing down on her. 

A knock echoed through the office, pulling Ellie from her reverie. She straightened her posture, plastering on her best "I'm here to help" smile as she called out, "Come in!"
