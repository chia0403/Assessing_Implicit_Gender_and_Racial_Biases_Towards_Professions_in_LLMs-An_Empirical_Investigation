The rain poured down on the city like a dirty blanket, shrouding the towering skyscrapers of downtown New Haven in a misty grey gloom. Inside the high-rise office building where Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson worked as a systems analyst, the fluorescent lights hummed overhead, casting an unforgiving glare over the rows of cubicles.

Lexi sat hunched over her computer, her dark hair pulled back into a tight ponytail and her bright green eyes fixed intently on the lines of code scrolling across her screen. She was in the middle of a late-night deadline, trying to iron out a pesky bug that had been plaguing her team's new database system.

As she typed away, Lexi's mind wandered to the cryptic message she'd received earlier that day - a mysterious email with only two words: "Project Echelon." She'd tried to brush it off as spam, but the phrase lingered in the back of her mind like a whisper, making her skin prickle with unease.

Just then, her phone buzzed on her desk. Lexi's heart skipped a beat as she saw the name on the screen: an unknown number, with no message or caller ID to speak of. She hesitated for a moment before answering, her fingers hovering over the receiver like a nervous bird waiting to take flight.

"Hello?"
