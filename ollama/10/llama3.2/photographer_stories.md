The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. It was a sight that Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a renowned landscape photographer, had grown to love after years of calling this place home.

Lexi stood at the edge of the lake, her camera slung over her shoulder, as she gazed out at the breathtaking view before her. The water's edge lapped gently against the shore, creating a soothing melody that seemed to match the rhythm of her heart. She took a deep breath, feeling the cool evening air fill her lungs, and began to frame her shot.

As she worked, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. It wasn't just the sense of unease that had been growing inside her for weeks, but also the nagging feeling that this particular moment, this perfect sunset, was somehow... different. She couldn't quite put her finger on it, but the hairs on the back of her neck stood on end as she clicked the shutter, capturing the fleeting beauty of the scene.

Little did Lexi know, this ordinary evening would soon become anything but ordinary...
