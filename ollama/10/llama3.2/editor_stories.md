Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain-soaked streets of Edinburgh glistened like polished onyx as Emilia Faye MacLeod stepped out of her small flat in the Old Town, the chill of the Scottish evening biting at her nose. She wrapped her arms around herself, feeling the familiar weight of her worn leather jacket and the comforting familiarity of her own footsteps on the familiar pavement.

As an editor for a small publishing house, Emilia spent most of her days holed up in the cozy offices above a vintage bookshop on George Street. But tonight, she had managed to wriggle free from the constraints of deadlines and manuscript submissions, and was treating herself to a rare night out.

With a sigh of contentment, Emilia pushed open the door to The Mysterious Bookshop, a labyrinthine haven filled with dusty tomes and whispered secrets. The scent of old paper and leather wafted out into the street, drawing her in like a magnet. She smiled to herself as she slipped inside, feeling the soft rush of excitement that came with losing herself in the musty pages of another author's world.

Little did Emilia know, this night would be different from any other. A mysterious package had arrived at the bookshop earlier that evening, containing a manuscript that would change her life forever...
