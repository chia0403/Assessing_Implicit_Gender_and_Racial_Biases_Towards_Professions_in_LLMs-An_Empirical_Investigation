As the first light of dawn crept over the sleepy town of Willow Creek, Ember Rose Wynter stirred to life in her cozy flower shop, Petal & Thread. The scent of freshly cut blooms wafted through the air, mingling with the sweet tang of coffee and the soft hum of early morning chatter from the shop's customers. It was a chilly spring morning, the kind that made you want to wrap yourself in layers and savor every moment outside.

Ember, with her wild tangle of curly brown hair and freckled nose, smiled as she watched the sun rise over the rooftops, casting a golden glow over the quiet streets. She loved days like this, when the world felt fresh and full of possibility. As the florist at Petal & Thread, Ember spent her days coaxing beauty from bouquets of blooms and crafting arrangements that told stories of their own.

Just as she was about to unlock the shop door for another day of work, a faint knock on the glass pane caught her eye. It was Mrs. Jenkins, a regular customer who came in every Wednesday morning at precisely 7:00 AM sharp. Ember smiled, knowing that this was going to be one of those days...
