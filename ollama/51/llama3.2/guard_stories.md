The mist-shrouded streets of New Haven were always at their most unforgiving at dawn, when the darkness of night clung to every corner like a damp, cold shroud. It was just such an hour that Constable Emilia Grey found herself on patrol, her lantern casting eerie shadows on the wet cobblestones as she made her way through the deserted alleys.

As a seasoned guard in the city watch, Emilia had seen her share of crime and corruption, but there was something about this particular morning that felt off. Perhaps it was the lingering scent of smoke from last night's fire, or the nervous energy that seemed to hum through the air like an electrical charge. Whatever it was, Emilia knew that she was ready for whatever lay ahead.

Her eyes scanned the rooftops and doorways, her hand resting on the grip of her truncheon as she moved with a practiced ease that belied the weight of her responsibilities. She had seen it all before - the poverty, the desperation, the petty crimes that seemed to plague every city - but there was something about this morning that made her feel like anything could happen at any moment.

And then, just as she turned a corner onto Raven's Peak Street, Emilia heard it: a faint rustling sound, followed by the soft creak of leather and the muffled thud of footsteps. Her heart quickened in response, her senses on high alert as she readied herself for whatever was about to unfold...
