Sunlight streamed through the floor-to-ceiling windows of "Beatbox Fitness," illuminating the mirrored walls that reflected a kaleidoscope of toned limbs and determined faces. Amelia, her braids coiled tight against her head like springs, stood at the front of the studio, her voice ringing out with infectious energy. 

"Alright everyone, let's get those heart rates pumping! Ten more reps of these squats, feel the burn!"
