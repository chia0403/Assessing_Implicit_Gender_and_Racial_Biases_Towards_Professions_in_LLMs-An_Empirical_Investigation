Perhaps it was the faint scent of smoke wafting through the air, or the way the flickering candles seemed to cast eerie shadows on the walls.

Amelia took a deep breath, steeling herself for the long night ahead. She had just enough time to tidy up the living areas and prepare for the guests arriving in a few hours, before she could take her well-deserved leave. Little did she know that tonight would be one of the most eventful nights at Oakwood Manor.
