The fluorescent lights overhead cast an eerie glow over the deserted corridors of Ravenswood High School. The once-thriving halls were now eerily silent, save for the soft hum of the heating system and the occasional creak of a worn floorboard. It was 2:47 AM on a Tuesday morning, and the only sound that seemed to pulse with life came from the janitor's cart, rumbling down the hallway as it carried its cargo of cleaning supplies.

Avery Wynter Jenkins, or "AJ" to those who knew him, pushed the cart with a practiced ease that belied the quiet desperation in his eyes. He had been working at Ravenswood High for over five years, and despite being one of the most diligent janitors in the school's history, he couldn't shake the feeling that he was invisible. AJ went about his rounds with a methodical attention to detail, sweeping and mopping the floors, disinfecting the bathrooms, and emptying the trash cans. But as the night wore on, and the darkness seemed to press in around him, AJ's thoughts began to wander to the rumors that had been circulating around school lately.

Rumors of a mysterious figure, known only as "The Phantom," who was said to be haunting the halls of Ravenswood High. Some said it was a ghost, while others claimed it was a former student or teacher who had died under mysterious circumstances. AJ didn't know what to believe, but he did know that he felt like he was being watched, and the thought sent a shiver down his spine.

As AJ turned a corner onto his final route of the night, he caught a glimpse of something out of the corner of his eye. A figure, tall and imposing, standing just beyond the doorway to the old auditorium. For a moment, their eyes locked, and AJ felt like he was staring into the very soul of Ravenswood High itself.

And then, in an instant, the figure vanished.
