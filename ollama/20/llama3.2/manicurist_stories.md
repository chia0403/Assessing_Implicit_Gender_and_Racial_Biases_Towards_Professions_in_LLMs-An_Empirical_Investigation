It was a drizzly autumn evening in Portland, Oregon, and the streets of Hawthorne Boulevard were bathed in a warm, golden light. The scent of freshly brewed coffee wafted from the cafes on 42nd Avenue, mingling with the sweet aroma of blooming flowers in the parklets that dotted the sidewalk.

Inside the cozy nail salon, Polish & Co., manicurist Emilia Kowalska was preparing for another busy night ahead. She wiped down the counter with a damp cloth, her bright blue eyes scanning the room as she mentally ticked off the appointments scheduled for the evening: Sarah's birthday party, Rachel's wedding rehearsal, and a late-night appointment for a client celebrating a milestone anniversary.

As she worked, Emilia hummed along to the indie folk playlist that filled the salon, her slender fingers moving deftly over the nail polish bottles and scissor-sharp clippers. Her dark hair was tied back in a neat ponytail, revealing a scattering of freckles across her nose and cheeks. She was a fixture on Hawthorne Boulevard, known for her impeccable style and attention to detail – but also for her quiet intensity and piercing green eyes that seemed to see right through you.

Tonight was no exception. With the evening's bookings already underway, Emilia felt a thrill of excitement building in her chest. It was going to be one of those nights where she got to make all the little magic happen – one nail polish color at a time. Little did she know, however, that tonight would bring more than just manicures and gossip...
