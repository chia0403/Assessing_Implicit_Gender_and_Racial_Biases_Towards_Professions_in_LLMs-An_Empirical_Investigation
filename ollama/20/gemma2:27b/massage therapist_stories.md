Rain lashed against the windows of Serenity Spa, blurring the fairy lights strung across the courtyard into shimmering halos.  Inside, Elara Solstice adjusted the lavender-scented candle on her table, its flame flickering in the dim light. Her hands, calloused yet gentle, kneaded a mound of warm aromatherapy oil, the scent of bergamot and rosemary filling the air. Elara was known for her intuitive touch, her ability to melt away tension with a blend of Swedish techniques and deep tissue massage. But tonight, a shiver ran down her spine, unrelated to the storm raging outside. 

A new client had just arrived, shrouded in a heavy raincoat, his face obscured by the shadows of the hood. Elara couldn't shake the feeling that this wasn't just any ordinary appointment...
