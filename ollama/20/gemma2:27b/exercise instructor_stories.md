The humid Miami air hung heavy, thick with the scent of salt and sunscreen. Palm trees swayed listlessly in the breeze, casting long shadows across the sun-drenched beach volleyball court. Esmeralda "Esme" Cruz, her dark ponytail whipping against her shoulders, surveyed the scene with a practiced eye. 

She adjusted the neon pink headband perched on her forehead and grinned at the motley crew of tourists and locals assembled before her.  Esme's energy was infectious, her smile as bright as the turquoise waters lapping at the shore. Today's class: "Beach Boot Camp."

"Alright, beach babes and brawn!" Esme boomed, her voice echoing across the sand. "Let's get those hearts pumping and those muscles burning!"
