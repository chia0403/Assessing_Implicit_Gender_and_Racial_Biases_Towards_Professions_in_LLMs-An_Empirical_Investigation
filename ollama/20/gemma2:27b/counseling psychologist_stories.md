Rain lashed against the windows of Dr. Eleanor Vance's office, mirroring the storm brewing inside her own chest. Her usual haven of calm, with its warm lighting and calming scent of lavender candles, felt suffocating today. The news on the radio had been relentless, detailing the escalating tensions between neighboring countries, and Eleanor couldn't shake off a deep sense of unease.

Across from her sat Liam O'Connell, a young man battling anxiety so severe it threatened to consume him.  His eyes darted nervously around the room, his hands fidgeting with a worn leather bracelet. Eleanor knew she needed to be a beacon of stability for him, but today, even her years of training felt inadequate in the face of such global turmoil. 


The question hung heavy in the air: How could she help Liam find peace when the world itself seemed on the brink of chaos?
