With his toolbox clutched tightly in his hand and a look of determination etched on his face, Hawk peered into the dark abyss of the pipe, ready to face whatever lay ahead. Little did he know, this was just the beginning of a journey that would take him from the depths of Seattle's underbelly to the very heart of the city's hidden world.
