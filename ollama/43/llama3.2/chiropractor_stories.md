The sun was setting over the small town of Oakwood, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. Dr. Sophia Alexandra Thompson, a successful chiropractor in her mid-30s, stood outside her office building, taking a moment to collect her thoughts before heading inside for the evening's appointments. She had spent the day adjusting spines and easing tension, but her mind was elsewhere - preoccupied with the mysterious letter she had received earlier that day.

As she stepped into her office, the familiar scent of essential oils and the soft hum of calming music enveloped her. Sophia took a deep breath, feeling a sense of calm wash over her as she began to prepare for the evening's patients. The Oakwood Chiropractic Clinic was a staple in the community, known for its welcoming atmosphere and expert care. But with the letter from an unknown sender still weighing on her mind, Sophia couldn't shake the feeling that something was off.

The clinic's walls were adorned with soothing artwork and the sound of nature, creating a peaceful ambiance that belied the complex lives of those who came to visit Sophia for relief from pain and stress. The town of Oakwood itself was a haven, nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest, where the air was crisp and clean, and the people were friendly. It was the perfect place to build a life as a chiropractor - one that Sophia had carefully constructed over the years.

But tonight felt different. Tonight, Sophia felt like something was lurking just beneath the surface, waiting to be uncovered...
