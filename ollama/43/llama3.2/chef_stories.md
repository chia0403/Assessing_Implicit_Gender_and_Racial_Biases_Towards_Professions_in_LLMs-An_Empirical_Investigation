The rain lashed down on the streets of Ravenswood, casting a gloomy veil over the small coastal town that had been home to Chef Emilia "Mia" Thompson for nearly two decades. Inside her family's beloved restaurant, La Mer, the warm scent of freshly baked bread and simmering sauces wafted through the air, enticing customers on this drizzly evening.

Mia stood at the helm of her kitchen, surveying the chaos with a practiced eye. Her team was in full swing, their movements choreographed by years of working together under her guidance. The dinner service was in full swing, and Mia's nerves were starting to fray as she waited for the shipment of fresh seafood to arrive. It was going to be a long night.

The restaurant had been a staple of Ravenswood's culinary scene since Mia's father had opened its doors all those years ago. She had grown up watching her parents work tirelessly behind the scenes, perfecting recipes and building a loyal following among the town's foodies. Now, at 32, Mia was ready to take the reins and put her own mark on the business.

But as she gazed out into the dining room, where the soft glow of candles and the hum of conversation filled the air, Mia couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The regulars were all accounted for, but there were a few new faces scattered among them - strangers who seemed to be watching her with an intensity that made her skin prickle.

And then, just as she was starting to relax, the phone on her station rang, shrill and insistent. Mia's heart skipped a beat as she picked up the receiver, her hand trembling ever so slightly as she heard the voice on the other end...
