---

The rain-soaked streets of Ravenswood were always at their most unforgiving on nights like these, when the mist rolled in off the Thames like a damp shroud. It was a night like any other in this old, industrial town, where the factories stood as monoliths to a bygone era and the people struggled to eke out a living.

In her small office above the local pub, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson sat hunched over her desk, sipping lukewarm coffee and staring at the latest batch of police press releases. As a reporter for the Ravenswood Gazette, Lexi had seen it all - from the petty crimes that filled the front page to the occasional murder that made headlines. But tonight, something about the latest bulletin caught her eye.

"URGENT - Body Found in Local Industrial Park," the headline read, accompanied by a grainy photograph of a young woman lying on her back, her eyes frozen in a permanent stare. Lexi's gut tightened as she scanned the accompanying details: the victim had been identified as 22-year-old Emily Wilson, who worked at one of Ravenswood's major factories.

Lexi felt a shiver run down her spine as she picked up her pen and began to scribble notes on her pad. This was going to be a big story - and she was determined to get the scoop before anyone else did. Little did she know, this was just the beginning of a night that would change everything.

Setting: Ravenswood, a small, industrial town in England, with a rich history of manufacturing and industry. The town is located near the Thames River, which runs through its heart. The weather is typically cool and rainy, reflecting the town's grey, overcast skies.

Main Character: Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a 25-year-old reporter for the Ravenswood Gazette. She is determined, resourceful, and always on the lookout for her next big story.
