He stared out at the tempestuous scene, his mind as tumultuous as the weather outside.

Alistair Cavendish, Chief Executive Officer of Cavendish Industries, was not a man easily rattled. His steely blue eyes, usually sharp and penetrating, were clouded with worry. His meticulously styled silver hair, normally slicked back in perfect order, was tousled by nervous fingers running through it. For forty years, Alistair had navigated the treacherous waters of the corporate world with unwavering composure. But today, a storm raged within him, threatening to capsize everything he had built.
