Rain lashed against the windows of the Sunnyside Daycare Centre, blurring the cheerful yellow paint into streaks of grey. Inside, Amelia Rose Cartwright was attempting to herd six giggling toddlers toward the craft table, their tiny voices rising in a chorus of excited shrieks. Crayons rolled across the floor, forgotten beneath chubby hands clutching stuffed animals.

Amelia sighed inwardly. It was days like these that made her question her sanity, but then she'd catch a glimpse of Maisie's gap-toothed grin or hear little Liam's enthusiastic pronouncements about "blue birdies" and remember why she loved this job.
