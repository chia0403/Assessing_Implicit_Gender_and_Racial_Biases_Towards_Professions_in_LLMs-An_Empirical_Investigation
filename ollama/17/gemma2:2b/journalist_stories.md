Rain lashed against the cracked asphalt of 14th Street, each drop exploding in a miniature geyser as it hit the grime-coated gutter. The wind howled like a wounded beast, whipping up debris from the overflowing dumpster behind the dingy bar. Inside, the air hung thick with the smell of stale beer, cigarette smoke, and something vaguely metallic - a smell that reminded Amelia "Amy" Hayes, seasoned investigative reporter for the *City Sentinel*, of a long-forgotten tragedy.

Amy was hunched over her notebook, the flickering neon glow of the “Open” sign casting harsh shadows on her face as she furiously scribbled. She had been waiting for hours, trying to piece together the puzzle of the missing scientist's disappearance. The case had gone cold months ago; a sudden shift in the city's underbelly and an escalating gang war meant all but the bravest investigators abandoned the search.  But Amy, driven by a relentless instinct, refused to be deterred.
