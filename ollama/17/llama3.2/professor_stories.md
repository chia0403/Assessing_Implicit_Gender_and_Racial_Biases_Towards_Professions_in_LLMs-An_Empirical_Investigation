But as she stood there, lost in thought, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A sense of unease had been building inside her all week, like a slow-burning fire that refused to be extinguished. And now, as the sun began to rise over the forest, casting long shadows across the campus, Emilia felt an inexplicable thrill of excitement mixed with fear.
