But tonight was different. Tonight, Aurélie was cooking for a very special client – someone who required not only the finest ingredients and techniques but also discretion and secrecy. As she expertly chopped fresh herbs and sautéed succulent foie gras in a sizzling hot pan, she couldn't help but wonder what lay ahead.

The phone on her workbench rang, shrill in the quiet kitchen. Aurélie's heart skipped a beat as she picked up, her mind racing with possibilities. "Bonjour," she said, her voice smooth and professional.

And then, in a low, gravelly voice, she heard words that would change everything: "Madame Dupont, I need you to cook for me tonight."
