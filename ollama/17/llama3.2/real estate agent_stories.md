Opening:
As she stood at the edge of the rooftop bar, sipping on her whiskey sour and gazing out over the glittering Manhattan skyline, Realtor Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson couldn't help but feel a sense of unease. The city that never slept was supposed to be her playground, but lately, it had started to feel more like a prison.

Setting:
Lexi's office, Thompson & Associates Real Estate, was located in the heart of Midtown Manhattan, overlooking the towering skyscrapers of the financial district. Her clients were typically high-end buyers and sellers, seeking luxury penthouses with breathtaking views of the city. But Lexi had bigger ambitions - she wanted to break into the commercial real estate market and make a name for herself as one of the top agents in the city.

As she sipped her drink, Lexi's mind wandered to the proposal she was due to present to her partner later that evening. It was a high-stakes deal, with millions on the line and only hours until the deadline. She took another sip of her drink, feeling a familiar knot of anxiety in her stomach.

Just then, her phone buzzed with an incoming text from her best friend and confidant, Rachel: "Hey girl, got any news? The meeting is tomorrow at 10am - don't be late!" Lexi smiled wryly to herself, knowing that she was already running a little behind schedule. But as she glanced out over the city, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off...
