The sun was setting over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and worn buildings. The air was alive with the scent of freshly cut wood and the distant rumble of the river that ran through the heart of the town.

In his small workshop, nestled between a vintage bookstore and a bustling café, Elijah Benjamin "Eli" Thompson carefully sanded down a newly cut plank of cedar. His hands moved with a precision born of years of practice, as he coaxed the wood into submission. Eli was a master carpenter, renowned for his attention to detail and ability to bring even the most intricate designs to life.

As he worked, Eli's mind wandered back to the mysterious letter that had arrived at his doorstep just days before. The words on the page were simple, yet they sent shivers down his spine: "Meet me at the old oak at midnight. Come alone." Who could have written such a cryptic message, and what did they want with Eli? He pushed the thoughts aside, focusing on the task at hand – but he couldn't shake the feeling that this was only the beginning of something much bigger.
