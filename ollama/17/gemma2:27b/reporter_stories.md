The stale air in the diner hung thick with the scent of burnt coffee and yesterday's fry oil. Rain lashed against the grimy windows, blurring the neon glow of the "Open 24 Hours" sign into an abstract smear. Amelia Thorne, her trench coat dripping onto the cracked vinyl booth, tapped a pen impatiently against her notepad. Across from her sat Gus, a wiry man with eyes that darted around nervously like trapped sparrows. He was her source, a low-level mechanic who claimed to have stumbled upon something big – something that could shake the foundations of Havenwood's seemingly idyllic façade.

Havenwood, nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains, prided itself on its tight-knit community and quaint charm. Quaint it may be, Amelia thought wryly, but beneath the surface lurked secrets as dark as the storm raging outside.
