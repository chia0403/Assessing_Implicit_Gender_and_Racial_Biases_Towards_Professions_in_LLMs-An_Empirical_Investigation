Rain lashed against the grimy windows of Bartholomew "Bart" Finch's cramped office, blurring the already hazy view of the alleyway below.  The air hung thick with the scent of stale coffee and desperation. Bart, his tie askew and his brow furrowed in concentration, stared at the mess of numbers sprawled across his desk. He wasn't a man accustomed to chaos, yet here it was, threatening to engulf him like the relentless storm outside.

Bart Finch, Certified Public Accountant, prided himself on order and precision. But this case, involving a missing shipment of rare artifacts for a shadowy collector known only as "The Raven," was anything but straightforward. The ledger entries were riddled with inconsistencies, dates seemed to jump forward and back in time, and the sums simply refused to add up.  

A shiver ran down Bart's spine. He knew he was wading into something dangerous, something that could potentially cost him more than just his reputation.
