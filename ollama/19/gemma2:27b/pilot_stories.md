The rusty wind whipped Amelia "Ace" Harding's braids against her face as she surveyed the desolate landscape. The setting sun painted the sky in bruised purples and oranges, casting long shadows across the cracked earth and skeletal trees that clawed at the crimson horizon. Ace tightened her grip on the yoke of her battered Cessna 172, its engine sputtering a tired protest against the thin air. Below her, the abandoned mining town of Dust Devil groaned under the weight of forgotten dreams.

Ace's mission was simple: deliver a package to the lone survivor who stubbornly refused to leave this forsaken corner of Nevada. It sounded easy enough on paper, but Ace knew better. Dust Devil held a darkness deeper than its shadowed canyons, a secret whispered in the wind that gnawed at her intuition.
