As he leaned against the workbench, running a calloused hand through his thick brown hair, Jack's gaze drifted out the grimy window, lost in thought.

It had been three weeks since the mysterious package arrived at his doorstep, its contents shrouded in an air of secrecy and intrigue. Since then, Hawk's life had become a tangled web of secrets, lies, and ancient mysteries waiting to be unraveled. Little did he know, this was only the beginning of a journey that would take him from the rooftops of Willow Creek to the farthest corners of his own troubled past.
