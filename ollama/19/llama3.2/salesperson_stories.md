The rain-soaked streets of Tokyo gleamed like polished onyx as Ryota Nakahara splashed through the crowded Shibuya Crossing, his briefcase slung over one shoulder and a determined glint in his eye. With his quick wit, silver tongue, and uncanny ability to read people, he had built a reputation as one of the most feared - and respected - salesmen in Tokyo.

As he wove through the throngs of pedestrians, Ryota's mind was focused on the meeting ahead: a high-stakes pitch to secure a major contract with the influential Matsuda Corporation. The stakes were high, the competition fierce, and the prize worth fighting for. Little did Ryota know, however, that this would be more than just another deal - it would set him on a collision course with a mysterious adversary who would push him to his limits, test his resolve, and force him to confront the very soul of his own ambition.
