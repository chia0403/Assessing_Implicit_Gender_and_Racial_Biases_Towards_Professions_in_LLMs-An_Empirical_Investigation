**The Story Opening**

It was a drizzly autumn evening in New York City, and the neon lights of the Manhattan skyline cast an otherworldly glow over the crowded streets of Midtown. The smell of wet pavement and exhaust fumes hung heavy in the air as people scurried to and fro, hurrying to find warmth and shelter from the rain. In a small hotel nestled between a vintage bookstore and a Korean restaurant, a lone figure stood patiently by the lobby desk, his eyes fixed on the clock ticking away on the wall.

**The Setting**

Welcome to the Regency Plaza Hotel, a modest but reliable establishment that catered to the needs of travelers and locals alike. For 27-year-old bell hop, Jasper Everett Wells, this was home - or at least, it felt like home. With his rumpled suit, faded tie, and weary eyes, Jasper blended seamlessly into the hotel's worn decor, as if he'd been a fixture here for years. In reality, he had only been working at the Regency Plaza for six months, but to him, it might as well be an eternity.

As Jasper stood watch, his thoughts drifted to the peculiar package that had arrived earlier that day - a mysterious box with no return address and a single word scrawled on the label: "Jasper." The hotel's management was tight-lipped about its origins, but Jasper couldn't shake the feeling that this enigmatic delivery was somehow connected to his own uncertain future.

With a sigh, Jasper squared his shoulders and prepared for another long night of navigating the hotel's labyrinthine corridors and dealing with demanding guests. Little did he know, this drizzly autumn evening would be just the beginning of an adventure that would shake the very foundations of his life...
