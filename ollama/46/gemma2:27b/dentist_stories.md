The whirring of the drill seemed to echo Amelia Rosalind Cartwright’s mounting anxiety. Outside her window, the relentless sun beat down on the dusty Texas town of Pecan Grove, its heat shimmering off the asphalt like a mirage. Inside her cramped dental office, the air hung thick with the sweet, metallic scent of disinfectant and fear. Fear that, despite years of training and countless successful procedures, Amelia was about to face her worst nightmare.

Today wasn’t just any day for Dr. Cartwright. Today, she was scheduled to perform a root canal on Mayor Thaddeus Butterfield, Pecan Grove's most beloved (and notoriously stubborn) citizen.
