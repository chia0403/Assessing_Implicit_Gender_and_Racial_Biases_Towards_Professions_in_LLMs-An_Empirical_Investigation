The rain-soaked streets of downtown Seattle glistened like polished onyx as the sun set over the city, casting a golden glow over the towering skyscrapers and cramped alleyways. Inside the imposing glass-and-steel façade of Harrison & Associates, a prestigious law firm nestled between a vintage bookstore and a hip coffee shop, attorney Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood at the edge of her fourth-floor office, gazing out the floor-to-ceiling windows.

A native Seattleite with a fierce determination burning in her eyes, Lexi was known for her tenacious advocacy style and razor-sharp instincts – skills she had honed over years of fighting for justice on behalf of her clients. Her dark hair was pulled back into a sleek ponytail, revealing the sharp contours of her heart-shaped face and piercing green eyes that seemed to bore into those she met.

At 32, Lexi had already established herself as one of the most respected lawyers in the firm's history, but tonight, she felt restless, like there was something missing from her life. She paced back and forth across the room, her high heels clicking against the polished hardwood floor as she tried to shake off the sense of unease that had been building all day.

Just then, her phone buzzed with an incoming call from the office's receptionist, signaling that a new case had landed on her desk. Lexi's eyes narrowed as she picked up the receiver, her voice crisp and commanding as she answered, "Harrison & Associates, this is Lexi Thompson speaking." Little did she know, tonight would bring more than just a stack of files to keep her busy...
