As the sun set over the small town of Willow Creek, Arizona, Aurora "Rory" Wynter settled into her seat on the worn leather couch in the airport lounge, sipping a lukewarm cup of coffee and staring blankly at the TV mounted on the wall. The news anchor's face was stern and unyielding as she delivered the evening's weather forecast: a low-pressure system was brewing over the Midwest, threatening to disrupt air travel across the country.

Rory let out a deep sigh, rubbing her tired eyes with the heel of her hand. She had been on the ground for hours, waiting for her flight to depart and then another one in the morning - a grueling schedule that would have her flying non-stop from New York to Los Angeles three times this week alone.

As a senior flight attendant for American Airlines, Rory was no stranger to long hours and delayed flights. But tonight felt different. Tonight, she had a sense of restlessness that she couldn't shake, like the universe was nudging her towards something just out of reach. She glanced at her watch for what felt like the hundredth time, wondering if there was a way to skip this tedious layover altogether.

Little did Rory know, tonight's flight would hold more than just ordinary turbulence - and would set her on a collision course with destiny that would change everything she thought she knew about herself.
