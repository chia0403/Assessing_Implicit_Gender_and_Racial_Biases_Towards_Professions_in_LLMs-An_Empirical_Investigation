Here's a potential story opening and setting:

It was a drizzly autumn evening in the sleepy town of Ravenswood, where the misty veil of the nearby forest seemed to cling to the streets like a damp shroud. In a grand Victorian mansion on Elm Street, housekeeper Emilia Isabella Winters stirred the embers of the fireplace, her eyes scanning the dimly lit hallway as she went about her evening routine.

Emilia had been working for the reclusive Mr. Edward Blackstone for five years now, and she knew every creaky floorboard, every hidden nook, and every secret that the old house held within its stone walls. Tonight was like any other – a solitary night of tidying up, polishing silverware, and ensuring that the master's bed was made to perfection.

But as Emilia made her way to the kitchen to start dinner, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A faint scent of damp earth and decaying leaves wafted through the air, like a whispered warning from beyond the threshold. And then, there were the strange noises – the creaks and groans of the old house taking on a slightly more menacing tone than usual.

Emilia's heart quickened as she wondered what secrets the night might bring to the halls of Ravenswood Manor...
