The wind howled like a banshee across the moors, rattling the ancient windowpanes of Professor Alistair MacAllister's cluttered study. Rain lashed against the glass, blurring the view of the desolate Scottish landscape beyond.  Inside, the fire in the hearth crackled merrily, casting dancing shadows on the walls lined with towering bookshelves. Alistair himself sat hunched over a massive oak desk, his brow furrowed in concentration as he pored over a dusty tome bound in worn leather. 

His name was whispered in hushed tones throughout the hallowed halls of St. Andrews University - Professor Alistair Sinclair MacAllister, renowned archaeologist and expert on ancient Celtic lore. But tonight, Alistair wasn't delving into past civilizations. Tonight, his focus was on something far more immediate, and far more unsettling: a series of cryptic messages that had begun appearing in his study just weeks prior.
