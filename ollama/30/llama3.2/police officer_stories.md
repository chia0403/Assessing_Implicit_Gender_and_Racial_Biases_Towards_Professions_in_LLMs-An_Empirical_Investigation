Detective Emily Jane Wilson stood at the edge of the rain-soaked rooftop, her eyes scanning the darkened cityscape below. With a sigh, she turned away from the view and began her descent into the rain, her eyes fixed on the dark stairwell that led down to the floor below. She was no stranger to the city's seedy underbelly, but something about this case had her instincts on high alert.

As a seasoned detective with the Seattle Police Department, Emily had seen it all - from petty thefts to murder and mayhem. But there was something about The Fox that resonated deep within her, a sense of intelligence and cunning that made her want to catch him before he struck again.
