Lexi's mind was already racing with possibilities as she walked towards the hospital, her eyes scanning the crowded corridors for the familiar face of Dr. Patel, her colleague and friend. She knew that this case would be a tough one - Olivia seemed like an innocent victim, but Lexi had a feeling that there was more to this story than met the eye.

As she entered the hospital's secure wing, Lexi felt a sense of unease wash over her. She had worked with some of the most disturbed individuals in the world, but there was something about Olivia that sent a shiver down her spine.
