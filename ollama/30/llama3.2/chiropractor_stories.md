Her small office was a cozy oasis amidst the urban chaos, filled with soothing colors and calming sounds that put even the most anxious patients at ease. As she sipped her coffee and surveyed her schedule for the day, Dr. Wilson felt a sense of contentment wash over her - it was shaping up to be a great day.
