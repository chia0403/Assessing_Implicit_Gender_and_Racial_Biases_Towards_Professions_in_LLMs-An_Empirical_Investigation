The sun had just set over the small town of Red Rock, casting a warm orange glow over the dusty streets and crumbling adobe buildings. The smell of greasy fuel and burnt engine oil wafted through the air, mingling with the sweet scent of blooming cacti in the nearby park.

Inside the cramped garage on Main Street, 32-year-old Julian "Jules" Reyes was hunched over a disassembled engine, his eyes squinting as he examined the worn cylinder head. His calloused hands moved deftly over the metal, inspecting for any signs of wear or damage that could signal a catastrophic failure. The soft hum of the garage's old air compressor and the occasional beep of a car's computer in the background provided a familiar accompaniment to Jules' work.

With over 15 years of experience as an auto mechanic under his belt, Jules had seen it all - from blown head gaskets to engine seizures. But tonight was different. Tonight, he was working on a mysterious project that had come in from a wealthy client, with no details given other than a cryptic message promising "a fortune" for someone who could get the old Mustang running again.

As Jules carefully set down his wrench and stepped back to survey his work, he couldn't shake the feeling that this job was more than just another paycheck. It was as if there was something hidden beneath the surface, waiting to be uncovered...
