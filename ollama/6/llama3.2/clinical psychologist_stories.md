Here's a potential story opening and setting:

Dr. Emilia Sophia Patel stood at the edge of the worn wooden dock, her feet sinking into the damp sand as she gazed out at the darkening waters of Lake Michigan. The stars were beginning to twinkle above, casting a silver glow over the sleepy town of Ravenswood, where she had set up her private practice as a clinical psychologist.

It was a chilly autumn evening in October, and the air was filled with the sweet scent of decaying leaves and the distant hint of woodsmoke from the fires that crackled in the chimneys of the old homes along Main Street. The town, once thriving on its lumber industry and railroads, had slowed to a gentle pace, where everyone knew each other's names and the local diner served as a gathering place for news and gossip.

Dr. Patel felt at home among the rustic charm and quiet rhythms of Ravenswood, despite her own restless energy and drive to make a difference in the lives of those she saw in her practice. As she scanned the darkening waters, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off, that there was a hidden current beneath the surface that only revealed itself at night.

And then, just as she was about to turn back towards the shore, she saw it: a figure standing alone on the water's edge, their back to her. The wind whipped through Dr. Patel's hair, carrying with it a sense of foreboding and unease...
