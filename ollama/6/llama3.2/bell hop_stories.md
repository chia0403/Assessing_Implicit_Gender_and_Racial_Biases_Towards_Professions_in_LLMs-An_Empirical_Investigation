---

The neon sign creaked in the gentle breeze as it cast a gaudy glow over the deserted parking lot. Inside, the lobby of the Golden Crown Hotel was quiet, except for the soft clinking of glasses and the muted hum of the jazz band playing in the corner. Amidst the rows of gleaming chrome fixtures and polished marble floors, bellhop extraordinaire Reginald "Reg" Thompson stood at attention, his bright blue eyes scanning the lobby with a practiced air of attentiveness.

At 25 years old, Reg had been working as a bellhop for nearly five years, and he knew every nook and cranny of the Golden Crown by heart. From the hidden compartment in the concierge desk to the secret passageway behind the fountain, Reg had it all memorized. But despite his familiarity with the hotel's inner workings, Reg couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The air felt thick with tension, and the usual bustle of the lobby seemed muted tonight.

As he checked in the last of the evening's guests, a sleek black sedan pulled up to the curb, its driver-side window rolling down to reveal a glimpse of smoke-stained leather and piercing green eyes. Reg's instincts prickled; this was no ordinary guest. Little did he know, his life was about to take a drastic turn, one that would lead him down a path of secrets, lies, and danger lurking in every shadow of the Golden Crown Hotel.

Setting: The Golden Crown Hotel, located in the heart of Los Angeles, California, on Sunset Boulevard.
