Rain lashed against the arched windows of the Grand Blackwood Library, each drop exploding in a miniature firework display on the cobblestone courtyard below.  Inside, the air hung heavy with the scent of aged paper and beeswax polish. Eloise Alistair Winthrop adjusted her spectacles, peering down at the ancient ledger spread across her desk. The flickering gaslight cast long shadows that danced across the towering shelves crammed with leather-bound tomes. Eloise traced a calloused finger along a faded inscription – "Collected Dreams and Nightmares of Blackwood Manor" – a shiver running down her spine.  This was no ordinary library; nestled in the heart of a crumbling gothic manor, its collection held secrets whispered only in hushed tones. And tonight, a new secret seemed to be beckoning Eloise from the dusty pages...
