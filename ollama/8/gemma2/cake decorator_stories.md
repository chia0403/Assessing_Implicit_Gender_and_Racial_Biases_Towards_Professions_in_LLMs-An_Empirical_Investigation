The air hung thick with the scent of vanilla and powdered sugar in "Sugarspun Dreams," Elara Willowbrook's tiny bakery tucked away on a cobblestone street in Edinburgh. Outside, a drizzle turned the ancient stones slick and grey, but inside, warmth radiated from the oven and the twinkling fairy lights strung across the ceiling. 

Elara hummed softly to herself as she carefully piped buttercream roses onto a towering chocolate cake destined for a high-society wedding. Her hands moved with practiced precision, transforming plain sponge into edible masterpieces. A faint smile played on her lips; there was magic in creating something beautiful and delicious, especially when it brought joy to others. 

But today, Elara felt a prickle of unease amidst the sugary bliss. A single, crimson-coloured feather lay abandoned on her worktable, shimmering like a drop of blood in the soft light.  She had never seen anything like it before in her cozy bakery, and a shiver ran down her spine as she wondered where it came from...
