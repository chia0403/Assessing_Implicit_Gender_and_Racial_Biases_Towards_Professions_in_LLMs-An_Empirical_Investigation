With a practiced air of nonchalance, Lexi flicked her cigarette into the gutter, watching as it sputtered and died in the puddles left behind by last night's downpour. The rain-soaked streets seemed to swallow up the sound of her own footsteps as she made her way towards the offices of the New Haven Daily Chronicle, where she worked as a investigative reporter – and where one fateful day, everything would change forever.
