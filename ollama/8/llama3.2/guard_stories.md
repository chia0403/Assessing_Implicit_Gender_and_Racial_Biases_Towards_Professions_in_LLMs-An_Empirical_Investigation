The mist-shrouded town of Ravenshire lay huddled in the heart of the mystical forest of Elvendom, its residents living in uneasy harmony with the ancient magic that seeped from the trees like a slow-moving fog. Amidst this eerie beauty, Constable Amaranth Wellesley stood watch on the crumbling stone walls of the town's east gate, her eyes scanning the shadows for any sign of trouble.

Amaranth, a sturdy and practical woman with a sharp mind and quicker wit, had been a member of the Ravenshire Guard since she was a teenager. She knew every inch of this forsaken place, from the twisted oak trees that seemed to lean in like sentinels to the crooked alleys where whispers of dark magic were said to linger.

As she stood at attention, her gaze drifted toward the mist-shrouded forest beyond the gate, where rumors had been circulating about a series of gruesome disappearances among the local population. The villagers were on edge, and Amaranth couldn't shake the feeling that something was watching her from the darkness, waiting for its chance to strike.

With a flick of her wrist, she adjusted her tunic and took a deep breath, the night air filling her lungs like a promise of secrets yet to be uncovered. Little did she know, this was just the beginning of a night that would test everything she thought she knew about Ravenshire, its people, and herself.
