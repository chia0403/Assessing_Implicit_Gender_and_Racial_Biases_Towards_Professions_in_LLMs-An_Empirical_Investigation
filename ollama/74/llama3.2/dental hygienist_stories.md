The misty morning air clung to the small town of Willow Creek like a damp shroud, casting a gloomy veil over the sleepy streets. In her cozy apartment above the local diner, dental hygienist Emilia Rose Thompson stirred from a restless sleep, her mind still reeling from the strange dreams that had plagued her for weeks. It wasn't just the vivid images of old, abandoned houses and forgotten towns that lingered in her subconscious – it was the sense of unease that seemed to seep into every pore of her body.

As she sat up in bed and rubbed the grit from her eyes, Emilia's gaze drifted out the window to the quaint Main Street below. The diner, "Sunny Side Up," was already bustling with the morning crowd, its bright yellow sign creaking gently in the breeze. It was a far cry from the quiet, predictable life she'd left behind in college – but as much as Emilia loved her work and her friends, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off, waiting just beneath the surface of Willow Creek's idyllic facade.

Little did she know, this morning would be the day everything changed.
