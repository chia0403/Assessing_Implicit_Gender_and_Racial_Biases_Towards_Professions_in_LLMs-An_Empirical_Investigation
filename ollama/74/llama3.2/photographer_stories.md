The sun had just set over the small coastal town of Ravenswood, casting a warm orange glow over the weathered buildings and bustling harbor. The air was filled with the salty scent of the sea and the sound of seagulls crying overhead.

Ava Elizabeth Thompson stood at the edge of the water, her feet sunk into the sand as she gazed out at the waves. She had always found solace in this spot, where the rhythm of the ocean seemed to match the beat of her own restless heart. As a photographer, Ava was drawn to the beauty and power of the sea, and Ravenswood's rugged coastline was her favorite subject.

Ava's dark hair was tied back in a messy knot, and her eyes were shaded by a pair of worn-out sunglasses. She had spent the day shooting the town's iconic lighthouse, trying to capture its grandeur on film. Now, she stood lost in thought, her mind wandering to the strange occurrences that had been plaguing Ravenswood lately.

Rumors had been circulating about ghostly apparitions and unexplained phenomena, and Ava was both fascinated and skeptical. As a photographer, she had always sought out the truth through her lens, but there was something about this town that made her feel like she was on the cusp of uncovering a mystery that would change everything.

As she stood there, the stars beginning to twinkle in the night sky, Ava felt an inexplicable pull towards the water's edge. It was as if the sea was calling to her, urging her to come closer and listen to its secrets. With a sense of trepidation and curiosity, she took a step forward, the waves lapping gently at her feet...
