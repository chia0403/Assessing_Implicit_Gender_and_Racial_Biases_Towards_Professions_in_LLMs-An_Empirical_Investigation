As the last wisps of sunlight faded from the evening sky, Flight Attendant Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson prepared for another long-haul journey across the Pacific. But for now, she had to focus on getting the passengers settled in for the night. She began to review the safety briefing materials one last time, mentally preparing herself for the long hours ahead.

Lexi had worked as a flight attendant for nearly a decade, but the thrill of adventure still coursed through her veins like adrenaline whenever she boarded a new aircraft. Yet tonight felt different - perhaps it was the lingering sense of unease that lingered in her bones after receiving an anonymous warning earlier that morning about the turbulent flights ahead.

As the flight attendants gathered around the crew room to finalize the pre-flight checks, Lexi's phone buzzed discreetly on the counter - a text from an unknown number.
