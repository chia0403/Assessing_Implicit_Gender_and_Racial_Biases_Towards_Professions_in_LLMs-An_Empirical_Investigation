The scent of eucalyptus and damp towels hung heavy in the air, a familiar comfort to Amelia "Amy" Beatrice Hartley.  She adjusted her black tank top, the seams straining slightly against her toned biceps, and surveyed the room. The early morning light filtered through the floor-to-ceiling windows of the Equinox studio, illuminating the dozen or so faces staring back at her with varying degrees of apprehension and determination. Amy smiled, a wide, genuine grin that crinkled the corners of her hazel eyes.  

This was her sanctuary, this hour before the city truly woke up. This space, filled with the promise of sweat, exertion, and transformation, was where Amy came alive. 


"Good morning, everyone!" she called out, her voice ringing with energy. "Ready to crush it?" A smattering of enthusiastic cheers met her greeting, a welcome sound that propelled her into her carefully crafted routine.
