In the sleepy coastal town of Tidal Cove, where the smell of saltwater and seaweed filled the air, and the sound of gulls echoed through the streets, 27-year-old Aurora "Rory" Thompson worked her magic on the hands of the townspeople. As a skilled manicurist at the local salon, The Nail Parlor, Rory took pride in transforming dull nails into works of art, all while listening to the latest gossip and secrets shared by her clients.

As she expertly shaped and polished the pinky nail of Mrs. Jenkins, a regular at the salon, Rory's mind began to wander. It was a Friday afternoon, just as the summer solstice celebration was about to kick off in Tidal Cove. The town was buzzing with excitement, and Rory couldn't help but feel a sense of restlessness. She had plans for this weekend that didn't involve painting nails or listening to small talk...
