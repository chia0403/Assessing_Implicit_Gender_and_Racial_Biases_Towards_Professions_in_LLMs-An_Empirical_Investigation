Eloise longed for nothing more than to trade her tax forms for a mojito and a beach chair.

But alas, tax season was upon them, and Eloise Montgomery, seasoned CPA and self-proclaimed "Tax Whisperer," had a mountain of returns still awaiting her attention.  She sighed, pushing a stray curl behind her ear and reaching for the next folder in the stack.
