The flickering fluorescent lights hummed overhead, casting a sickly yellow pallor over Amelia Beatrice Finch's face as she hunched over her keyboard. The air hung thick with the scent of stale coffee and desperation. Lines of code scrolled across her monitor, each one a battlefield in the ongoing war against a relentless bug that threatened to bring down the entire city's power grid.  

Amelia, a systems analyst for the beleaguered Power Authority of Aurora, was running out of time. The clock on the wall ticked with ominous finality, each second another nail hammered into the coffin of her sanity and career. Outside, the first storm clouds of winter were gathering, promising a night choked with darkness, both literal and metaphorical. 


If Amelia couldn't find the bug – and fast – Aurora would plunge into chaos.
