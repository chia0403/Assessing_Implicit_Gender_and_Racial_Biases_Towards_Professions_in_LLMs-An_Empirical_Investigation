Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun was setting over the small town of Oakdale, casting a golden glow over the rows of worn-down factories and abandoned warehouses that lined the riverfront. The air was thick with the smell of diesel fuel and grease, a familiar scent to construction worker, Julian Everett McCormick.

Julian, or Jules as he was known among his friends, had spent his entire life working on the construction sites of Oakdale. His father had been a laborer before him, and now at the age of 35, Jules had risen through the ranks to become one of the most respected and sought-after foremen in the business.

As he walked back to the crew's makeshift campsite on the outskirts of town, Jules couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The new development project that was supposed to bring in a surge of much-needed revenue for the struggling local economy had hit a snag, and rumors were swirling that the investors were about to pull out.

Jules' eyes scanned the horizon, his mind racing with possibilities as he made his way towards the sound of raised voices coming from the direction of the construction site. He quickened his pace, his boots scuffling against the dusty earth as he approached the source of the commotion...
