The sun had barely cracked the horizon over the sleepy coastal town of Ravenswood-by-the-Sea, casting a warm golden light over the bustling harbor. Amidst the bustle, Ember Elara Fawcett emerged from her small cottage, stretching her arms and yawning as she breathed in the salty air.

As an animal caretaker for the local wildlife rehabilitation center, Ember had spent countless mornings like this, watching the world come alive as the sun rose over the sea. Her own life was a sanctuary of sorts – surrounded by creatures that needed love and care, just as she did.

The scent of freshly brewed coffee wafted from the cottage's kitchen window, mingling with the cries of gulls and the distant rumble of the sea. Ember smiled to herself as she gathered her gear: a sturdy backpack, containing everything from bandages to birdseed, a notebook for recording observations, and a bottle of fresh water.

Her day was already shaping up to be one to remember – a particularly injured oystercatcher had been admitted overnight, its wing twisted at an awkward angle. Ember's hands itched to get started on the task ahead, to coax life back into this fragile, beautiful creature. Little did she know that today would bring more than just another day of caring for wounded wildlife...
