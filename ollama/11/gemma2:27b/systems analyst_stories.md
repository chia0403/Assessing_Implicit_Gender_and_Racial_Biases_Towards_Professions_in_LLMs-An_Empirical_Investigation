Rain lashed against the grimy windows of the abandoned warehouse, each drop exploding in a miniature firework display on the cracked concrete floor. Eleanor Vance, her dark hair plastered to her forehead with sweat, hunched over a flickering laptop screen.  The air was thick with the smell of damp cardboard and rusting metal, punctuated by the sharp tang of ozone from the overloaded server humming beside her.

Eleanor, a systems analyst renowned for her ability to untangle even the most convoluted code, had taken on this seemingly impossible task: retrieving lost data from a decommissioned network housed in this derelict building. It was a desperate job, commissioned by a shadowy organization she knew little about, but the promised reward – enough to buy her a ticket out of this dead-end city and into a life of freedom – was too tempting to resist.
