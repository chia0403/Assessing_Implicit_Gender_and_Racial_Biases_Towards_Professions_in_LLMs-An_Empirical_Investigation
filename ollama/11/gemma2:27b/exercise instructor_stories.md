Sweat glistened on the brows of her class, bodies contorted in warrior three poses, each straining for balance and perfect alignment. Sera moved among them with the grace of a panther, correcting form here, offering encouragement there, her own muscles rippling beneath her neon pink tank top.  

Outside, the humid Miami air hung heavy, pregnant with the promise of a thunderstorm. But inside "Body & Soul," energy crackled, fuelled by endorphins and Sera's infectious enthusiasm. For an hour, worries were forgotten, replaced by the rhythm of breath and movement.
