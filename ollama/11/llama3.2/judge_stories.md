It was a far cry from this sleek, modern metropolis, where the suits and ties seemed to blend together into a sea of sameness.

Emilia's own life had been shaped by the lessons she'd learned on those country roads: hard work, integrity, and a strong sense of justice. She had worked her way up through the ranks of the courts, earning a reputation as a fair but firm arbiter of the law. But as she gazed out at the faces before her now, Emilia couldn't shake the feeling that this latest case was going to be different.

It was about to change everything.
