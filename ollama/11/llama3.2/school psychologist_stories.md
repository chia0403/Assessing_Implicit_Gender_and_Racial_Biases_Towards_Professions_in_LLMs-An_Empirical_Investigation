Lexi took a deep breath and shouldered her bag, her eyes scanning the parking lot for any sign of disturbance or disarray. Her mind was already racing with possibilities - a note left on her desk from a student in crisis, perhaps? Or maybe something more sinister lurked beneath the surface of this seemingly ordinary day?

As she pushed through the front doors and into the school's main office, the familiar hum of fluorescent lights and the soft murmur of administrative voices enveloped her like a warm hug. But Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that today was going to be anything but ordinary.
