The sun was setting over the bustling streets of downtown Manhattan, casting a golden glow over the towering skyscrapers that seemed to touch the sky. Amidst the hustle and bustle, real estate agent Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood outside her office building, sipping on a lukewarm coffee and gazing out at the city she had grown up in.

At 32 years old, Lexi had already made a name for herself as one of the top agents in her firm, with a keen eye for spotting hidden gems and a talent for negotiating deals that left her clients beaming. But despite her success, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. She had just received an unexpected phone call from an old flame, Alexei Petrov - someone she hadn't heard from in over five years.

As she pondered what to do next, Lexi's phone buzzed with a text message from her best friend and confidant, Rachel Patel: "Hey girl, meet me at the bar on 5th Avenue at 7pm. We need to talk about you." Lexi raised an eyebrow - what could Rachel possibly have to say that was so urgent? She glanced up at the sky, where a dark cloud was beginning to gather on the horizon, like a storm brewing on the city's streets.

With a sense of trepidation, Lexi tucked her phone back into her pocket and made her way towards the bar, wondering what lay ahead.
