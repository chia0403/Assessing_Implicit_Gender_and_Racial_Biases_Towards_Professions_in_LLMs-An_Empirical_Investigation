As she sipped her lukewarm coffee, Emily Anne Thompson gazed out the rain-soaked window of her cramped office, wondering how it had all come to this. Once a rising star in the world of tax preparation, she now found herself stuck in a drab, fluorescent-lit room, surrounded by dusty filing cabinets and the faint scent of despair.

The year was 2023, and the small town of Oakdale, Illinois was not exactly known for its financial wizardry. But Emily Anne had always prided herself on her expertise - she could navigate even the most Byzantine tax code with ease, and had built a loyal client base on her promise of accuracy and compassion.

Now, as she scrolled through the endless stream of forms and invoices that filled her inbox, Emily Anne couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The government was tightening its grip on small businesses, and the economic uncertainty of the past few years had left many of her clients struggling to stay afloat. And yet, despite the best efforts of people like her - the unsung heroes of the tax preparation world - it seemed like no one was getting any younger, or any richer.

Just then, Emily Anne's phone rang, shrill and insistent. She picked up, hoping for a break in the monotony of her day. But as she listened to the voice on the other end, her heart sank just a little bit more...
