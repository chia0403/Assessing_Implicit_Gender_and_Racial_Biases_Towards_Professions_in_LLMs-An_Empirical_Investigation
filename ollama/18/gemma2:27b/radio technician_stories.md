The air in the cramped workshop buzzed with static, mimicking the storm raging outside. Rain lashed against the corrugated iron roof, drowning out the hiss of Elara Rhys's soldering iron as she worked.

Elara wasn't one for complaining. She loved the comforting smell of solder and ozone, the clink of tools, and the satisfying click of a well-tuned circuit. Tonight, however, the storm mirrored her own inner turmoil. A cryptic message from an old friend, hinting at something dangerous brewing in the remote mountain town of Havenwood, had thrown Elara's meticulously ordered life into disarray.

Havenwood. A name whispered in hushed tones amongst radio enthusiasts, a place where signals vanished and whispers of government conspiracies lingered like smoke.  It was the kind of place that drew Elara in like a moth to a flickering flame.
