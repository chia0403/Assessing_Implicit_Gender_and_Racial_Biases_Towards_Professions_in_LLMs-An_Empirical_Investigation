Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain-soaked streets of Ravenswood were always at their most unforgiving during the spring equinox. The grey sky seemed to weigh heavier on the city, as if the very fabric of reality was being stretched to its limits. Amidst this bleak backdrop, a lone figure emerged from the shadows of his small, cluttered shop.

His name was Jasper Everett Wychwood, a 32-year-old plumber with a reputation for being one of the best in town. With a thick shock of curly brown hair and a scruffy beard to match, Jasper looked more like a rugged outdoorsman than the son of a family of modest means who had grown up fixing leaky faucets and unclogging drains.

As he wiped the sweat from his brow on a grease-stained rag, Jasper gazed out at the rows of crumbling Victorian tenements that seemed to lean in, as if sharing some ancient secret. His eyes lingered on the dilapidated mansion across the street, its ornate facade shrouded in vines and neglect. He had always been fascinated by the strange, unexplained noises that emanated from within those decaying walls.

Little did Jasper know, his life was about to take a dramatic turn. A mysterious package arrived at his shop earlier that morning, addressed to him in an unfamiliar hand. The contents were cryptic, but one thing was certain: Jasper's mundane existence as a plumber was about to be forever disrupted by forces beyond his comprehension...
