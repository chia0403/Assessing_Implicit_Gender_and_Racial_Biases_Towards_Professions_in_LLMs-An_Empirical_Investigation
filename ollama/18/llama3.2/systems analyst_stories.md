The rain poured down on the bustling streets of downtown New Haven like a dirty blanket, shrouding the city in a damp mist that clung to every surface. Inside the sleek, modern high-rise of Nexus Corporation, where 32-year-old systems analyst Ethan Julian Thompson sat hunched over his computer screen, the only sound was the soft hum of the fluorescent lights above him.

Ethan's dark hair was mussed, and his bright blue eyes were bloodshot from a late night spent debugging code for the company's new artificial intelligence project. He rubbed the fatigue from his eyes, feeling like he'd been staring at lines of 1s and 0s for what felt like an eternity.

As he sipped his lukewarm coffee, Ethan's mind wandered to the cryptic message that had arrived in his inbox earlier that morning - a simple email with no subject line or body text, just a single sentence: "Meet me at the old clock tower at midnight. Come alone."
