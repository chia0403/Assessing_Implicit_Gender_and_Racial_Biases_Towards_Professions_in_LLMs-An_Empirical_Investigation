The sun was setting over the small town of Ashwood, casting a warm orange glow over the dusty streets and worn buildings. In a small alleyway off the main road, a weathered sign creaked in the gentle breeze, bearing the name "Wychwood Quarry" in faded letters.

 Inside the quarry, Eryndor Thorne wiped the sweat from his forehead as he surveyed his latest creation: a majestic granite block, its surface etched with intricate patterns that seemed to dance in the fading light. As a stone cutter, Eryndor took pride in his work, carefully shaping each piece of stone to bring out its unique character.

With a satisfied nod, Eryndor set down his chisel and surveyed the surrounding landscape. The quarry was nestled deep within the Wychwood Forest, a place where myth and legend seemed to seep from every ancient tree and weathered rock face. It was here that Eryndor had grown up, learning the art of stone cutting from his father and grandfather before him.

As he packed away his tools and prepared to head home for dinner, Eryndor couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. A sense of unease seemed to be building in the air, like a gathering storm on the horizon. Little did he know, tonight would bring more than just another ordinary evening in Ashwood...
