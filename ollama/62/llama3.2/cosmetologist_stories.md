The sun had just begun to set over the bustling streets of downtown Los Angeles, casting a warm orange glow over the crowded sidewalks and neon lights of Hollywood Boulevard. In a small salon nestled between a vintage clothing store and a tattoo parlor, cosmetologist Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson was busy prepping for another long night of appointments.

At 28 years old, Lexi had built a loyal client base among the city's fashionistas and celebrities, thanks to her talent for creating stunning looks on a budget. Her fingers moved deftly as she styled a client's hair, her eyes scanning the salon's social media feeds for inspiration and gossip. The scent of hairspray and hot tools filled the air, mingling with the hum of conversation from the other clients.

As Lexi finished up the last touch-up on a client's nails, the phone rang, shrill in the quiet moment before the evening rush. She picked it up, expecting another booking or maybe a call from a local designer looking for her expertise. Instead, she was met with an unfamiliar voice on the other end of the line...
