The rain-soaked streets of Seattle glistened like polished onyx as registered nurse Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stepped out of the drizzly morning air and into the warm glow of the hospital's emergency department. The soft hum of fluorescent lights overhead seemed to reverberate through her very being, a familiar comfort in a chaotic world. With a deep breath, Lexi shed her worn denim jacket, revealing a sea of colors beneath - a riotous tangle of bright scarves and colorful tattoos that told the story of a life lived on her own terms.

The ER, with its constant din of beeping machines and hushed conversations, was Lexi's domain. She'd worked here for five years, watching families face their worst fears and helping strangers find solace in the darkest moments. As she changed out of her rain gear and into her crisp white uniform, Lexi felt a sense of purpose wash over her - this was where she belonged.

Little did she know, today would be different from any other day on the job. A mysterious patient had just been wheeled into the ER, shrouded in secrecy and wrapped in bandages that seemed to hold more than just gauze within them...
