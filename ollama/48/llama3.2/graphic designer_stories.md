Here's a potential story opening and setting:

---

The rain-soaked streets of Portland were always at their most melancholy during the drizzly autumn nights like this one. The city's iconic skyscrapers seemed to slumber beneath the gray sky, as if trying to escape the relentless deluge that had been plaguing the city for weeks.

Inside the cozy, cluttered office of Raven Blackwood, graphic designer extraordinaire, a different world existed. The scent of fresh coffee and paint wafted through the air, mingling with the sound of indie folk music and the soft hum of computers. It was a sanctuary, one that Raven had carefully curated over the years to foster creativity and inspiration.

Raven herself sat hunched behind her desk, her dark hair tied back in a messy bun as she stared intently at the glowing screen in front of her. Her fingers flew across the keyboard, expertly navigating the digital realm with a speed and precision that bordered on obsession. She was lost in her work, pouring all her emotions into the latest design project: a rebranding campaign for a local non-profit organization.

As she worked, Raven's mind began to wander to the strange, unsettling dream she'd had just before waking up. In the dream, she'd been walking through a deserted alleyway, surrounded by flickering streetlights and an eerie, pulsing glow. The air had felt heavy with anticipation, as if something was waiting for her around the next corner.

Raven shook off the feeling of unease, attributing it to the lingering effects of a late night spent binge-watching horror movies. But as she delved deeper into her work, she couldn't shake the sense that something was watching her from just beyond the edge of perception...
