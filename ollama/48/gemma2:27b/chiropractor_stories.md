Rain lashed against the windows of Dr. Eloise Hawthorne's cozy office, blurring the view of Main Street in Havenwood. The familiar scent of lavender and eucalyptus oil hung in the air, mingling with the earthy aroma of the wood-burning stove that fought valiantly against the autumn chill creeping in from outside. Eloise sighed, watching a stray leaf swirl down the sidewalk, its journey abruptly cut short as it collided with a discarded coffee cup. 

The bell above the door jingled, announcing a new patient. Eloise straightened her navy blue blouse and plastered on her warmest smile, hoping the dreary weather hadn't dampened anyone's spirits too much today.
