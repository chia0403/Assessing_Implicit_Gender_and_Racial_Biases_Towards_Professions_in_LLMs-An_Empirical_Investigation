Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The rain poured down on the cobblestone streets of 19th Street, casting a misty veil over the bustling neighborhood of Greenwich Village. Inside the elegant lobby of the luxurious Bellwether Hotel, concierge Étienne Laurent watched as the evening rush of guests flooded in from the stormy night outside.

Étienne, with his chiseled features and dark hair, stood tall behind the reception desk, a smile etched on his face as he welcomed each guest by name. His sharp eyes scanned the arrivals, his trained ear picking up the whispers of hushed conversations and the soft rustle of designer handbags being unpacked.

As the last of the evening's guests settled into their rooms, Étienne took a moment to survey the lobby, taking in the familiar sights and sounds of the hotel's opulent interior. The crystal chandelier above the reception desk refracted the dim light, casting a kaleidoscope of colors across the polished marble floor. The scent of old leather and wood polish wafted through the air, mingling with the sweet aroma of fresh coffee from the lobby café.

It was moments like these that Étienne loved most – the stillness before the evening's chaos, when he could take a deep breath and survey his domain. Little did he know, however, that tonight would bring an unexpected arrival that would shake the very foundations of his life...
