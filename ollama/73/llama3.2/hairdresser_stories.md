Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun was setting over the small coastal town of Tidestone, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint shops and homes that lined its narrow streets. Amidst the bustling activity of the day's final shoppers and dinner diners, hairdresser Emilia Rose Waverley stood at her station, carefully sweeping away stray hairs from her clients' locks as they chatted with each other.

Emilia's own salon, "Tides of Beauty," was a cozy haven on Main Street, where she spent most of her days taming tangled tresses and making her clients feel like queens. As she worked, the scent of coconut oil and honey wafted through the air, mingling with the sound of seagulls crying outside and the hum of conversation from the lunch crowd.

Despite the peaceful atmosphere, Emilia's mind was elsewhere - her eyes fixed on a small, crumpled piece of paper that had been tucked into the pocket of her apron, where it lay hidden among her scissors and combs. It was an invitation to a mysterious dinner party at the old lighthouse on the outskirts of town, hosted by Emilia's enigmatic great-aunt, Luna Nightingale. The note was cryptic, but one thing was certain: this was no ordinary gathering.

As the last client of the day filed out of the salon, Emilia's hands stilled, and she let her gaze drift out into the fading light, wondering what secrets lay hidden in the shadows of Tidestone's beloved lighthouse...
