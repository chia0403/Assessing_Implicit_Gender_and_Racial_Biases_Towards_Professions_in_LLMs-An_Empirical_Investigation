Here's a potential story opening and setting:

The sun had just set over the small coastal town of Tidal Cove, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint shops and restaurants that lined the beachfront. The air was filled with the sweet scent of saltwater taffy and the sound of seagulls crying overhead.

In her cozy massage therapy studio, nestled between a vintage surf shop and a bustling cafe, 32-year-old Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson was preparing for another long day of helping clients relax and unwind. A licensed massage therapist with a passion for holistic healing, Lexi had built a loyal client base in Tidal Cove, where everyone knew her name and valued her expertise.

As she expertly rolled out the tension from a client's shoulders, Lexi couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over her. She loved her job, not just because it allowed her to help people find peace and relief, but also because it gave her the freedom to live in this beautiful coastal town, surrounded by the rhythms of the ocean.

But as she finished up with her final client and began to tidy up the studio, Lexi's mind began to wander. She had been noticing strange occurrences around town lately - equipment gone missing from the local surf shop, eerie noises coming from the old lighthouse at night, and an air of unease that seemed to settle over the community like a fog.

As she locked up the studio and stepped out into the cool evening air, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. And as she walked along the beach, watching the stars begin to twinkle overhead, she felt an inexplicable pull to investigate...
