The misty dawn broke over the small town of Willow Creek, casting a mysterious veil over the quaint streets and homes. In the heart of this quiet community, a lone figure emerged from the morning fog, his eyes scanning the sleepy neighborhood with a practiced air of caution.

His name was Emrys Alexander Wynter, but to those who knew him, he was simply "Alex" – a ruggedly handsome carpenter with a quick wit and a love for restoring old houses to their former glory. With his worn jeans, scuffed work boots, and weathered leather apron, Alex moved through the streets like a ghost, his presence barely noticeable as he went about his daily routine.

As he made his way down Elm Street, the sweet scent of freshly baked bread wafted from the local bakery, enticing him to stop and grab a quick breakfast. But Alex's mind was already on the day ahead – a job that had been waiting for him at the old Victorian on Oakwood Avenue, a house rumored to be cursed by its previous owners.

The once-grand mansion loomed before him, its turrets and gargoyles reaching towards the sky like skeletal fingers. A shiver ran down Alex's spine as he pushed open the creaky front door and stepped into the musty darkness within...
