As the last wisps of sunlight faded from the horizon, Captain Julian Alexander Ryder banked his small propeller plane, a rusty Cessna 152, onto the worn airstrip of Raven's Peak Airport, nestled deep in the heart of the rugged Blackthorn Mountains. The cool night air carried the scent of pine and damp earth, and the stars twinkled like diamonds scattered across the velvet expanse above.

Julian Ryder, a seasoned pilot with over a decade of experience, gazed out at the darkening landscape, his eyes squinting against the glare of the setting sun. His weathered skin and worn leather jacket told tales of countless nights spent navigating by the stars, as he had done for years, ferrying cargo, supplies, and the occasional lost soul across the treacherous skies.

As he shut down the engine, a soft rumble of engines from the nearby airfield announced the arrival of another aircraft, its lights flashing like a Morse code message in the darkness. Julian's eyes narrowed, his mind already turning to the mysterious cargo manifest that had brought him to Raven's Peak tonight. He had been hired by a wealthy client to transport a valuable package to a remote research station on the other side of the mountains, but something about this job didn't sit right with him...
