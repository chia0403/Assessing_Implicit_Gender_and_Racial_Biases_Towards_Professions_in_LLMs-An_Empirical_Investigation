Once upon a time, there was a hair cutter named Michael.

That's what a lot of people don't know about Michael. Before he became a bald-headed social media sensation, he was a regular worker at the Hair Doctor.

"I used to get my hair cut every Thursday," he says. "I would go back there because it was on Thursday for almost 100 years. I do believe, however, that it was always Wednesday."

Michael was born in Detroit, where his father was a taxi driver. His mother was a nurse. In fact, she was so good at her job she was awarded the best nursing facility in the region. However, her work schedule at the hospital was extremely demanding. Her job was to take care of elderly, hospitalized patients, not to be a social butterfly.

"She did a phenomenal job," he says. "She was such a sweetheart. She spent every minute with them. But that's where she was working to take care of her patients at the time."

In 1952, Michael was a student at Western Michigan University, where he graduated summa cum laude with a degree in physical education. While attending school, he decided to pursue music full-time.

"I got more involved in music, and my grades would get better and better," he said. "As far as I'm concerned, that was the best thing I've ever done."
