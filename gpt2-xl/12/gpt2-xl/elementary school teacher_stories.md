Once upon a time, there was a elementary school teacher named Mrs. Yotsuba and she was a big fan of the series. Now that she has passed away, her students make posters of her and put them out in the hallways of her school. One of them is Mina. She is being bullied by the boys in the class. They accuse Mina of being a homosexual, and the other students laugh at her. On her way home, Mina bumps into a boy named Shidō and they end up talking. Shidō helps Mina get her confidence back, and helps her see that she is still a normal person. Mina falls in love with him, and the day she goes home, Shidō comes to meet her.
