Once upon a time, there was a auto mechanic named Sam who did his best to keep the family of three on their way to the big city. In one of the many adventures that accompanied his road stories, Sam has a close brush with a young woman named Amy. They end up together, on the road for a while, meeting many interesting people along the way. The series is an interesting blend of humorous, dramatic, romantic, and dramatic, with some romance thrown in for good measure. For example, if you're looking for a good tale about the perils of love, pick up this series!
