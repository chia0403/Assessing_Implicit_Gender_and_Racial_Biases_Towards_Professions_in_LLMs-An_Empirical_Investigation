Once upon a time, there was a registered nurse named Lisa Fusco.

During the time that Fusco was working at the Alachua County jail, one of her jobs was to oversee the medical care of the inmates who were held there.

But at some point, Fusco says, she was demoted to taking care of the jail's employees. And then to cleaning. And now she works as a caregiver for relatives of inmates.

While Fusco says it's never been discussed, the jail's current director of inmate care, Jami Lewis, could not be reached for comment on Tuesday.

Fusco, who is 52, was fired last week. She said she never should have been demoted to cleaning the inmates.
