Once upon a time, there was a private detective named Harry Potter, and he had the reputation of being the fastest moving wizard alive.

Today, on a dark street in London, he has a mysterious partner.

And one day, both men will meet their ends.

What starts as a simple murder investigation soon spirals out of control and into the depths of London's dark underbelly, and in the pursuit of the truth about what happened to Harry Potter, a true hero can be born...
