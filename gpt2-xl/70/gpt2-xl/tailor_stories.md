Once upon a time, there was a tailor named Tommen. Tommen was an unruly child with a great love of clothes. A tailor's daughter named Margaery might have been his daughter, because the tailor's daughter was Margaery.[13]

Margaery is a noblewoman of House Tyrell, in the House of Black and White, and one of the Seven Kingdoms' wealthiest and most influential families. She is well known for her wit, her beauty, and her love of the fine things in life. She is known for having an affinity with clothes of all shapes and colors.[14]

She and her husband Tommen have a younger sister, Margaery Tyrell, who became queen of the Seven Kingdoms in her place.

History

Early life

Margaery Tyrell was born to Ser Gregor Clegane and his wife Lysa Arryn in the city of King's Landing. Her father, who had previously married a commoner, lost his position as Lord Commander of the Kingsguard during the Red Wedding and was put to death on the scaffold.[15]

In the books
