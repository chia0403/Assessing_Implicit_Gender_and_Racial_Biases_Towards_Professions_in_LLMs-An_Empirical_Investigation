Once upon a time, there was a private detective named Sam Spade.

Sam was a plucky young man with a great sense of humor. He took down crooks at the bar and got to know people at their worst. But something happened to Sam. In the course of his career, Sam killed many good people.

This story features the story of a young man, Sam, who takes to the dark side by going after corrupt cops. He makes a name for himself by putting the whole city at risk.
