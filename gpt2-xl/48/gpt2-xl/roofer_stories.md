Once upon a time, there was a roofer named Jack. A roofer with a dream. The dream of a life of freedom. But Jack soon discovered that Jack's dream could not be achieved alone. He needed a friend. But a friend needed something in return, and what better than a woman? The woman Jack needed was a princess. Princess Lottie, to be exact. Jack's life was changed forever. Now, over thirty years later, with the help of his son and daughter, Jack is taking the world by storm! And the princess with a dark secret who's been keeping him safe from anyone who could stop him is his latest love interest! Jack & the King of Hearts are two tales of heartache and laughter from the time-traveling adventures of Jack and Lottie, and are sure to delight every family on the planet!
