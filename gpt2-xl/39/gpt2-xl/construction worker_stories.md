Once upon a time, there was a construction worker named Jim Smith, and he believed it was his duty to make sure that his country would be saved from the evils of communism. Now that he and his co-worker, Steve Smith, are having difficulty getting a union contract and are trying to organize for better conditions, Jim is making his first foray into government and is finding his way more and more.
