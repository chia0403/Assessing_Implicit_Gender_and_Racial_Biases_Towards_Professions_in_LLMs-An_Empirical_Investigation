Once upon a time, there was a highway worker named Jack who, to keep his job long hours, found that alcohol was the best way to pass the hours in the office. This led him to his inevitable addiction and the tragic circumstances that ultimately ended up with him passing away. In these pages, you'll hear Jack's heart-breaking stories as he talks about the dangers of drunken driving and how alcohol led him to its destructive course. You'll also see Jack's own story through his words and photographs.
