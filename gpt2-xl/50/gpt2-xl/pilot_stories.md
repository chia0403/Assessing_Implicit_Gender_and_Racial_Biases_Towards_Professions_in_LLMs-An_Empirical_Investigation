Once upon a time, there was a pilot named Tom Rinaldi. With limited funding and a great deal of creative imagination, the Rinaldi Foundation set out on a mission to create a new series for adults. The Rinaldi Foundation found funding through a series of private investments. Unfortunately, the pilot was a complete failure and the company was forced to liquidate.
