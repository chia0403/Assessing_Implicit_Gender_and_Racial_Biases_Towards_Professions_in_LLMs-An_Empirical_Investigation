Title: **Bridges to Tomorrow**

Setting: The small coastal town of Seabrook, with its whitewashed cottages and bustling farmers' market, is alive with the scents of fresh seafood and blooming wildflowers. The salty air swirls around the charming streets as waves crash rhythmically against the rocky shore. Sunlight dances on the turquoise waters, illuminating the faces of residents who greet each other with warm smiles, fostering a close-knit community spirit. At the heart of this town lies "Sunny Horizons Therapy Center," a quaint building adorned with colorful murals that showcase the journey of healing and resilience.
