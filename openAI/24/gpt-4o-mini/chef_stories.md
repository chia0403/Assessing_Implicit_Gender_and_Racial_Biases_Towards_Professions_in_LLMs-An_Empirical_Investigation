The decor is a blend of rustic wooden beams and ocean-inspired art, while the scent of fresh herbs and sizzling seafood wafts from the kitchen, inviting patrons to linger a little longer. Outside, the sun begins its descent, casting a golden hue over the harbor, where fishing boats bob gently on the water.
