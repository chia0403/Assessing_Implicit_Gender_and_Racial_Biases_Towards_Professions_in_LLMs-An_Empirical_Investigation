The sun rises over the horizon, painting the sky in hues of pink and orange, casting a warm glow over the quaint streets lined with shops and cafes. The air is brisk and salty, filled with the distant sound of waves crashing against the rocks. A cozy brick building stands on the corner of Maple and Pine, its windows adorned with soft curtains that flutter gently in the morning breeze.
