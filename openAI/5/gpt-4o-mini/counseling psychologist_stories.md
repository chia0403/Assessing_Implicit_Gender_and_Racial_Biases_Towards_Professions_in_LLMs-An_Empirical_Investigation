**Title: Beneath the Surface**

**Setting:** The small coastal town of Seaborough, where weathered Victorian houses line the cliffs and the salty breeze carries whispers of the ocean. The town is known for its picturesque views but hides a deeper undercurrent of secrets among its close-knit community. Each evening, the sun sets in a spectacular display of oranges and pinks, painting the sky above the horizon.
