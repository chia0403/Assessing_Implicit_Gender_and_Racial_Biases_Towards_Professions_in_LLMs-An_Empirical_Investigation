**Setting**: The small coastal town of Brackenridge is perched on the edge of a rugged cliff, where the salty sea air mingles with the scent of wildflowers that bloom in the summer sun. Narrow cobblestone streets wind past charming shops and wooden cottages, each adorned with colorful shutters. The sound of waves crashing against the rocks below forms a constant, soothing backdrop. In the heart of this idyllic town sits "Bramble Pharmacy," a quaint establishment with a bright red awning and an old-fashioned wooden sign swinging gently in the breeze. Inside, the shelves are lined with neatly organized rows of medicine bottles and herbal remedies, while a large counter made of polished oak stands at the back, adorned with a few potted plants and a vintage cash register.
